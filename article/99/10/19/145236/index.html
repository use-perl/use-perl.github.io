More Changes Hit www.perl.com

pudge on 1999-10-19T14:05:36

Mark-Jason Dominus has taken over as Managing Editor of O'Reilly's www.perl.com site. He starts off his new job by posting the first in a series of weekly summaries of activity on the perl5-porters mailing list. In the first edition, Dominus summarizes discussion about perl 5.005_62, Unicode character classes, module bundling, and other stuff.
