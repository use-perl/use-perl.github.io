Mailing List for Perl Trainers Announced

pudge on 1999-10-27T09:29:26

Kirrily "Skud" Robert is running a list for Perl trainers, covering methods and techniques for teaching Perl; course materials, development of course notes; logistics (organising courses, venues, bookings, publicity); dealing with problems in training (difficult students, etc.); and other perl-training-related discussion.

To subscribe, send mail to perl-trainers-subscribe@perl.org.
