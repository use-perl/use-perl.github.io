EarthWeb Sponsors Open Source Event

pudge on 1999-09-20T14:57:09

EarthWeb, publisher of The Perl Journal and bunches of other developer publications, is presenting The Bazaar, an Open Source conference in New York City on December 14-16, 1999. TPJ is a Media Sponsor of the event.

In a press release, speakers were announced as Richard Stallman of the Free Software Foundation, Bruce Perens and Eric Raymond of The Open Source Initiative, Jeff Bates and Rob Malda of Slashdot, and others.

Registration for the exposition and keynotes is free.
