ValueClick Hosts Perl Sites

pudge on 1999-09-14T14:23:10

Our friends at ValueClick have taken on the hosting for several key perl sites in the transition of perl.org and cpan.org from the defunct Perl Institute to Perl Mongers. ValueClick, and their intrepid Perl hacker Ask Bjoern Hansen <ask@valueclick.com>, hosts ftp.perl.org and ftp.cpan.org, and handles all the perl.org mail.
