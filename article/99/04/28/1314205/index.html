O'Reilly Perl Tutorials Kick Off in Boston

pudge on 1999-04-28T13:14:05

Yesterday and today mark the two days of tutorials in Boston, with two days more in Santa Clara, CA, on May 4th and 5th. Perlers Mark-Jason Dominus, Randal Schwartz, Dan Klein, Tom Christiansen, Graham Barr, AEleen Frisch, Lincoln Stein, and brian d foy are instructing on everything from Learning Perl to Adminstrating NT with Perl to Tricks of the Wizards.

The first day of tutorials seemed to go well. Attendees that remarked seemed pleased with the content and instructors. Reports are still coming in from the Boston Perl Mongers bash last night, and hopefully most of the injuries were minor.
