More MacPerl Binaries

pudge on 1999-01-20T08:00:38

Posted in Chris Nandor's CPAN directory are new binaries for DBI 1.06, Data::Dumper 2.10, String::CRC 1.0, and Devel::DProf 19990108. The shared library builds can only be used with PPC and CFM68K builds of MacPerl.

Also included in there are MacPerl builds with -DDEBUGGING on, for PPC and CFM68K. Download one today and play with Chip Salzenberg's latest article in The Perl Journal.
