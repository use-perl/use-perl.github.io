O'Reilly Readies More Books

pudge on 1999-08-19T08:52:24

Windows NT Workstation Configuration and Maintenance is a newly released O'Reilly title, by Matthew M. Lavy and Ashley J. Meggitt, that covers automated management of NT workstations, using Perl scripts.

Alligator Descartes and Tim Bunce are writing Programming the Perl DBI for a late 1999 release. It can be preordered now at various online shoppes, and O'Reilly will be taking advance orders at a discount rate at the Perl Conference next week. Word is there will be a draft of the book there to leaf through.

And, of course, the Wolf Book, Mastering Algorithms with Perl by Jon Orwant, Jarkko Hietaniemi, and John Macdonald, has been released. It is about ... Mastering Algorithms. In Perl. If that doesn't get you excited, you should read a different news page than this one.
