PerlMonth Issue Two Available

pudge on 1999-06-10T13:06:22

Baiju Thakkar has published issue two at http://www.perlmonth.com/, with articles by Vicki Brown, Paul Vininig, Steven McDougall, Dave Cross, Stas Bekman, Slaven Rezic, Kurt Starsinic, and Doug MacEachern. Read and enjoy, and send feedback to feedback@perlmonth.com.

Doug MacEachern and Stas Bekman both wrote about mod_perl for this issue of PerlMonth. Bekman, who just released a new version of mod_perl Guide, wrote a piece called "What is mod_perl?," and MacEachern discusses interactive debugging under mod_perl.
