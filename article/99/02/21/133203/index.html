Yet Another Perl Conference Scheduled for June

pudge on 1999-02-21T13:03:03

Kevin Lenzo is organizing yet another perl conference (yapc), an low-cost event with its roots in the Perl Mongers user groups. The first yapc is to be held at Carnegie Mellon University on June 24 and 25, 1999. An official call for participation is expected soon.
