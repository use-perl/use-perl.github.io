Second German Perl Workshop Slated for March 2000

pudge on 1999-11-20T10:49:07

Gerald Richter writes in to note that the second German Perl Workshop [English] is slated for March 8-10, 2000. Last time the workshop had only limited space available, and they are planning for similar accomodations for the next one. Richter asks that people interested in attending mail a short reponse to <ws2000-feedback@port25.com> letting them know, so the organizers can decide what requirements they will need for next year's event.

Suggestions for lectures and presentations (mostly in German, though English presentations are welcome) can be sent in, too. To subscribe to the event's mailing list, send the body "subscribe perl-ws2000" to <perl-ws2000@gmd.de>.
