Make Your Own Portal with My Portal

pudge on 1999-11-14T00:04:24

Perl News, and many other news sites, are available as RSS (an XML content distribution specification) files. We've put together a customizable site, called My Portal, that displays any number of these "channels" on one page. If My Portal does not offer the channels you want or is not customizable enough, you can download the source, fix it up, and install it on your own server.
