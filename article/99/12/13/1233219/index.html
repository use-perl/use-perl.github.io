perl5.005_63 Development Release Released

pudge on 1999-12-13T12:33:19

There's a whole lotta patching going on. Sarathy has posted 5.005_63 (the latest development release for the upcoming 5.6 release). They reportedly have fork working under Win32, File::Find now follows symlinks, and you can write open(my $fh ...) to get a lexically scoped filehandle created automagically for you. See the announcement for more information. Remember, this is a development release. Caveat Porter.
