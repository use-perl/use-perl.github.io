German Perl Workshop 2.0 Calls for Participation

pudge on 1999-12-21T14:25:44

The organizers of the German Perl Workshop 2.0 are calling for participation and contributions. Send ideas and contributions to perl-ws-chairs@gmd.de; abstracts will be accepted until January 7, 2000.

The second annual workshop is scheduled for March 8-10, 2000, in Sankt Augustin, FH Rhein-Sieg, Germany (near Bonn).
