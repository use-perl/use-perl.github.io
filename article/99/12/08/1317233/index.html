PerlMonth Issue 7 Published

pudge on 1999-12-08T13:17:33

Baiju Thakkar has put up issue 7 of PerlMonth, the monthly online journal devoted to Perl. Articles this month include:

  • The MacPerl Development Environment, by Vicki Brown
  • XS Mechanics - Part 2, by Steven McDougall
  • Perl Mailing Lists, by Dave Cross
  • mod_perl Strategy and Implementation - Part 5, by Stas Bekman
  • Advanced listboxes with Tk::HList, by Slaven Rezic
  • Book Review: Elements of Programming with Perl, by Rachel Rawlings
  • Site Tuning with Apache::ASP, by Joshua Chamas

PerlMonth now has a jobs listing section, too, containing only Perl-focused jobs.
