OSCON 2009 Perl Track Welcomes Proposals

brian_d_foy on 2009-01-21T04:02:00

Peter Scott writes "The Call for Participation for OSCON 2009 has been posted by O'Reilly Media. The deadline for submissions is February 3.

As a member of the Perl Track committee I would like to encourage the submission of sessions and tutorials that highlight the utility and innovation of Perl, the tools that get important jobs done and the future of Perl. Make the committee's job hard by giving us difficult choices like we had last year. Feel free to ask me any questions you may have about the proposal process.

OSCON will take place July 20-24 in San Jose, California, the first time outside Portland in several years."
