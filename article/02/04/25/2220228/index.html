Yes, They're at Saint Louis University, the local Catholic and Jesuit University. For those who stay in the dorm and would be interested in an 'exorcist tour' of the campus let me know :) The movie the exorcist was based on events that happened at and near SLU.

It's a lovely campus but should you stay in the dorms either have a car [ something I recommend to everyone as getting around in St. Louis is very inconvenient without one ] or hop a cab after dark as you do not want to walk from WU through Forest Park to SLU after sundown. Be sure to check on your last will and testament before trying it anyway.
