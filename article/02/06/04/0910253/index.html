Lightning Talks Submission Deadline This Week

Simon on 2002-06-04T09:10:07

Dominus writes "If you thought you might like to give a lightning talk at YAPC::NA 2002, you should send your proposal soon, because the deadline is the end of June 5, which is about 48 hours away." More details inside.

Lightning talks are talks that are five minutes long. They're easy to propose, easy to prepare, and easy to deliver. Instead of giving a comprehensive overview of your topic, you focus on only one small detail.

To propose a talk, send the title of your talk and a description of up to four sentences to mjd-yapc-lt-2002+@plover.com before the end of Wednesday June 5, -0400.
