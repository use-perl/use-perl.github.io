Parrot/Perl 6 talk and Fundraiser in Phoenix

pudge on 2002-06-11T16:51:56

Dan writes "I'll be making a detailed presentation on Parrot to the Phoenix Perl Mongers on Thursday, June 20th, at 7 p.m. This is a fundraising event for the Perl Foundation, with the fine folks at Stonehenge matching all donations made at the meeting. Anyone interested should contact erik@jundy.com, or visit http://www.jundy.com/parrot/, so we can get a head count."

Location: Bowne (1500 N Central Ave.)

Directions: It will be held at Bowne, which is located at 1500 N. Central Avenue, which is on the Southwest corner of Central and McDowell. The parking lot is gated, so just press the button on the intercom, and tell the receptionist that you are there for the Perl meeting. Park in the lot that is straight ahead from the entrance on the South side of McDowell. Park in any uncovered, non-reserved space. Proceed to the main lobby, which is on the Northeast side of the parking lot.
