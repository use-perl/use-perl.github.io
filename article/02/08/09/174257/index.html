TPF Announces Newsletter Mailing List, Translations

KM on 2002-08-09T17:03:18

gnat writes "We've set up a mailing list for the monthly Perl Foundation newsletters, and the July newsletter went out on Thursday. If you donated to YAS and The Perl Foundation, you are already subscribed at the email address you provided when you donated. If you haven't donated and want to subscribe, send an empty email message to foundation-newsletter-subscribe@perl.org (and also consider donating :-).

In other newsletter news, we have a French translation of the July newsletter on the web site. Thanks to mirod (Michel Rodriguez) for making that happen. Translations into other languages are welcome--please contact Kevin Meltzer (kevinm AT yetanother.org)." I *heart* your translations!
