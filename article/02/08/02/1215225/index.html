Perl 6 Mini-Conference, September 12-13, Zurich

KM on 2002-08-02T12:15:14

David Schweikert writes "Larry Wall, Damian Conway, Dan Sugalski, Hugo van der Sanden, and Allison Randal will be in Zurich during a week in September for a workshop to coordinate their current activities. During that time a two-day conference will be held where they will present what awaits us with Perl 6. On perl6.ethz.ch you can find all the details about the conference."
