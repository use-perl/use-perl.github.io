Spreading the Word

Theory on 2002-08-26T19:13:59

The questions so far posted make me realize just how little people know about Perl. They're asking things like (paraphrasing here), "Where do you see Perl going?" and "How do you respond to the criticisms that Perl isn't OO because it doesn't support inheritance?"

I'm not sure how to do it, but somehow, we in the Perl community have to do a better job explaining just what Perl is, what features it offers, and where it's going. Fortunately, I think that the Slashdot interview will contribute to the solution, since Larry himself will be helping the very people asking those questions to better understand Perl.

Kudos to Larry for agreeing to submit to the Slashdot "Ask" process!
