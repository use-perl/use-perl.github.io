Damian Conway Offers Advanced Perl Workshop in Seattle

pudge on 2002-05-23T03:35:56

yumpy writes "As part of Seattle's Dam-i-a-Thon 2002, Dr. Damian Conway will be offering his new Advanced Perl Workshop in Seattle during July:

  • Advanced OO Perl, 7/15-16
  • Advanced Module Implementation Techniques, 7/17
  • Programming in Perl 6, 7/18
  • Beyond Regexes: Text Parsing with Perl Modules, 7/19
A social program consisting of a Cruise, Dinner, and 'Open Source Fellowship' ceremony near the house of Bill Gates are also on the agenda for the preceding Sunday, 7/14. Space is limited, and some discounts expire 5/31, so register soon."
