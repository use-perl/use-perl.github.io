Damian Conway in Toronto

pudge on 2002-05-07T16:37:12

Richard Dice writes "Damian Conway, the legendary Perl speaker, trainer, module developer, etc. will be in Toronto, Canada between July 5 and July 12, 2002, inclusive. During his stay in Toronto he will deliver 3 free public talks and up to 4 days of commercial corporate training. The free talks are presented under the auspices of his Yet Another Society grant."

"What do I want you to know?

  • Show up for the free public talks!
  • Please contribute a bit of $$ to help fund his trip here!
  • If you think you can benefit from Damian's corporate training (and you can!), then get in touch with me to book your space!

All the details can be found at http://www.dice-con.com/damian/. This site will continually be updated as new information becomes available.

Cheers,
Richard"
