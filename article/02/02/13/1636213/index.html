Chicago event raises funds for Perl

ziggy on 2002-02-13T18:00:00

merlyn writes "Randal L. Schwartz presented his Once and Future Perl6 talk to the Chicago Uniforum Perl SIG, and in response to a request for donations for The Perl Foundation, received $515 in pledges and $859 collected at the meeting, for a total of $1374.

As with the SF.pm meeting three weeks ago, Randal has authorized Stonehenge to match that contribution,. for a grand total of $2748.

Stonehenge is proud to be a sponsor and evangelist for the continuing work of Dr. Damian Conway, Dan Sugalski, and Larry Wall, in their efforts to bring Perl to the masses."

This is a really great trend. When your local Perl Mongers group holds a pledge drive for The Perl Foundation, please let us know.
