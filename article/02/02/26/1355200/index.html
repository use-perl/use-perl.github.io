Perl Mongers in Israel

ziggy on 2002-02-26T13:52:10

gabor writes "We are organizing the probably first ever Perl Mongers meeting in Israel. The meeting will be held on 18th March 2002 in Tel Aviv in the Azrieli center in a restaurant or pub. The exact place will be announced later on perl.org.il and on our mailing list.

In order to have an approximate head count we would like to hear from you if you plan to come. So please send a note to Gabor that you are comming."

Although there have been Perl Mongers in Israel before, this does indeed appear to be the first Perl Mongers meeting in Israel. If you're in the area, show up and say Shalom!
