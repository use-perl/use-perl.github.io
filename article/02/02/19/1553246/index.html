Cold Light Of Day

davorg on 2002-02-20T11:13:43

I've been thinking about this overnight I think my initial post may have been a little hasty. Here's what I now think[1]:

  • There's nothing wrong with posting press releases like that on use Perl;. I overreacted yesterday because I'd just received the same announcement as spam in my inbox.
  • It's not a particularly effective type of advertising anyway. In general the people reading use Perl; aren't the target audience for that kind of announcement.
  • london.pm isn't the London Perl Mafia. We don't have the power to stop anyone else using Perl in London. We shouldn't want that power and we should make sure we don't give any one the impression that we have (or want) that power.
  • It would be nice if people doing Perl stuff in London did get in touch with us as it would probably be mutually beneficial.
  • However, given the reputation that the Perl community has in certain areas, it's understandable why someone aiming at the corporate marketplace might not want to contact us. This is a bug in the Perl community that needs to be fixed :)

Hope that's clearer. Apologies to pudge for getting angry about it.

[1] And if anyone's unclear, I should point out that I'm not speaking officially for london.pm or anything like that.
