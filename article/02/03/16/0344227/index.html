Bricolage 1.2.2 Released

ziggy on 2002-03-16T12:00:00

Theory writes "The Bricolage development team is proud to announce the release of Bricolage version 1.2.2. This is a maintenance release with many bug fixes. All Bricolage 1.1 and 1.2 users are strongly encouraged to upgrade to this version in order to take advantage of its greater stability and reliability.

More information on Bricolage can be found on its home page, and it can be downloaded from SourceForge."
