ANNOUNCE: YAPC::Europe Committee

KM on 2002-11-13T14:10:35

Norbert Gruener writes "Nomination of YAPC::Europe Committee The President of YAS, Kevin Lenzo has named an internal committee which will deal with all matters concerning YAPC::Europe conferences. He has nominated members of each European YAPC to be on this committee: Leon Brocard and Greg McCarroll from London, Ann Barcomb from Amsterdam, Richard Foley and Norbert Gruener from Munich. Norbert Gruener has been appointed as the chair of this YAPC::Europe committee.

Norbert Gruener
Chair "YAPC::Europe" Committee" And of course, YAPC::Europe::2003 will be in Paris.
