CPAN rsync changed

KM on 2002-11-15T19:03:16

jhi writes "The FUNET people reshuffled their cluster which hosts the CPAN master site and one result of the shuffle was that they decided to migrate the rsync service to rsync.nic.funet.fi::. The ftp.funet.fi:: seems still to be working, but if I understood correctly that is not to be trusted upon. So if you've been mirroring either of the CPAN or perlsnap rsync targets, now it's time to change your scripts. All public CPAN mirrors have already been notified."
