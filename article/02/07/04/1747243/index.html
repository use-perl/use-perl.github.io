Damian Conway Training Winners

pudge on 2002-07-05T10:47:22

yumpy writes "Consultix held its first drawing for free Seattle training with Damian Conway last night, from contestants who donated funds to The Perl Foundation.

One guy won a seat in Programming Perl 6 on the basis of a $71 donation, and the other a seat in Advanced Module Implementation Techniques with a $10 donation. However, because we raised $297 for TPF, there were really no losers; we all win by supporting our own cause!

The next drawing will be on 7/10; see contest rules for details."
