Perl Mentoring BOF at OSCON

ziggy on 2002-07-11T14:04:02

cwest writes "For those of you attending the Perl Conference 6 (or Open Source Convention), there is a golden opportunity to get some free coaching and consultation on your work. A Birds-of-a-Feather session on 'Perl Mentoring/Code Reviews' will be given for the third year running. Some top Perl People will be on hand as resident experts to advise you on your own Perl projects.

Date: Thursday 7/25
Time: 7:00pm - 9:00pm
Location: Grande Ballroom A in the East Tower"

"Here's how it works: You bring some Perl code (ideally at most 3 pages, as a printout, or on a laptop, handheld, or floppy) and receive specific advice which might normally cost a large amount of money in consulting fees. Feel free to ask for advice on anything from style to efficiency.

This is *one-on-one* coaching: No spectators, no audience, no distractions; just personal mentoring, targeted to your specific application, from some of the best people qualified to give it.

Time spent with the expert will depend on supply and demand."
