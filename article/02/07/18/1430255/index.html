Last Call for Papers for YAPC::Europe::2002

pudge on 2002-07-19T07:30:32

Richard Foley writes "Share your 'pe[a]rls of wisdom' with the Perl community: attendees from all around the world, gurus and geeks alike, will converge on Munich for YAPC::Europe Sept. 18-20 to listen to the talks and tutorials presented at this gathering of minds.

This year's theme is 'The Science of Perl', which means that we would really like to hear about suggestions for talks, projects, experiences which involve both perl and science. This should not be regarded as a restriction, more as a 'nice to have' :-)

Proposals may be submitted between January 1st 2002 and July 30th 2002."
