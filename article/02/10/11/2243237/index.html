Mark-Jason Dominus Teaching in Seattle

gnat on 2002-10-11T22:40:40

yumpy writes "Mark-Jason Dominus, top-rated speaker at Perl conferences, will be teaching November Perl classes in Seattle, through Consultix. In contrast to his conference presentations, most of these will be Hands-On offerings, with students consolidating their learning experience by writing programs under Mark's guidance. Early Bird tuition discounts expire on 10/18. See http://teachmeperl.com for details."

The classes, which run from 11/19-27, are: Intermediate Topics in Perl,

Tricks of the Wizards,

Regular Expression Mastery,

Hands-On Repair Shop and Red Flags, and Object Oriented Programming.
