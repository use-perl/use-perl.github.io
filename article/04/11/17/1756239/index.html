Perl DBI: Tenth birthday, Roadmap, and Call for Funding

rafael on 2004-11-17T17:35:00

Tim Bunce posted to the dbi-users mailing list an announcement that begins by these touching words: "The first public release of the Perl DBI was ten years ago, on the 12th of October 1994."

DBI is undoubtedly one of the most popular perl modules ever, and Tim (its author) published a roadmap of planned developments. Meanwhile, the Perl Foundation set up a DBI Development fund for the users of DBI to give back and ensure DBI's future enhancements -- for the next ten years.
