Mark Alldritt writes "Late Night Software Ltd. is pleased to announce the release of its newest Mac OS X scripting product: Affrus 1.0, an integrated Perl editing and debugging environment for Mac OS X. Whether you're just beginning to experiment with Perl and want an easy way to get your feet wet, or you're a Perl expert wishing to understand and hack into a vast body of existing code, Affrus is for you. Go to the Affrus page for more product information and to download the Affrus demo."
