The Perl Review Offers Cheaper Subscription Options

pudge on 2004-12-07T22:47:00

brian_d_foy writes "A lot of people have asked about an online-only edition of TPR, so I created one. International customers can pay the same price as US subscribers ($US16), although they won't get the print edition. Those subscribers still have full access to the PDF versions.

We are, however, working on a European edition. If we get enough European subscribers, the online-only edition will convert to a print subscription at no additional cost. Online-only subscribers can also get the print versions for the price of shipping if they change their minds later.

We've also reduced the price to $US20 for Canada and Mexico, since the shipping is a bit cheaper than overseas. Anyone who recently subscribed from either of those countries can contact me if they want to get the lower rate (or something else for the extra money)."
