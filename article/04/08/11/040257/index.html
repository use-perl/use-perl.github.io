Perl MicroWorkshop in Budapest

ziggy on 2004-08-11T13:20:00

gabor writes "The Hungarian Perl Mongers are organizing a second get-together. Sort of bi-annual Micro Workshop. The meeting will take place on the 28th of August. There will be some presentations in Hungarian and some in English. Afterwards we head to a restaurant. If you are interested to join us, please contact Gabor Szabo

We are also looking for additional speakers so sending some proposals would be also welcome. Check this link for further details in English about The Hungarian Perl Mongers and the event."
