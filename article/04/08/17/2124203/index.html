Call for Participation 7th German Perl Workshop '05

pudge on 2004-08-17T23:42:00

asigel writes "The 7th German Perl workshop, also accepting contributions in the English language, is tentatively scheduled for Tuesday, February 8 through Friday, February 11, 2005. A local organizing crew can still submit a venue proposal (see CfV). You are kindly invited to submit proposals for talks or tutorials related to Perl in interesting ways, see 1st CfP. A final CfP will be published until 1st of October, 2004, on www.perl-workshop.de, with submissions due October 31."
