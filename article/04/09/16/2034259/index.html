Simon Cozens' Modules Need New Maintainers

pudge on 2004-09-16T22:54:00

Andy Lester writes "Simon Cozens, the not-entirely-mad genius, has left for God School. He's retiring from the CPAN, and leaving his legacy of Perl modules behind. I've stepped up to take on the task of making sure his 100 modules don't fall into disuse, and that they have proper new masters and mistresses, like I did with Iain Truskett's modules when he passed away last year. If you'd like to take over maintenance of one of Simon's module distributions, this is your chance. Read my journal entry for details."
