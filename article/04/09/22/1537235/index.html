YAPC::NA::2005 Venue Chosen

KM on 2004-09-22T15:56:00

KM writes "The Perl Foundations Conferences Committee has selected Toronto as the venue for YAPC::NA::2005. The proposal was submitted by Richard Dice and the Toronto Perl Mongers.

Dates: Wed - Fri 22-24 June 2005
Location: University of Toronto

We hope for another great YAPC::NA in 2005! Congratulations to Toronto!

Details will be found in the coming months at YAPC::NA Website"
