London Perl Workshop

rafael on 2004-09-17T15:03:00

muttley writes "A small group of London Perl Mongers have organised a 1 day Perl Workshop to be held at the Imperial College Union on Saturday, December the 11th. It will have two tracks seperated into Beginners and Advanced (or Scary :) Perl.

It is likely to be very cheap and quite laid back in typical London.pm style. Everyone is welcome to attend and equally welcome to send in a talk proposal.

More details, including the Call for Proposals, are available from the website where you will also find instructions for subscribing to an announce list if you want to keep up to date on all the happenings and goings on and be first in line when registration opens."
