Austrian Perl Workshop schedule

Simon on 2004-05-08T14:54:00

domm writes "The schedule for the Austrian Perl Workshop is finished.

Thursday is "language day" with talks on Perl, Perl6, PHP, Python and Obfuscated Perl. Friday is "perl day" with talks on various Perl topics (scripting to mod_perl, testing to debugging). Saturday is "hacking day", especially Parrot hacking (supervised by Leo Tötsch). See here for the full schedule (in german)

So if you're in/near Vienna, Austria, around 20th to 22nd May, feel free to drop by!"
