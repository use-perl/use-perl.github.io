YAPC::Australia::2004 Call for Papers

KM on 2004-06-02T18:00:00

Scott Penrose writes "YAPC::Australia::2004 is now part of the OSDC (Open Source Developers Conference). The conference is being organised by the Melbourne Perl Mongers and will be a YAPC-style grass roots conference for developers organised and presented by developers, aiming to promote open source development in Australia.

Our OSDC/YAPC Call for Papers can be found at Main Web Site"

"The important dates are:

  • Proposals deadline: 28th June 2004
  • Proposals acceptance: 29th July 2004
  • Submission deadline: 20th September 2004
  • Review results: 8th October 2004
  • Proceedings version: 8th November 2004
  • Conference: 1st - 3rd December 2004
