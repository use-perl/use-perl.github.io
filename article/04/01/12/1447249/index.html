YAPC::NA::2004 Call for Participation

KM on 2004-01-12T15:07:00

Jim Brandt writes "Time to start thinking about those YAPC::NA::2004 presentations for June! I'm pleased to announce the official call for participation for YAPC::North::America in Buffalo. We're accepting abstracts for everything from 5-minute Lightning Talks to half-day tutorials. See the YAPC::NA::2004 site for details. The deadline is March 27, 2004. Looking ahead, we'll have registration and accommodation details in February."
