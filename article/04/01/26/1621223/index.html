After thoughts

Mr. Muskrat on 2004-01-30T05:34:12

I think that the talk went really well. Tommy looked (and sounded) a little nervous but I expect that from any first time speaker. He was very thorough in explaining all of the details from the module's preconception up to and beyond placing it on CPAN. We had many questions but he had answers. I look forward to seeing more from him in the future.

If you are interested in learning more, an electronic version of tonight's presentation should be available soon from the D/FW Perl Mongers.
