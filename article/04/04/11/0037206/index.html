Dublin.pm Lives

ziggy on 2004-04-11T00:56:00

DoC writes "The Dublin.pm perl Mongers group, for Dublin, Ireland has reformed. Potential members from Dublin (and anywhere in Ireland, really) are invited to join the list (details at http://dublin.pm.org/). f2f meetings, and heading up to YAPC::Europe in Belfast later this year are also on the cards."
