jdavidb on 2004-04-22T16:07:39

I know you all want to hear Damian talk about Perl 6, but go see Andy Lester's talk, "The Open Source programmer's guide to getting a great job." From the descriptions:

You don't have to live on the west coast to get a great job and still give back to the Open Source community. Unlearn the conventional wisdom that cripples most job hunters. Forget what you think you know about getting a great job. This interactive session is in three sections, with plenty of time for Q&A.

I can promise you this will be well worth your time. You can learn about Perl 6 later. Andy really knows what he's talking about here.
