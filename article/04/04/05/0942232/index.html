Perl Monger Leadership Changes

gnat on 2004-04-05T10:01:00

davorg writes "It seems to be the season for Perl Monger groups to appoint new leaders. Here's a summary of the recent changes:

  • Matt Diephouse has taken over from Bill Day at grand-rapids.pm
  • Joel Pinckheard has taken over from Tim Allen at barcelona.pm


  • Harald Jörg has taken over from Norbert Grüner at munich.pm


Thanks to all the outgoing leaders and good luck to the incoming ones :-) Also, a couple of previously sleeping groups have just woken up, so we'll expect to hear more soon from dublin.pm and sofia.pm."
