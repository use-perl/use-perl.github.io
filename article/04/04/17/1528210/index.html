New Mailing List for Perl Beginners

pudge on 2004-04-18T06:46:00

offerk writes "Over the last few years the Israeli Perl Mongers mailing list has grown significantly in both number of subjects and number of posts, to the point where there are now hundreds of posts per month. Since the large number of topics and the sheer volume of messages may intimidate Perl beginners from joining the list, the group's leader, Gabor Szabo, has decided to open a new mailing list, dedicated to questions from Perl beginners. All beginner level questions as well as any materials (links, books, tutorials etc.) which might interest Perl beginners are welcome. The list language is strictly English, so the list is *not* restricted to persons living in Israel!" There are also beginners and beginners-cgi, at perl.org, which are fairly well-established.
