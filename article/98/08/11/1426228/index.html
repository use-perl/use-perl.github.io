MacPerl Near Tops in Development Downloads

pudge on 1998-08-11T14:26:28

MacPerl has reached second place in total downloads of Mac development tools at the popular download site, DOWNLOAD.COM.

Interestingly enough, MacPerl is not one of DOWNLOAD.COM's Top Picks (selections of best available software) in the category, but cgi-lib.pl 2.17 is.
