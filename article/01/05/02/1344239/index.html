Calling all Unicoders: Please Break Perl

pudge on 2001-05-02T13:36:55

Jarkko Hietaniemi, the current perl pumpking, has put out a call to people to download the latest development release of perl and try to find bugs in the Unicode support.

"But other than those, I have this fallacy that we are in pretty good Unicode shape in the developer snapshots. Please prove me wrong.

Now is your chance to shake out Unicode problems so that Perl 5.8 will just work with Unicode."
