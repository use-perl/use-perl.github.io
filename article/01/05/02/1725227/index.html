The Road Trip to YAPC::America::North

KM on 2001-05-02T17:24:15

R. Geoffrey Avery writes "YAPC::America::North will be in Montreal June 13-15. And for the third year PHILLY.pm and NY.pm are planning a road trip.

A survey can be found at http://JustAnotherPerlHacker.org/cgi-local/yapc/survey.pl. If you will be going to YAPC and passing through Albany or starting in New England, please complete this form so we can coordinate travel arrangements. You will still need to arrange for a place to stay and go to the YAPC registration page to sign up for the conference. If you want to be in the dorm they strongly suggest you sign up by May 12."

"The final details, like how many cars we have, where we will meet, and how much it will cost will be worked out in early June. But if you budget $75 you will be able to afford a seat. One plan under consideration is to rent one or more 15 seat full size vans for the week. Including gas and mileage, it is possible to rent one for $900 in Philadelphia. It is believed that it will cost more in New York, so we may arrange for vans to be rented further south and pick up NY.pm. Question 2 asks about your age because renting the van will cost extra if we allow drivers under 25.

A new mailing list "pm-road-trips" has been set up to discuss the plan. To sign up go to http://vorpal.jabberwock.org/mailman/listinfo/pm-road-trips or send email.

Thank you for considering making your YAPC experience a little longer and spending some time on the road with other Perl Mongers.

R. Geoffrey Avery
Cruise Director, Philly.pm"

Road Trip!!
