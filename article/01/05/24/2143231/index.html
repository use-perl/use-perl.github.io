Beginners List Splits in Two

ziggy on 2001-05-24T21:44:28

cwest writes "The beginners list, with over 1000 subscribers, has been split into two mailing lists:

  • beginners@perl.org (subscribe by sending a blank message to beginners-subscribe@perl.org
  • beginners-cgi@perl.org (subscribe by sending a blank message to beginners-cgi-subscribe@perl.org
All the folks who are currently subsribed to beginners@perl.org will remain subscribers. If you want to subscribe to beginners-cgi@perl.org, follow the above instructions or fill out the form on learn.perl.org.

This is an attempt to distribute some of the volume in order to keep the list(s) productive."
