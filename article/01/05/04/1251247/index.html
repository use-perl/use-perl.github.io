All the Perl that's Practical to Extract and Report

New List: datetime@perl.org

KM on 2001-05-04T12:50:36

srl writes "The Reefknot team, which is working on standards-compliant shared calendaring tools in Perl, realized that there's no common place for authors of date and time handling modules to talk about our work. So, we're starting the datetime@perl.org list. "

Topics include anything and everything about dates and times, including timezones, multiple time systems (Gregorian, Islamic, Maya...), calendaring, and best modules to use for particular purposes. We'll also be trying to identify what modules the community at large needs most. To subscribe, mail datetime-subscribe@perl.org.

Thanks to Rich Bowen for suggesting this, and to Ask for setting up the list.
