DBI and MacPerl Mailing Lists Move to perl.org

pudge on 2001-01-26T16:33:27

hfb writes "The DBI Mailing lists have moved from their former home at the ISC who hosted them previously to perl.org. Thanks to the ISC for hosting them for so long and to perl.org for giving them a new home.

See Tim Bunce's announcement of the move and the Mailing List Database for further details."

I should add a note here that the MacPerl lists, all nine of them, have also moved on. Rich Morin and Vicki Brown hosted them over at their site for a long time, and the MacPerl lists, too, are at perl.org now.
