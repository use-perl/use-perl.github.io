BOFs at TPC5

pudge on 2001-07-14T09:13:16

pjscott writes "I've again scheduled a BOF for Perl Trainers at the Perl Conference in San Diego. ... This BOF will be (like last year) for Perl trainers to share war stories, tips, tricks, magic moments, things that worked, things that didn't, and ask questions about the same."

There are a lot of BOF sessions (that's Birds Of a Feather, meetings of like-minded people, or, more popularly, "cults") at TPC this year (including three I am planning on being a part of: Slash, MacPerl, and RSS). Check out the schedule and make your plans early!
