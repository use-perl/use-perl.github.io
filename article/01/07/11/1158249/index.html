Damian Conway in Portland 31 Jul - 3 Aug

pudge on 2001-07-11T11:58:11

merlyn writes "Damian Conway is in Portland (Oregon) the week after the Perl Conference. If you're in the area (or can get there in time) please join him as he reviews the latest news about Perl 6 and other conference observations. Details and RSVP available at OGI-CPD's web site.

Damian is also presenting two short courses:

  • Intermediate and Advanced Object-Oriented Perl
  • Powerful Text Manipulation with Perl
Advance registration for these courses are mandatory.

These seminars are co-presented by Stonehenge Consulting Services and The Oregon Graduate Institute's Center for Professional Development."
