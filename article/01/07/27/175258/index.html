Yet Another Society, Perl Monks and Perl Mongers Merge

pudge on 2001-07-27T17:04:17

Yet Another Society, Perl Monks, and Perl Mongers are joining forces, creating a new foundation inside YAS, announced Kevin Lenzo today at the Perl Conference.

YAS will continue to do the same work, including funding Perl Development Grants (like the one that is funding Damian Conway's work for this year), running conferences, and helping Perl user groups.

Larry Wall will be on the board, and the group is setting up a vehicle to accept funding. Please see the Yet Another site for more information as it develops.
