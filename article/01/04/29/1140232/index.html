CPAN mirror stats then and now

pudge on 2001-04-30T11:39:05

hfb writes "In October of 1998, CPAN celebrated the addition of its 100th mirror site at Harrisonburg, Virginia. It then had 1 mirror in Africa, 15 in Asia, 6 in Oceania, 52 in Europe and 25 in the Americas.

Today, in 2001, we are heading towards our 200th mirror with 179 mirrors around the globe, with 4 mirrors in Africa, 27 in Asia, 4 in Oceania, 86 in Europe and 58 in the Americas.

We have had 6 new mirrors register in the April:

  • cpan.mirrors.easynet.fr, France, Europe
  • ftp.timisoara.roedu.net, Romania, Europe
  • ftp.tku.edu.tw, Taiwan, Asia
  • mirror.csit.fsu.edu, United States, North America
  • archive.progeny.com, United States, North America
  • cpan.cse.msu.edu, United States, North America

A special thanks to all the mirror operators who make CPAN possible and such a reliable resource!"
