TPC 6 Call for Participation

pudge on 2001-12-22T02:13:43

The Call for Participation is available for The Perl Conference 6 / Open Source Convention 2002, set for July 22-26, 2002, in San Diego. Proposals are due March 1, 2002 for session presentations of 45 and 90 minutes, tutorials of three and six hours, and lightning talks of five minutes.

There's a theme of "doing more with less," though they will accept proposals not related to the theme. Speaker notification is March 11, and presentation files are due April 29.
