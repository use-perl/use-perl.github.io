Module Listings off Home Page

pudge on 2001-12-18T13:35:57

OK, the daily Module Listings ("New CPAN Distributions") postings are taking up a bit too much room on the home page. So I am moving them off the home page.

You can still see them in two ways, though: first, by going to the Module Listings section page directly. Second, by going to your user preferences and selecting "Collapse Sections", which folds all section-only stories onto the home page.

Of course, if you are an anonymous user, you just get the default. I haven't decided yet whether or not to have Collapse Sections by the default if you aren't logged in. Let me know your thoughts on that, if you have any.
