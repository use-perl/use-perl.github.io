Damian to Play "Stump the Guru" in Seattle

pudge on 2001-02-07T14:44:48

Yumpy writes "Dr. Damian Conway, "The Perl Wonder from Down Under" and author of the groundbreaking book "Object Oriented Perl", will be presenting two free talks in the Seattle area in February. On Feb. 19th, he'll discuss what's in store with Perl 6.0, and on Feb. 21st, he'll be playing "Stump the Guru" by inviting audience questions on (most) things Perlish!

See http://www.consultix-inc.com for more information on these free events, as well as the "Adv. OOP" and "Data Munging" seminars he's teaching in the same week."
