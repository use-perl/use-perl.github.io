Perl for Perl Newbies Lectures in Haifa

pudge on 2001-02-07T14:52:58

Shlomi Fish writes "The first lecture in the Haifa Linux Club's upcoming series of introducing Perl for Perl newbies, is available online on the series' site.

The second lecture is in progress. Notice that a lecture may actually span more than one two hour-long session. The lecture slides are distributed under the public domain.

Your comments and suggestions are welcome. Note that the lecture was generated using a program I wrote and call QuadPres, which is written in Perl and is available upon request (also under the public domain)."
