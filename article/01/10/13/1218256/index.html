Visual Perl and XP

jlttb on 2001-10-19T19:20:52

CRN cites Visual Perl as a top example of Microsoft's 'new development philosophy' for XP. "Microsoft is supplying platform-independent services and a language-independent runtime environment. The strategy stands to dilute some of Microsoft's bad standing with developers who feel trapped in the world of Windows-based proprietary solutions... Though such signs are positive for developers, Microsoft has a long way to go before its new platform gains broad acceptance from corporate developers, many of whom deserted its camp for Java, and the open-source community."
