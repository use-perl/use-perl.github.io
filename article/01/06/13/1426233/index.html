Damian Opens YAPC with Perl 6 Overview

pudge on 2001-06-13T14:20:20

Damian Conway, taking the ill Larry Wall's place, opened YAPC today with an intro of Perl 6. Check out the PDF of a previous version of the talk.

Damian mentioned the new variable syntax (@hash{LIST} will be %hash{LIST}), and to the boos and hisses, said "You are gonna love this." He noted he hated it too at first, but has grown to love it dearly. Not much new information about Perl 6 was introduced. He will talk more about Perl 6, with Nat Torkington, at a later session.

Damian said he expected the Perl 6 design to be done by the end of 2001, with an alpha Mayish 2002, and a release Julyish 2002 (give or take six months (cumulatively?) on each).
