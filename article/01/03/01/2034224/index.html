unimatrix on 2001-03-04T09:52:18

Just got back from GPW; it was fantastic. Kudos to the organizers who worked tirelessly to make sure everything ran with german efficiency. Damian's Quantum::Superpositions talk caused people to sprout stupid quantum jokes for the next 24 hours; he and Jon Orwant also did a Perl6 overview; Marc Lehmann enlightened us (amongst other things) about Perl's Unicode handling; Jost Krieger had some constructive criticism about CPAN; and Brian Ingerson's Inline.pm won an award.

The talks were generally of a high quality with a good mix of german end english talks; but even better was the socializing - enough ideas were floating around to provide hacking fodder for the next few months.

Well done, everyone, and I'm already looking forward to GPW 2002.
