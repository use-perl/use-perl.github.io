New Palm Scripting Mailing LIst

pudge on 2001-08-04T01:41:58

chbarr writes "A new Yahoo! Group has been created dedicated to the discussion of scripting languages on Palm OS systems. Scripting languages includes Lua, Python, and, of course, Perl. Palm OS systems include Palms and Handspring Visors. Topics to include what one would normally see on a scripting list, reports on ports, as well as Palm-specific issues. Go to Palm OS Scripting page."
