Linguana Mailing Lists

pudge on 2001-08-30T19:33:14

garron writes "Linguana is a project I discussed at the 2001 OSCon, and partly the subject of my TPC paper of the same name. The mailing lists for the project are now live for anyone desiring participation in a large Natural Language Processing project. Open Source, of course, and in need of both programming and non-programming "armchair linguists".

The effort is to enable collaboration on a dynamic English lexicon, based on and extending Wordnet, for use by applications across the Internet."
