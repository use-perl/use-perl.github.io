Status board for NY and Washington Perl Mongers

pudge on 2001-09-12T10:59:27

Damian writes "Being so far away from the tragedy unfolding in New York, Washington, and Pennsylvania, I was at a loss how to help my many friends and acquaintances from those Perl communities, or even get news of them. I'm sure many other members of the international Perl community feel the same way. So I have set up a webpage to collect information on the safetly and well-being of any member of the community who have been affected by these events.

Please send me any relevant information about yourself or others and I will add it in."
