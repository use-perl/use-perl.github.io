KM on 2001-09-14T15:17:00

Dan writes "We have the first language that targets Parrot as its back end. Gregor Purdy whipped up a small language he calls Jako[1] that compiles to Parrot assembly code. [1]Jako is one of the species of African parrots, also called the "gray parrot""

The CVS repository is located at http://cvs.perl.org
