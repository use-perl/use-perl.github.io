Vienna.pm Winter of Code

brian_d_foy on 2007-12-01T15:31:00

YAPC::Europe 2007 left Vienna.pm with quite a lot of money. We want to give part of this money back to the Perl community by hosting the Winter of Code (with apologies to Google...).

You can find detailed information in our new and shiny Wiki.

For those too lazy to follow links, here's the super-short summary:

  • We have about €20,000 to spend.
  • ~ €6,000 will go into various kinds of awards.
  • ~ €5,000 will be paid for fixing TODO tests.
  • we will pay for weekly p5p summaries.
  • the rest will go either into more bounties or be donated to the grant programm of TPF.

Please check out the wiki for more information, and discussion. We may even set up a dedicated mailing list for Winter of Code... Stay tuned!
