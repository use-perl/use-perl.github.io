brian_d_foy on 2007-10-31T19:29:00

naterajj writes "LA.pm announces its next technical meeting for Thursday the 8th of November!



We will have a series of short presentations, so far:

  • Perl 5.10 (based on rbjs' presentation at the PPW).
  • Creating Domain Specific Languages with Parrot.
  • A/B Testing by Ben Tilly
  • Introductory Talk about Perl and Template Toolkit by Pablo Velasquez


The meeting will be held at the Rent.com offices in Santa Monica.



For details please visit the LA.pm website"
