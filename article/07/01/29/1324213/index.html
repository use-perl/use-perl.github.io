Nordic Perl Workshop

brian_d_foy on 2007-01-29T13:44:00

brian_d_foy writes "The Copenhagen Perl Mongers will host the Fifth Nordic Perl Workshop on April 28-29, 2007. Submit proposals for papers, offer sponsorship, or volunteer to help.

The price for two days of the workshop with lunch included is 500 DKK (about $US90). As usual, the workshop fee is waived for speakers, so submit a talk!

Hope to see you in Copenhagen!"

The previous years the talks of the workshops have primarily been in English, we have a high attendance of foreigners and at the same time we aim at being a Nordic workshop and Danish/Swedish/Norwegian might sound alike and is related, but it is easier to just do it in English.

So do not be frightened by the location (or weather), if you want to ask us something Copenhagen Perl Mongers are represented on IRC (irc.perl.org) with our own channel #cph.pm
