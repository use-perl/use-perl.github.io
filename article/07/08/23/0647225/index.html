YAPC::Europe 2007 News

brian_d_foy on 2007-08-23T07:06:00

Some more YAPC::Europe News

First of all, this YAPC seems to get the biggest YAPC::Europe ever, with currently more then 400 registered attendees, and more than 320 who actually paid their ticket (or will give a talk). Wow!

Now for the news:

  • WLAN:
    We've written up some informations on how to get on the WLAN at the venue:
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=WLANSetup

    There is a small catch, though, as the 'easy' WLAN might not be available any more when the conference starts. We're currently trying to make it stay! To make sure that you can enjoy the wireless (especially if you're running Linux), please read through the instruction and make sure you come equipped with all kernel modules you might need :-)

  • Pre-Conference Meetup (and early registration) on Monday:
    This event will take place on Monday, 27th, from 19:00 to 24:00 in a pub called 'Centimeter III' (Liechtensteinstrasse 42, 1090 Vienna)

    You can find the pub on the google map:
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/map.html#activity

    We meet not only for food, chatting and drinks, but you can also register there, and claim your goodie-bag!

    More information is available on the wiki:
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=PreconferenceMeeting

  • speaking of the Wiki:
    If you're arriving early or staying longer, you can use the Wiki to find other attendees for some sightseeing etc:
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=ThingsToDoOnMonday
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=ThingsToDoOnFriday

    You can also tell us, which Hackathons or BOFs you want to visit:
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=Hackathons
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=SuggestedBOFs

    Plus lots of other infos...

  • Lockers
    There are quite a few safe lockers in the basement of the venue. Please store your valuables there. We can't guarantee the safety of you unattended belongings in the conference halls, since the venue is publicly accessible. You will need a 2 Euro Coin as a deposit.

  • Job Fair
    At the second day of the YAPC::EU 2007 - the 29th of August - a job fair will take place in the foyer of the venue from 10:30 until 17:30.

    There are plenty of companies presenting themselves, and some even have some special goodies for interested attendees...
    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/jobfair.html

  • CPAN Forum Tagging Competition
    Several weeks ago Gabor Szabo added tagging capability to CPAN::Forum so users can add tags to any of the CPAN modules.

    On http://www.cpanforum.com/stats/ you can see the stat of the top taggers. The 3 participants on YAPC::EU who are going to be on the highest positions will get some present from Gabor!

    http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ye2007/wiki?node=CPANTagging

  • Auction
    As you might expect, there will be an auction at the end of YAPC::Europe, hosted by the incredible Greg McCarroll. We already have quite a bunch of interesting things to auction off, but we're still looking for more.

    Eg: T-Shirts from past and upcoming Perl events, Books, Haircuts, T-Shirt-Colors (or fabrics), Names of unborn children, .. we'll take everything! Just drop us a line...

    The net profit of the auction will go to next years YAPC::Europe, so even if you do not donate anything to auction off, come packed with cash to not only buy cool merchandise (or funny items), but also support Perl! (We'll take payments in any currency that's exchangable into Euros, plus credit cards...)

If you have any questions that are not awnserd on our website: http://vienna.yapceurope.org/ please do not hesitat to send an email to vienna2007@yapceurope.org or try #yapc on irc.perl.org

Looking forward to seeing you (OMG, 5 days left...)
Thomas Klausner
on behalf of the YAPC::Europe 2007 organisers.
