YAPC::NA has a Blog

brian_d_foy on 2006-02-03T10:32:00

jmcada writes "You knew we had to do it. The Perl Foundation has a blog. The Perl NOC has a blog. Even some cat has a blog. So yes, YAPC::NA 2006 now has a blog.

What is it for?

It is for keeping up with the quickly approaching YAPC North America. We are planning on having a really exciting conference this year. In keeping with the tradition of YAPC, we will have an affordable conference created by Perl programmers for Perl programmers. However, we will be having a few twists this year, but you’ll have to watch the blog to stay informed. Of course, with the help of RSS, it’s just too easy isn’t it."
