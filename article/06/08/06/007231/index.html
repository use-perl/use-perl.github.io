Creation of APPP - Portuguese Perl Association

brian_d_foy on 2006-08-06T00:26:00

We're proud and pleased to announce that we finally have a entity devoted to Perl in Portugal.

APPP - Associação Portuguesa de Programadores de Perl (Portuguese Perl Programmers Association).


We already have an onion logo and we'll be opening a public contest for the website design pretty soon.

And here's the current status of the Portuguese PM groups:

  • Lisbon.pm - 78 members
  • Viana.pm - 14 members
  • Braga.pm - 13 members
  • Porto.pm - 4 members
  • Cascais.pm - 2 members
  • Guimarães.pm - 1 member
  • Oeiras.pm - 1 member
