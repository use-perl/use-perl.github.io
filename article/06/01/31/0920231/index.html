ActiveState To Spin Out

davorg on 2006-01-31T09:25:00

ActiveState (makers of the best-known Perl distribution for Windows) is being sold by its parent company, Sophos, to venture capitalists Pender Financial. This effectively means that ActiveState will become an independent company once more. The press release is here and there are more details from CTO David Ascher here.
