I have to say that I had read 5 or 6 State of the Onions (just the plain transcript) before ever seeing him give one in person, and from the plain transcript he always came across as a bit of a lunatic.

In retrospect, I know this was because most of his humour is visual-based, and so any plain text transcript fails to capture the talk.

It's nice to see that this year he made it SO difficult to provide a transcript "This is a photo of my daughter" that it finally has the slides in there as well.

I have to say it's about time, and I'd really like to see some of the older ones again, this time with the other half of the talk (i.e. the slides).
