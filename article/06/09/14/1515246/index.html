The Dutch Perl Workshop 2007

rafael on 2006-09-14T15:30:00

H.Merijn Brand writes "After several succesful Dutch Perl Workshops, we will organize a Dutch Perl Workshop again in February or March 2007. (most likely). Is there anyone that would like to attend and/or give a speech there? We have no date set yet, and if you *really* would like to come, we can still try to fit it around the dates that are possible."

"I would like to mention the website of the previous Dutch Perl Workshop: http://workshop.perlpromo.nl/. It is in Dutch, but before the workshop will be held, an English version will be made.

At previous Dutch Workshops, people like myself, Leopold Tötsch, Thomas Klausner, Johan Vromans, Mark Overmeer (MarkOv), Abigail and Juerd have given presentations and tutorials. We aim at different levels: novice, advanced, expert. Usage of the English language is not a problem.

If you are in February or March in the vicinity (like Belgium Germany, United Kingdom, Denmark) and you don't mind a detour or a small extension of your trip, we would like to see you at the Dutch Perl Workshop. At this point, no definitive date has been set, and we can shift the date to accomodate you.

Thanks in advance, on behalve of the organizing team.

(Also note that the DPWS is a low-budget happening, so paying for people to come over from Australia or the VS is unlikely to happen.)"
