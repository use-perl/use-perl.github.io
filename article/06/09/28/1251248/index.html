Dates for YAPC::NA 2007

davorg on 2006-09-28T13:11:00

The dates for YAPC::NA 2007 have finally been established! The Houston.pm group and YAPC::NA 2007 organizers are pleased to announce that next year's YAPC::NA will be Monday, June 25th through Wednesday, June 27th, at the University of Houston. Please feel free to contact us with any questions or concerns you may have. You can also visit the YAPC::NA homepage for updates and information. The site format and content should be changing soon so be sure to check back as often as you like. We'll try to post a schedule of when various things will be announced so that people know when to expect what (suggestion from this year's yapc wiki).
