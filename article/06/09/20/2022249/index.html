Ridiculously Good Looking Pittsburgh Perl Workshop

pudge on 2006-09-20T20:38:00

cwest writes "The first-ever Pittsburgh Perl Workshop is coming September 23rd, and it's going to be fantastic. We have two excellent sets of sessions scheduled, an awesome after-event venue, and a bunch of surprises. It is nearly sold out, so please register now ($40 for general admission, $20 for students) to reserve your spot.

We still need more volunteers to help make this a success. If you're interested, please sign up for the available positions on our wiki.

We are planning to go to the festive and microbrew-licious Church Brew Works for an after-party at 7 p.m. It's very important to sign up if you want to go, so we know how many tables to reserve."
