Birmingham.pm April Technical Meeting

brian_d_foy on 2006-04-19T16:31:00

barbie writes "Birmingham Perl Mongers are pleased to announce their April technical meeting featuring 'Project Splinescan' by Andrew Lewis and the '2006 World Tour - April Update' by Barbie on Wednesday 26th April 2006 from 6pm onwards
at O'Neills, 90 Hurst Street, The Arcadian Centre, Birmingham."

"We have another meeting with at least one interesting talks (Andrew's), and are again stepping away from the really technical talks. This month our guest speaker, Andrew Lewis, is coming along to explain more about his splinescan project, which involves 3D scanning.

As always entry is free, no knowledge of Perl is required, and we'd be delighted to have you along. Feel free to invite family, friends and colleagues ;)

Technical meetings are held at O'Neills on Hurst Street, by The Arcadian Centre. We will be in the main function room ("the band room" if you've ever been there at the weekend). It's approximately 5-10 minutes walk from New Street station, and only slightly longer from the city centre.

If you get stuck or lost, please call me on 0771 401 6184 for directions."
