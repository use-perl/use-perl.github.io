Just curious, but if it's in Canada, why isn't the price also listed in CAD? :)

Re:Pugs Hackathon at YAPC::NA

RichDice on 2005-03-21T12:29:08

Two reasons:

1. The back-end systems are run by The Perl Foundation, which is based in Michigan. I don't know if they could charge CAD$ even if I wanted them to.

2. I expect that something like 60% of the conference attendees will be American, and I want to make it easy on them. Canadians will have no difficulties making the needed mental adjustments to the CAD$-consequences of the conference being paid for in USD$, because we have to do these kinds of mental adjustments for things all the time. Americans rarely-if-ever have to "think in CAD$", so that might be a problem for some.

Cheers,
Richard
