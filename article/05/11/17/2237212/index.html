YAPC::NA (Chicago) 2006, June 26th - 28th

brian_d_foy on 2005-11-17T22:50:00

Josh McAdams writes "Come and join us in the windy city of Chicago for YAPC::NA 2006. The conference is scheduled to be held on June 26th - 28th at the Illinois Institute of Technology.

We have a lot of big plans for the conference and a lot of expectations to live up to. With low costs, a central location, and a wealth of local Perl talent, be ready for a YAPC to remember.

For those who don't know, YAPC stands for Yet Another Perl Conference. It is a Perl conference that aims to be affordable and accessible to the Perl community at large. Each year, two YAPC's are held. One in North America and one in Europe. These conferences are planned and executed by volunteer Perl programmers on the behalf of Yet Another Society, a non-profit corporation for the advancement of collaborative efforts in computer and information sciences.
