CFP: German Perlworkshop 2006

davorg on 2005-11-09T10:39:00

schwigon writes "The 8th German Perl-Workshop will take place from Wednesday, 1st March 2006 to Friday, 3rd March 2006 and you are invited to submit proposals for talks or tutorials. We will happily accept proposals for interesting talks related to Perl. Extended closing date is 20 Nov 2005. See full length CfP at http://www.perl-workshop.de/2006/docs/cfp.htm. Please use http://www.perl-workshop.de/db/addtalk.epl to enter your proposal."
