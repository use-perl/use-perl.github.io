YAPC::Europe::2006 in Birmingham, UK

KM on 2005-09-07T13:31:00

Richard Foley writes "The YAPC::Europe committee is very pleased to announce that Birmingham PM has been selected to host the seventh conference to be held in the late summer of 2006.

The YAPC::Europe::2005 in Braga, Portugal with their Perl Everywhere theme, was an example to us all of a well organised and interesting European Perl conference, and thanks are due to the organisers. So much unseen work goes on behind the scenes, during the preceding year as well as during the 3-day event itself, to create and manage a culturally diverse meeting of this magnitude.

Birmingham PM have their work cut out to continue the tradition of improving on past events, great things are expected from the centre of the UK, and we wish them an interesting and successful conference next year.

You can subscribe to the YEF::mailing lists to keep up to date on all developments."
