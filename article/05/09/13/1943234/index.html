Call for Papers, 8th German Perl-Workshop 2006

Robrt on 2005-09-13T20:01:00

cynix writes "The Call for Papers for the 8th German Perl-Workshop is online. The workshop will take place from March 1st to 3rd, 2006 at the Ruhr-Universität in Bochum, Germany. As always, we welcome proposals for talks or tutorials from international participants. The deadline for proposals is Sunday, October 30th, 2005."
