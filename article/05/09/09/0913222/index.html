Nordic Perl Workshop 2005 - Call for papers

davorg on 2005-09-09T09:30:00

claes writes "The Nordic Perl Workshop 2005 is preliminary scheduled to take place in lovely Stockholm, Sweden the 22nd and 23th of October. Now we need you to make this a memorable workshop by submitting your talk-proposals. Please read the call-for-papers which is in effect now. For more information, visit our website for updates and/or subscribe to our mailing-list by sending an empty mail to nordic-workshop-subscribe@perl.org. Hope to see you in Stockholm."
