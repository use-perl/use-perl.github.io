The Perl Review 2.0 Hits Presses

pudge on 2005-08-24T22:22:00

brian_d_foy writes "The Perl Review 2.0 (Fall 2005) is on its way to the presses on two continents: YAPC::EU (and other EU subscribers) will get a special European edition printed in Portugal by Alberto, and U.S. subscribers will get the same thing minus the words 'Special EU edition' and with different ads."

"In this issue:

  • Logic Programming in Perl -- Curtis "Ovid" Poe
  • TPF: Google Summer of Code -- Jim Brandt
  • Object-oriented Perl -- Alberto Manuel Simões
  • The Visitor Design Pattern -- brian d foy
  • along with Perl news, book reviews, and more

Subscibe now to get the paper version as well as access to the PDFs of the current and past issues. If you haven't renewed your subscription but have already got a year of issues, it's time to sign up again. There is still a handful of you holding out!"
