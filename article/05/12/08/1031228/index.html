Autrijus Tang talks at Sydney.pm

davorg on 2005-12-08T10:51:00

oneiron writes "On Monday 12th December 2005, the exuberant Autrijus Tang completes his seemingly endless 2005 World Tour by jetting in from the bustling metropolis of Lismore to the even bigger smoke of Sydney, deplaning just in time to entertain Sydneysiders with tales from his leading edge Pugs project.

The first talk, delivered to an audience chock-full of academics and Haskell boffins, is at 2:00pm-4:00pm in the UNSW Computer Science building, Kensington campus, building K17-113 (Lvl 1 Seminar Room).

The second, for Sydney.pm, is at 6:30pm-8:30pm in the UTS Broadway campus, room CB01.27.26 (Building 1, Level 27, Room 27.26, take the lift to Level 26 and then the stairs to Level 27)."
