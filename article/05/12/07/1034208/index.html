Toronto.pm Posts Audio Archive for Aug to Nov 2005

davorg on 2005-12-07T10:50:00

fhew writes "I'm sorry, but I've been lax at announcing TPM recordings, even though they have been posted regularily (as those using RSS would have seen). But at last here is the collection of missed announcements. The Audio Archive of the August, September, October and November 2005 meetings of the Toronto Perl Mongers group is now available online."

"The list of talks are:

August

  • "Open Forum" - A open discussion about peoples (Perl) problems and solutions.
September - Lightning Talks
  • YAPC::EU Summary by Michael Graham
  • AI::Prolog by Shaun Griffith
  • Testing CPAN Dependencies by Michael Graham
  • Net::Telnet by Jim Harris
  • Which Way Did He Go? by Fulko Hew
  • The Decent of Man by Richard Dice
  • Debugging the de Bruijn Sequence by MJD
October
  • Two or Three Things I Learned about Maintaining CPAN Modules by Jim Keenan
  • A Book Report by Richard Dice
November
  • Using Unison for File Syncronization by Martin Cleaver
  • 21st Century Perl by Michael Graham
The URL for the archives is: http://hew.ca/talks_audio/"
