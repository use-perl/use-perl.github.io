Call for Venue for YAPC::Europe::2006 Conference

KM on 2005-05-17T00:08:00

Norbert Gruener, President of YAPC Europe Foundation writes "While the Braga Perl Mongers are organizing the YAPC::Europe::2005 conference in Portugal, with exceptional enthusiasm and much feedback, the YAPC::Europe Foundation is already looking for the candidates for the following year. If possible, the committee would like to announce the host city and country for 2006 at the end of the current (2005-Braga) conference. Any dedicated crew interested in organizing the YAPC::Europe conference in 2006 should send an informal application, stating their interest, to committee@yapceurope.org. Questions about the application and help to present it can also be asked at this same address. The deadline for applications is June 30th 2005. For general information about the venue requirements, check the YAPC::Venue module on CPAN, and look at the previous venues at the YAPC Europe web site."
