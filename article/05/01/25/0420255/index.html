7th German Perl Workshop Schedule and Registration

pudge on 2005-01-25T04:40:00

JStenzel writes "The schedule of the 7th German Perl Workshop, together with the abstracts, is now online. A registration form is provided on the same page. The Workshop will be held in Dresden from 9th to 11th February 2005. Both German and English talks will be presented in the typical workshop atmosphere which inspired me as often as I took part."
