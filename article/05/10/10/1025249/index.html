3rd Hungarian Perl Workshop

davorg on 2005-10-10T10:45:00

gabor writes " Registration for the 3rd Hungarian Perl Workshop is now open. The workshop will take place on Saturday, 22nd October 2005 at Chazar Andras str. 10 in Budapest. The keynote speaker will be Larry Wall. Participation is free of charge but registration is required. Register here and submit your talk proposals. Talks should be either in English or in Hungarian. For further details check out the English section of the conference wiki. Hurry up, it is less than 2 weeks away."
