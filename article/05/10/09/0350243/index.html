Nordic Perl Workshop 2005

Robrt on 2005-10-09T03:59:00

claes writes "Attention Perl developers! Nordic Perl Workshop 2005 - the third Nordic Perl Workshop will take place the 22nd and 23rd of October in Stockholm. This years topic is "Perl today, Perl tomorrow and tools for Perl" but several other topics will also be covered. Registration is now open."

We feel the workshop is a great way to learn new techniques and meet interesting people who all share the same interest for Perl.



Among this years speakers we find Autrijus Tang talking about Pugs - a Perl6 implementation written in Perl, Marcus Ramberg on Catalyst - a framework similar to Ruby on Rails and Spring for Java, Artur Bergman and several others.



The cost for this workshop is 50 EUR. For students we have a discounted price which is only 25 EUR.
