Nordic Perl Workshop in Copenhagen

KM on 2003-12-13T21:15:00

jonasbn writes "Copenhagen and Stockholm Perl Mongers are again this year arranging a Perl Workshop in Copenhagen, helped by DKUUG. The workshop will be a two day event in Symbion Science Park in Copenhagen as last year, where we will gather people from the nordic countries and around. With focus on Perl and everything related the program aims at being a mixture of presentations, BOFing and social arrangements. If you are interested in attending NPW2004 as a speaker or participant, then put a placeholder in you calender for the weekend the 27-28th of March. More information will follow later, or contact contact@copenhagen.pm.org."
