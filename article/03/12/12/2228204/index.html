Study in Open Source Collaboration

pudge on 2003-12-12T22:45:00

whiteg writes "We are conducting a Perl community survey, sponsored by Taiwan's Open Source Software Foundry (OSSF), as part of the government's Free Software Promotion Initiative. The purpose of this survey is to understand how Taiwan's developers can better communicate and work within international communities. We wish to learn about how members in the Perl community interact with each other, so we can provide some guidelines to local developers. We would like to invite all Perl folks to join our IRC interview session:

Host: whiteg, autrijus
Time: 2003-12-15 18:30~21:30 UTC Place: irc.perl.org #survey

If you'd like to participate but are unable to attend, please read the survey questions and mail your answers to me. Thanks!"
