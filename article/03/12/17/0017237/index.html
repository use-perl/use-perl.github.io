Call for Papers Nordic Perl Workshop 2004

ziggy on 2003-12-17T00:37:00

jonasbn writes "The official Call for Papers for the Nordic Perl Workshop have now been published. As something new at this workshop, we are inviting speakers to try a different kind of session called 'Panel talks', where the speaker acts more as moderator and invites other experts to his/her talk. We hope to be able to cover some more aspects and get more into deep with interesting subjects. So we hope to get submissions for this kind of BOF/Talk combo, if you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us (contact@copenhagen.pm.org)."
