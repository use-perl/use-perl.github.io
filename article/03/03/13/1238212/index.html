Barcelona Perl Mongers Back in Action

pudge on 2003-03-13T12:41:43

zeno writes "The Barcelona Perl Mongers group, after two years in existence, is now back in full swing. Our meeting on 2003.02.28 was a success, and our next meeting will be 2003.03.27. All our meetings, unless we say otherwise, take place the fourth Thursday of each month at 20:00 in the Cervecería Quin15s Tapes, Passeig Maragall 221. It's across the street from the Maragall metro, Av Borbó exit. We welcome Perl Monger tourists!"
