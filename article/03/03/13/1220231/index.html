ActiveState Sponsors pVoice

pudge on 2003-03-13T12:18:36

davorg writes "Jouke Visser has announced that ActiveState have agreed to sponsor the development of pVoice, an application which allows people with certain kinds of disability to communicate using a computer. Jouke initially started on the project to help his daughter Krista. It's a great example of Perl being used to help people and the project deserves all the support we can give it. This sponsorship takes the form of the donation of a licence for the Perl Development Kit. "
