datetime.perl.org

Simon on 2003-03-31T09:50:30

autarch writes "I just wanted to let people know about the new datetime.perl.org site, which is the official home for the Perl DateTime project. Right now it has some information about the project, links to date/time related resources on the web, and some guidelines for developers interested in contributing to the DateTime project.

In the future, we'll be adding a FAQ, and maybe some online calendar calculations. Of course, suggestions for other content are welcome."
