Call for Participation for Scandinavian Perl Works

hfb on 2003-04-11T08:23:49

jonasbn writes "Scandinavian Perl Workshop / Copenhagen, Denmark 25th - 26th April 2003

Copenhagen Perl Mongers in conjunction with DKUUG and Stockholm Perl Mongers are proud to host the first annual Perl workshop in Scandinavia. This event will take place in the beautiful city of Copenhagen between the 24th and 25th of April 2003.

This event is a great opportunity for Perl developers to learn, socialize and exchange ideas with fellow Perl developers from northern Europe and other parts of the World.

Threading, Optimizing, Imaging and Database development are just some of the great presentations that will be given during the workshop. To see the full schedule, please have a look at our website on http://www.perlworkshop.dk Please join us for two days of listening, talking and learning about Perl in lovely Copenhagen, Denmark.

The registration cost is 500 DDK and is not refundable. The price includes lunch and coffee for friday and saturday.

Registration can be done at http://register.perlworkshop.dk

Thank you; we hope to see you in Copenhagen.

If you have any questions, please mail contact@copenhagen.pm.org"
