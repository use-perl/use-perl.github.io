YAPC::Canada Slated for May 15-16, 2003

pudge on 2003-01-27T15:30:10

pjscott writes "YAPC::Canada is a low-cost 1 1/2 day conference, at Carleton University, Ottawa, Ontario, with its roots in the Perl Mongers groups. YAPC::Canada features two speaker streams and an optional day of pre-conference training. Pre-register at http://www.yapc.ca/ with no obligation and tell us: courses that interest you; if you would like to sponsor us; if you would like to present; any special needs; your ideas. If you encounter any problems with the site or would like to contact us directly then please write to help@yapc.ca."
