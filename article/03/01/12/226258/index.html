His website is archived online

n1vux on 2003-01-14T19:38:32

He left behind him a web site which unfortunately off-line already and ...

The Wayback Machine / Internet Archive has a multiple years of history of atheist.org.il online, Mar.1997-Nov.2001. The last updated frontpage is Nov 11, 2001, but Autoload scripts will make it impossible to view directly from there. The Jan 25 1999 reorganization renders at least the front page.

Note on copyright: At some point, he changed the website copyright from GPL to traditional, which may be of interest to archivists selecting what to resurect. Legally, his literary executor would control rights to republish the later, copyright websites. (IANL, and Israeli law may vary too.)
