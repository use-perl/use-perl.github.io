PerlBugathon Results

ziggy on 2003-07-21T13:32:00

Robrt writes "The results for the 2003 PerlBugathon are in! (And they're fuzzy!) Around 150 bugs were closed by volunteers at OSCON and around the world. OnyxNeon has doubled their bug-bounty to $2, and will be making a donation of $300 to The Perl Foundation. Thanks to everyone who was involved! (And remember, bug closing doesn't have to be a once-a-year event!)"
