PerlBugAthon

hfb on 2003-07-05T13:24:00

cwest writes "There are currently around 1450 open tickets in the perlbug database. Many of these are years old. Others are already resolved in current versions of perl. Some might not be bugs at all.

The goal of the PerlBugAthon is to reduce the number of open tickets by 500. We have a week to make it happen. The kickoff meeting is Sunday, July 6, 2 p.m. PDT (GMT-7) at the OSCON hotel lobby.

This event is not only for OSCON attendees, but rather a distributed bug squashing effort. Onyx Neon is sponsoring the PerlHackAthon, donating $1 to The Perl Foundation for every bug closed.

Thanks go to Robert Spier for organizing this event."
