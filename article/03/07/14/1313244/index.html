search.cpan.org Changes Colors

ziggy on 2003-07-14T13:05:00

As part of the TPF Auction at OSCon last week, Graham Barr auctioned off the right to change the color of search.cpan.org for one year. Although a consortium to keep the color the same won the auction with a bid of $1025 dollars, Graham is being a good sport and changed the site's color to orange for a period of 30 days.

If you do not like the orange makeover for search.cpan.org, Graham announced that he will return CPAN to its original color one day sooner for each $100 donated to The Perl Foundation.
