YAPC::NA::2004 Venue Chosen

KM on 2003-08-22T14:54:00

The YAPC::NA Committee has selected Buffalo, NY. as the venue for YAPC::NA::2004. The proposal was submitted by Jim Brandt. There were other excellent proposals submitted by the following:

  • Geoffrey Young - Delaware
  • Richard Dice - Toronto
  • Adam Turoff - Washington DC
  • Mark Jason Dominus - Philadelphia
On behalf of the YAPC::NA Committee I'd like to thanks everyone for their hard work and excellent proposals. It was a tight race, and hope to see these proposals again next year.

Hope to see you all in Buffalo in 2004! And, keep an eye on The Perl Foundation website for details as they arise (like the final dates).
