CFP for Scandinavian Perl Workshop

ziggy on 2003-02-24T18:39:35

jonasbn writes "The official website for the Scandinavian Perl Workshop is up, along with our official call for papers.

The name of the 'Copenhagen Perl Workshop' changed to 'Scandinavian Perl Workshop', due to the fact that our swedish friends from Stockholm.pm have joined us and DKUUG in organizing the Workshop.

The dates have also shifted a bit so the Workshop will be on the 25th. and 26th. of April 2003."
