not really perl related but in Jurassic Park, the System is Unix based. Near the end of the movie, when Raptors try to get into the control room, Sam Neill and Laura Dern hold the door. The little girl (earlier being teased for being a Nerd, she replied she was a Hacker), says that the system is Unix based and she knows it. She apparently browses around the files with a 3D GUI. This all appears to be part of IRIX, where fsn is shown (the website even has a As shown in Jurassic Park caption). Other scenes show windows more resembling an Apple GUI (the part where the sysadmin calls to keep the boat from leaving, you see a video feed window).
