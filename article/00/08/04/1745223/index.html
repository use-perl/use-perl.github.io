More CPAN Redirects

pudge on 2000-08-04T17:02:23

Elaine Ashton has added a few more redirects to the cpan.org domain. http://wait.cpan.org/ redirects to the WAIT (a documentation search engine for CPAN), http://perlfaq.cpan.org/ redirects to the Perl FAQ, http://perldoc.cpan.org/ redirects to all the Perl PODs.

Also, she notes that due to increased traffic and demands on search.cpan.org, she will be increasing the physical RAM on the box and will be scheduling an hour of downtime during the day, to be announced.
