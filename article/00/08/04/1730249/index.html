Perl 6 Lists and RFCs

pudge on 2000-08-04T17:05:46

Several new mailing lists have been created recently. perl6-build, chaired by Jarkko Hietaniemi, is charged with developing RFCs on the configuration and installation of perl 6. The perl6-language list has spun off a pair of lists, perl6-language-mlc (Michael J. Matthews) for redrafting the multiline comments RFC, and perl6-language-strict (J. David Blackstone) for discussing the strict pragma. Graham Barr is chair of the new perl6-stdlib list, for RFCs on the standard library.

As might be obvious, there are plenty of RFCs available, which are, with the mailing lists and archives, being kept at http://tmtowtdi.perl.org/. So far 28 RFCs have been posted.
