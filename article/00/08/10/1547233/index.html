ActiveState Releases First Beta of PerlMx

pudge on 2000-08-10T15:37:16

ActiveState released the first beta of PerlMx, its filter engine for Sendmail, the popular mail software. The beta release of PerlMx is available for x86 Linux and SPARC Solaris, and requires ActivePerl build 616 and either Sendmail 8.11.0 or Sendmail Switch 2.0.6.
