London.pm Adopts a Camel

pudge on 2000-08-15T13:02:00

davorg writes "London.pm has adopted a camel at London Zoo. What this means is that we give them a stack of money which they then use to look after the camel and in return we get our name on a plaque on the camel house. More details are available here.

There's a good chance that other zoos run a similar scheme, so perhaps other Perl Monger groups would consider following our lead."
