CPAN Uploads RSS

pudge on 2000-04-11T11:48:40

Thanks to Graham Barr and Elaine Ashton, we now have a CPAN Uploads RSS file and Slashbox, which contains the latest uploads to CPAN as reported by search.cpan.org. If you have not changed your custom Slashboxes, you should see the CPAN Uploads box right there on the right side of the home page. If you have changed your custom Slashboxes, go to your preferences and add it in.

Elaine says it is getting updated once a day now, but it may go to being updated several times a day. Thanks, both of you.

So, my question: will this obviate the need on this site for a Perl Modules story listing the latest uploads, like we've had on Perl News forever? I kinda think so.
