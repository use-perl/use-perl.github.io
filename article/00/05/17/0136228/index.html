Dominus Writes About COBOL, Fixing Broken Programs

pudge on 2000-05-17T01:35:28

www.perl.com's Mark-Jason Dominus has posted a couple of articles in recent weeks. He wrote Program Repair Shop and Red Flags, in which he identifies and fixes common programming mistakes. In Perl Meets COBOL, he writes about his experiences teaching Perl to COBOL programmers.

Dominus has also resumed his weekly P5P Digest, a summary of what is happening on with the core perl developers' mailing list.
