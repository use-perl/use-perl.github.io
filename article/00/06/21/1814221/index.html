This is Wednesday, but I'm not in Pittsburgh

chip on 2000-06-22T05:34:34

I really regretted missing YAPC 1999, and I've been looking forward to YAPC 19100 with bated (not to say baited) breath.

To my distress, however, I find myself once again a victim of circumstance, trapped here in sunny (!) California when seemingly everyone else in the North American Perl community is off in Pittsburgh ... and before that, in Alaska.

Still, I wish to be there in spirit if not in body. So if anyone gets a feeling of vuja de[1], well, that's me projecting my mental state from here. Or maybe it's just gas.

And to comfort those of you who were hoping to raise a beer with me (yeah, both of you): Look at the bright side! My having an extra week without travel makes my getting Topaz working by TPC4 just that much more likely.

[1] vuja de, n. That strange, otherworldly feeling that you have never been in this place before.
