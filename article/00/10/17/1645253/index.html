Bender, the Next Slash Release

pudge on 2000-10-17T16:38:19

krow writes "If you are interested in what will be in the next release of slashcode and live in (or will be around Seattle), I will be giving a talk tonight, October 17th, for the Seattle Perl Users group. Directions can be found here. I am going to go over the new architecture, how to support multiple databases, how to expand your slashsite via modules, use of templates and give an outline about where we are going in the future."

krow is one of the core Slash developers, and, as you might know, the Slash code is what is used at Slashdot and here at use Perl. Eventually, use Perl will switch over to the bender codebase.
