You Really Can't Ever Have Enough Books

pudge on 2000-02-25T14:14:27

Several new Perl books are hitting the shelves, from lots of different publishers: O'Reilly has Programming the Perl DBI by Alligator Descartes and Tim Bunce; Manning has Perl for Unix System Administration by Brian T. O'Neill; and Advice Press has Perl: Developer's Toolkit by Randy Kobes.
