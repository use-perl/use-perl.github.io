What Does Dominus Do With All That Spam?

pudge on 2000-02-10T12:10:03

In October 1999, Mark-Jason Dominus wrote an article for LinuxPlanet called My Life with Spam, or How I Caught the Spam and What I Did With it When I Caught it . In his followup for www.perl.com, he continues with Part 2 of the series, discussing domain pattern matching, and what to do with the domains once they are found.
