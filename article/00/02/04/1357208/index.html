The sixteenth issue of The Perl Journal has hit most newsstands and mailboxes and is now available to subscribers on the TPJ web site. This issue is focused primarily on poetry, with articles from Sean Burke about searching for rhymes and an article by Damian Conway written entirely in haiku meter.
