The POOP on Persistence

pudge on 2000-07-03T16:27:57

Michael Schwern writes: There's lots of modules out there for doing object persistence. Some are commercial, some are on CPAN, some are people's private little monsters. A lot of them are redundant. Few share code. None talk to each other.

Perl Object Oriented Persistence will attempt to recify this situation through the simple act of encouraging communication and archiving discussions amongst authors.

For discussion, development and general foo, join the POOP-group list. For announcements, join the POOP-scoop list.
