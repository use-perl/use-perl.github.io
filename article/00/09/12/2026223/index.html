Re:Interface with CORBA?

Elian on 2000-09-18T15:18:32

Consider the target audience for this magazine. Yes, CORBA/COM integration's already possible, and yes it's not taking up spot #1 on the "Gotta Have It" list, but doing it better would still be nice, and the article certainly doesn't mis-represent anything.

When one writes for a magazine, it's best to write articles that slant towards the general readership. The bias in this article's not at all bad, and the author went to some considerable trouble to get things correct. If the article had been for OracleWorld, for example, I'm sure DBD::Oracle and general database connectivity would've seen a higher profile.

End result is we got a nice, properly researched, positive article in a place with good visibility. I, for one, am rather happy with it. :)
