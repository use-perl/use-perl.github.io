Perl 6 Updates

pudge on 2000-09-08T15:48:23

We now have over 200 Perl 6 RFCs. Number 200 is from Nathan Wiger, "Objects: Revamp tie to support extensibility (Massive tie changes)."

In his www.perl.com update this week, Mark-Jason Dominus writes that the RFC phase will probably continue at least until next month, when Larry Wall announces the shape of Perl 6 at the Linux Showcase. In the meantime, he's written up a summary of the main Perl 6 working groups and discussion lists.

There's also a new mailing list, chaired by Dan Sugalski (the internals pumpking for Perl 6), called perl6-source-control.
