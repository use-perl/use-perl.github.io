Perl feedback for MySQL Magazine survey

grinder on 2008-05-31T06:46:00

brian_d_foy writes "Keith Murphy, the editor of MySQL Magazine, and Mark Schoonover have put together a comprehensive survey about MySQL and they asked me to make sure the Perl community knows about it so they can give their input. They've had over 200 responses so far, the they'll publish the results in the summer issue of MySQL Magazine."
