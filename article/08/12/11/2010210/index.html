Bulgarian Perl Workshop

brian_d_foy on 2008-12-11T20:30:00

I am (very) proud to announce the first ever Perl Workshop in Bulgaria.

It is on 31st of January 2009 (Saturday) in Sofia.

Official language is Bulgarian, but as I was informed, Bulgarians are good in English, thus English talks are welcome.

Alex, Jonathan and myself are already on the way to make up the itinerary.

The workshop is organized by OpenFest conferences and DeepText.
