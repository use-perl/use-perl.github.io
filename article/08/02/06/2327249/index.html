YAPC::Asia 2008, May 15-16 in Tokyo

brian_d_foy on 2008-02-06T23:47:00

miyagawa writes "YAPC::Asia committee is pleased to announce that YAPC::Asia 2008 is now scheduled on May 15-16, 2008 in Tokyo.

We are also announcing the call for presentations and early registration for the conference today. We are looking for 20 and 40 minutes presentations. The deadline for submissions is February 25th, 2008.

The conference website uses Act so you can sign in using your existent account if you've been to one of past YAPCs or Perl Workshops in North America or Europe. Special Thanks to Éric Cholet for helping us out setting up the site. We're working on Japanese translation of the site and hopefully will contribute back soon!"
