Oslo QA Hackathon

brian_d_foy on 2008-04-03T20:45:00

The Perl Foundation is supporting the Oslo QA Hackathon which is taking place this weekend. For past events, like Chicago and The Netherlands, we provided cash to cover costs. For Oslo, the organizers were set with the venue, but really wanted to bring in people from around the world. So we agreed to support the hackathon with some directed travel grants to David Golden and brian d foy. brian is blogging with progress over on use.perl.org.
