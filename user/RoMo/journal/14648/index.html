Two years ago in the noon i was sitting at my deskt chatting with some guys on ircnet's #apache channel.

I told the events to my colleagues. At first it sounded like a joke or something. But when the second plane crashed and the pentagon was hit there was no doubt that it was not an accident.

The company immediately reacted to prevent terrorism with 2 security guards standing next to the entry door until beginning 2002 and with the cancellation of the house-warming-party.
