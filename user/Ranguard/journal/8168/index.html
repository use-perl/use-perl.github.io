BIMA Award... we got 2!

Ranguard on 2002-10-04T11:35:25

The design agency which did our site (we did the back end they made it look pritty) had entered us into the BIMA (British Interactive Multimedia Association) awards and the event was last night. We were short listed for www.foxtons.co.uk in 2 categories.

We won the "Consumer and Public Services" but not the "Most Effective Communications". The big supprise came at the end when we were joint winners of the "Grand prix" award, www.habitat.net being the other winners.

It's nice to be recognised, especially when the design agency dragged us to a quiet club then puts a credit card behind the bar and starts of with 3 bottles of champers (which I don't really like, but it's the thought that counts) for 10 of us.

This morning was a last start... funny that...
