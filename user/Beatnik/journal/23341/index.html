I had 2 interviews today. The first one was for a Delphi job. I talked to the department chief. It was quite interesting, among other things asked stuff like which source control system I used. We ended up talking for about 2 hours and afterwards he asked me to send him the papers I did for YAPC::Eu. This job would be back to my roots for me. Around 5.30 PM I had another interview, with a techie this time. The job is for a company that sells SLA management tools. The techie had a list of questions from knowing where the hosts file is on Win32, how to start Registry Editor to what UDP or Spanning Tree is to explaining acronyms. I even knew what LOTR ment :) I answered to about 90% of the questions and answered correctly too!

Both people were really impressed so I'm happy for uhm ME!
