Beatnik on 2007-07-05T21:06:17

Belgian McDonalds restaurants have WiFi access for paying customers. They provide this service through a solution set up by one of the bigger ISPs in Belgium (in a previous life, I once crawled up on the attic of one of those restaurants to fix some cables for the AP). It just so happens that the AP has a quite common backdoor: UDP is unfiltered. When I used my PDA, Skype popped up and started connecting. I guess I'll be using NSTX any time soon now :)
