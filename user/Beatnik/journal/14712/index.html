Indiana Jones Trilogy

Beatnik on 2003-09-15T10:31:51

For the past 3 weeks, they broadcasted an Indiana Jones movie each sunday. Last night it was Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade. It's quite funny to see how Indy comes out of a sewer tunnel in Venice when that pit is actually about 1 meter deep. I've seen it :) I also wonder why there are still skeletons in the catacombs. Most remains have been gone for decades. I'm sure the catacombs in Venice aren't any different from the ones in Rome. Last but not least I wonder how Indy manages to get oil burning. When he pushed through a wall, he falls into a puddle of oil. He grabs a bone , wraps a piece of cloth around it and dips it into the puddle. Next thing you see is Indy wandering around the catacombs with a burning torch. Ofcourse pure oil doesn't burn. It needs to be refined properly.
