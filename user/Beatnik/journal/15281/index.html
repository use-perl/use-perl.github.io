  • Hacking Exposed (2nd Ed)
  • Hacking Exposed - Linux (2nd Ed) - Dutch
  • The Worst-Case scenario survival guide handbook - Dating and Sex (dutch)
  • The secrets of the vesuvious - Caroline Lawrence
  • The pirates of Pompeii - Caroline Lawrence
  • The odyssey - Homer
  • Professional Perl Programming
  • Professional Perl Development
  • The Encyclopedia of Alfred Hitchcock - Thomas Leitch
  • Bridget Jones's Dairy - Helen Fielding
  • Bridget Jones - The edge of reasoning - Helen Fielding
  • Rome - Art and Culture (this one is my favorite)

    • The sum I spent on these 12 books is...€89.50 (about the same amount in dollars). That's about 7 euros per book. Considering all are brand new and recent, it's a bargain. I didn't bought any cookbooks (this time) but there were tons of those as well.
