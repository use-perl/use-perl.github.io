Damn you, google!

Beatnik on 2006-05-18T12:13:46

One of the seniors got the same bug as I got a few weeks ago. He asked me if I had found any solutions to the problems and showed me the results google returned. The first that caught my eye was the post on my journal (the link was already in the clicked color). The conversation went something like this:

Me: Ah, I see you found my post
Senior: Yeah, I found your post on the mailing list
Me: No, I mean that post *pointing to the link to my journal*...*DOH*

The good news is that the problem is now solved. The other guy found out that the problem only occured in rows where there was a field empty. He also discovered that the solution was to install to DBD::Sybase version patched by the Vendor. That would also solve an issue with virtual databases.
