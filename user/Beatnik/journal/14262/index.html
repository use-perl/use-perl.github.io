Tonight on your TV screen #3

Beatnik on 2003-08-21T23:02:27

For Roseanna, with Jean Reno (one of my favorite actors) and Mercedes Ruehl.

The story: Marcello (Reno) is married to Roseanna (Ruehl). She has a terminal heart condition and will probably die really soon. There is a slight problem... There are only a small number of graves left on the cemetery and people die all the time. Roseanna would like to be burried next to her daughter and Marcello tries to do everything to fulfill that dieing wish.

It's both funny and emotional and I put it on my favorite movies list. I can really recommend this. There are no complex bits and it's very easy to watch... but the story is very solid.
