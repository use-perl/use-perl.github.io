I implemented access control on the administration as well as extensive user administration. Bordeaux is a wonderfull city.. with lots of pretty women. Speaking of pretty women. Expect some corny quotes in the next few days :D Tomorrow I will catch the train back to home and it'll be another weekend well spent.
