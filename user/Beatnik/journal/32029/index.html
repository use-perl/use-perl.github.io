The XMas loot this year contained a DVD for my collection: Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. I already saw it but it's still a welcome addition.

Other ones I saw recently: Harold & Kumar Go to White Castle: Hilarious! Go Rent! It has the chinese guy from American Pie and the indian guy from Van Wilder.

Team America: World Police: Recommended to me by a number of people.. Although it's a puppet movie, it's still damn funny! Go rent!

Code 46: strange movie. Not really one I'd recommend.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind: Probably the best movie I've seen in quite a while!
