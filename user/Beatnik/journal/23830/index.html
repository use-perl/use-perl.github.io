Long walk

Beatnik on 2005-03-24T22:55:00

After taking an extremly long bath, I walked over the the McDonalds and ordered a Royal with cheese.. I decided to head off to the cremerie but forgot to look up the address (which I realized when I was already near the Rue St. Denis). I walked around a bit until I reached the Centre de Pompidou (who makes up these names anyway??). Then I walked towards the Louvre, took a couple of shots of the glass pyramide and headed towards the Tour Eiffel (which is quite a walk), passed the Museu D'Orsay. I eventually took the metro at Iena station (near Trocadero).

Wearing headphones (even though they're not hooked up to anything) is quite handy.. It stops people from talking to you and doesn't give the touristy appearance.
