For 2008, I'm focussing on getting some certificates.. I started my quest by looking into the famous preparation sites (you know.. PDFs, test engines and shit). I've come to the conclusion that they're pretty much all scams. Prime example: Test King. There appear to be half a dozen sites with alike sounding names (testking-world, testkings, test-king, testsking,..) in a couple of TLDs. They all offer different pricings and different preparation material. I decided not to throw my CC information up for grabs to any russian, indian or chinese hacker that was clever enough to download a website template and threw in some forms for fun and profit.

I still haven't looked into fixing the mod_perl2 issues (or whatever I'll end up calling it in my 5 minute LT I'll submit for YAPC::Eu anytime soon now) but I took another jump into the unknown with Bootcamp. I dug up an 'old' XP CD and fired up the installation process. It all went relatively smooth and after inserting my Leopard CD and installing all the necessary drivers, everything seems to work just fine. Thanks Steve.
