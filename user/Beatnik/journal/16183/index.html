A few personal favorites

WebDragon on 2003-12-05T19:56:37

  • Jak & Daxter (collect *everything* and you get an extended ending, but it's fiendishly difficult in places. They just released Jak II which has been raved about by all the reviewers. looking forward to this)
  • Drakan (aerial combat a-dragonback, as well as on foot. lovely game, but the very very very end, left me hanging a little. After all the buildup, I expected more, but all-in-all a VERY enjoyable game)
  • GTA: Vice City (nuff said. vrooom! muhauahah. non-linear gameplay makes this one very very enjoyable, and a good way to get out those frustrations.)
  • Deus Ex (great combo of first person shooter, stealth, storyline. Loved this on my Mac so I got the PS2 version. Three different endings, and what you do along the way affects how the story unfolds. nice replay value.)

Just got the LOD expansion back for Diablo II for the Mac, so the ps2 is taking a well-earned breather, here. :-)
