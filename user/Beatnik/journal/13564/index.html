Italy trip journal - day 2 - 7 july 2003

Beatnik on 2003-07-20T15:40:02

The second day of our trip, we set out to visit the Forum Romanum, which was only 2 minutes away. Due to some strike, we couldn't get in. Our planned visit to the Colosseum also got cancelled due to strike. So we decided to walk around town a bit more, visit some squarres and ruins. In the afternoon, we visited the Piazza della Rotonda, the Pantheon and the Piazza Navona. We were free in the evening so we decided to head over to the Trevi fountain again, having our daily ice cream.
