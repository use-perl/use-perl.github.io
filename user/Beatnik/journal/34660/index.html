Electric shavers

Beatnik on 2007-10-12T12:34:03

A few weeks ago, I asked/got an electric shaver for my birthday. I was tired of not having the time to shave in the morning and I figured I could just do it on the way to work. It seems that, even with the on-the-road versions of these shavers, the shaving head can just pop off. I pulled up into the parking lot this morning and tapped off the machine through the open car window before getting out. I tapped too hard. The shaving head fell off (something it never did before when I tapped it, held it under the running water or even sprayed it with lube). Those shaving heads have a gazillion parts (well, about 11 anyway) and they all fell under my car. Fortunatly, I didn't crush any of them when I pulled my car back a bit to get to them and it took me a few minutes to puzzle it back together. Razor is working again although I will use some more lube before I use it again.
