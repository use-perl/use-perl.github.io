I've been seen as the penguin

Beatnik on 2003-02-27T21:06:29

Today was a blast. I wore a penguin suit all day (will put pics online ASAP). I got loads of contacts and apparently people didn't care I was wearing the suit, as long as the stuff I was saying made sense. For some odd reason, the suit gave me superhuman strength and I talked with loads of women (I got pics of me posing with some pretty ones too). I think a picture of me and some Adobe guy is now circulating at Adobe BENELUX.

I got quite a few fair goodies. Some large, yellow virus/security appliances vendor had a caricaturist so he drew me in the suit :) They also had someone blowing balloon animals (I got a balloon heart) and a drinking glass with logo. Besides that, I got loads of pens and candy (those candy hearts with stuff on it like Pretty, Call Me, Fax Me, Happyness, etc.), some mugs, a key leesh, some stress balls.

Fairs are fun..

Fairs are even more fun if you're running around in a penguin outfit and don't really care what people think about you.
