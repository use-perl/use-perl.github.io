Sex and romance questions...

Beatnik on 2004-03-07T22:38:15

The following questions were answered by 500 flemish citizens and are supposed to show how the average population thinks about sex and romance.

  • Your 14 year old daughter has her first boyfriend and asks you if she can start taking the pill. Do you allow her? 73% says Yes


  • You're tired and decide to go to bed early. Your partners goes to bed an hour later and you notice he/she is in the mood for sex. Do you fake being asleep? 24% says Yes


  • Did you ever pay for sex toys, movies, websites, etc? 12% has


  • You're single. At a reception, you meet a stranger to whom you are attracted. He/she offers having a one-night stand. Do you agree? 30% agrees


  • It's valentine and you're in bed. Your partners enters the bedroom, wearing edible underwear. Will you taste it? 69% will


  • A millionair offers you one million euros for a one-night stand with your partner. Do you agree? 14% agrees


  • You, and not your partner, gets offered one million euros to spend the night with a millionair. Do you agree? 19% agrees


  • You're at a party and your partner (who is drunk) is french kissing someone. Do you find it less offencing if he/she is kissing someone of the same gender? 43% finds it less offencing


  • Is an orgasm necessary while having sex? 37% thinks so


  • You're in a sauna and notice that one of your co-workers of the opposite gender is also present. Do you keep your towel on? 57% does


  • You're on a first date and fall in love with your date. Do you sleep with that person after the first date? 15% does


  • You work in the same company as your partner. All other employees have left and you're in the mood for sex. Do you have sex at work? 30% will


  • You have a new girl/boyfriend for the past 3 weeks. He/she suggests filming while you have sex. Do you agree? 11% agrees


  • Do you find yourself to be a good lover? 87% says Yes
