After ski party

Beatnik on 2007-04-04T06:47:17

Last weekend, we threw one of the biggest parties yet.. although our guests didn't agree. There were about 75 people on the guest list. Only about 20 showed up, most of them from my section of the guest list. Lots of people brought meat for the BBQ (Bring Your Own Cow BBQ), others brought beer, wine or liquor (Jagermeister!). We built a small skihut in the backyard, rented a kit to pull beer from the 50 liter keg of Stella Artois we also got. We even made some tartiflette (I still have about 3 whole Reblochon cheeses left). One of the guests brought english triffle.. By the end of the evening, the 50 liters of Stella was almost gone and all of us had a wonderful evening. Thanks guys!
