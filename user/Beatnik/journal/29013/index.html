iPod rants

Beatnik on 2006-03-16T10:58:21

I love my iPod.. but that doesn't stop me from ranting about it. I hit the iPod Rating bug rather quickly and now I'm used to waiting a bit between each click on the center button. iTunes gave me some headaches. It doesn't have DWIM :( I want to sync a random playlist, deleting the already existing music from my iPod. Smart Playslists kinda work but I seem to create a new one every time I want to update (that is, after I delete all the music from my iPod first). It doesn't automagically regenerate when I update (like I want). Update apparently means Add new songs but keep the old ones. Perhaps I just didn't bother reading the user manual.. but on the other hand, this application is used by meeeeeelions of people by now. Shouldn't this interface be a little more intuitive?

Gah! I sound like an average end-user like this!
