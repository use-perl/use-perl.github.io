Let's go to the movies.. let's go see the stars!

Beatnik on 2005-10-18T20:53:02

In the last 3 weeks, I went to the movies three times. I've wasted my money once. A small recap:

  1. Dark Water (2.5/10) : Not spooky, not exciting, thin storyline, vague characters, predictable ending.
  2. Four Brothers (6.5/10) : Story is so-so. Acting is OK. It has some unexpecting twists. Quite shocking and violent at times. Not Oscar material but good for some entertainment.
  3. The Brothers Grimm (6.5/10) : Story is OK.. fairy tale inspired. It has Monica Bellucci (although she'll never win a Grammy for 'best female performance' she's still OK as the wicked witch). Funny at times. Nice CG effects. You might notice Gareth from The Office (UK version).
Looks OK but didn't have the chance to see it yet:
  1. Broken Flowers : Bill Murray finds his long lost son by visiting each of his ex girlfriends.
