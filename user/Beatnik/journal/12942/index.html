WOW

Beatnik on 2003-06-19T10:51:41

We only had 5 minutes to introduce our non-JSP work for JSP exams. For some mysterious reason, my professor allowed us to turn in our mod_perl work we did at the internship for grades. He asked a bunch of questions... How many tables? 13... What are they called? blah blah blah... Are you using XML? No, but it can generate XML because we use templates the clean way. How many files are you using? 80, including tests. How many lines? 13000 but in Java that would be 5 times as much. etc.. After 5 minutes, WOW was all he could say. I think he would've said WOW WOW if he had given us 10 minutes instead. In the end, I actually handed him some graphs cvsplot made me.
