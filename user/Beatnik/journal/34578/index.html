Murphy at it's best

Beatnik on 2007-10-01T06:14:51

One of the things I got for my birthday is an electric shaver (the kind you can charge and then use cordless). These things run on batteries. Like all good things in life, batteries run out sooner or later. Unfortunatly, this happened in the middle of my shave... while I only had done half of a job.. without having the power cable anywhere near me (I was shaving while *cough* driving *cough*). I now look a bit like Twoface, except for all the ugliness. The good news is that it'll only take a few hours for my four o'clock shadow to appear. The bad news is, I leave the office at 5.30, which only gives me about 1.5 hours of looking like I need a shave worse than usual.
