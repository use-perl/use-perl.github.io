Extreme cabling... Sweet!

Beatnik on 2003-02-26T21:35:21

During the past 2 days, I hardly had some time for myself. Yesterday morning, I started working at 7 (which meant I was up at 6). We went off to do some cabling at the Info Security fair, where Better Access has a booth. I spent the better part of the day about 10 meters above the floor, stuffing those cardboard boxes full of Cat5 thru every ceiling beam I could find. We had about 3 boxes, today about half of the cable was used (quick math: approx. 450 meters). f34R MY 37H3Rn3t sK111S f0R 1 4M 13E7 *. Around 8.30 PM we headed to another fair where we had to do some more cabling and install a router and wireless base station. We headed home around 9.25. The party I was going to started around 10 so I left my bag in the car and met up with friends. Somehow, I partied til 3 AM (I confess I did have a good time... great company) and had a full 3 hours of sleep before my alarm clock went off again. The fair was officially opened today so I picket out some suitable clothes, shaved and met up with my boss. We are doing free security audits so I bothered potential customers all days, getting them to exchange business cards, get them interested in our services, products etc. Tomorrow I plan to ride around the fair on one of those push cart thingies... in a penguin suit.
