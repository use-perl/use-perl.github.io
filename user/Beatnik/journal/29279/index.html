GMail spam filter efficiency is dropping slightly

Beatnik on 2006-04-11T09:27:20

GMails spam filter seemed really good when I first got my account but it seems to be drop the ball once in a while these days. On an average day, I get about 1 spam mail in my mailbox for every 3 spam mails in my spam box. Ofcourse, it's not like I get tons of spam but on a good day, I delete about 20-30 viagra, vicodin, university degree, warez mails from my mailbox. Maybe putting my GMail account on CPAN wasn't such a good idea??
