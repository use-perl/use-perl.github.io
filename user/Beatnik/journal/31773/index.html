Last night I finally sat down and saw the final (for now) episode of Lost, season 3. It all turned out like nothing you'd ever expect! I'm in the undecided group of people right now.

I managed to have a look at the first half of Ultraviolet. It sucked bigtime! It has a nice setting and quite a few nice elements but Mila can't act in the one. It seems as if she can only pull it off if she has a good supporting cast. There is WAY too much sci-fi in this movie. The story is very shallow and has no special touch. I have the feeling that they tried to milk the comic book cash cow by putting Mila in tight costumes and adding big guns.

A co-worker introduced Torchwood as a lesbian alien hunter show. I have no idea what that's all about since I didn't have the chance to see it yet (that or Dr Who). In my turn, I introduced one of polish co-workers to Charmed.. Obviously not because of the good story line but because it has Alyssa Milano..
