Pesto Pasta!!

Beatnik on 2003-05-15T21:40:15

Gotta love cooking... and this time the Pesto was superb. I cook without a scale or a spoon (There is no spoon!). I just guess :)

  • a handfull of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 100 grams of parmesan cheese
  • 8-10 olives, without pits
  • 4 pieces of sun-dried tomatoes
  • 50 grams of pine nuts
  • loads of olive oil... add to own taste


    • Stuff that all in a blender and go crazy. I found that this is enough pesto to mix with some pasta and serve it to 3 people (well, 3 people in our family anyway).
