Business Lunch

Beatnik on 2003-11-13T06:10:20

After a long weekend of rest and relaxation, it was back to the office for me. We went out for lunch with some of our customers at a restaurant called Voltaire (which has a website with a really intense Frontpage feel to it). Anyway, I started with a fine Pineau, had a delicious chicory soup with crust. My main course was deer with cranberries and mashed potatoes (excellent deer!!) and some coffee afterwards. There was also wine and although I'm far from a wine lover, they were quite good. I wasn't all that productive anymore for the remaining part of the afternoon.

Note to my boss: Yes.. the waitress was nice!
