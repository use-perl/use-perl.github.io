11 Hours

Beatnik on 2005-02-13T13:03:32

I went on a date yesterday with someone I've been talking to for some time now. I was a bit afraid that it would turn out completly different but everything went smooth. We met around lunchtime, found a small tavern and had some food. We then drove over to Technopolis (which is like a science-meets-kids kinda place, with a lot of experimenting). We had a bit of fun and sat together figuring out a "towers of Hanoi" problem. After that, we went for a drink at another local pub and around supper time, we ordered some food and talked more. Around 11 PM, we decided to call it a day and head home. It were 11 hours well spent!

She's friendly, sweet, smart, has a sense of humor (the kind I like/have) and has a story to tell... Works for me!
