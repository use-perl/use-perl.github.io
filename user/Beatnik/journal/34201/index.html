Duplicate name

Beatnik on 2007-08-23T23:42:28

There is a time in any programmers life that, when he waits long enough, some application might appear that has the same name as one of the things he has beem working in for years. It's usually on the other side of the world, mine just happened on the same host, twice. I put my perl based cms on Sourceforge many, many years ago. It turns one some guy registered a variation of the name on sourceforge, planning a cms in PHP with similar features. On one hand, I'm curious to see what he ends up doing with it.. On the other hand, I can't stop and ask myself if people just don't do some quick research before the register something. The previous time someone did this, the project died soon after he changed the name.
