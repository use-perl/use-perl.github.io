IPW2200 on Drake

Beatnik on 2006-09-29T13:45:54

I had a Breezy Badger on my Dell Inspiron 6000. My IPW2200 based wifi connection dropped at very regular intervals and most of the time, I couldn't get it up and running again. After getting the latest version of the IPW2200 drivers and grabbing the kernel sources, I seemed to have broken my badger. I took the easy way out and upgraded to Dapper Drake. 45 minutes later, wifi was working again .. plus I had a shiny new desktop. Now I'll have to clean up apps, get all new funky apps inplace.
