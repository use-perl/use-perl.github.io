Swordfish

Beatnik on 2003-10-06T20:37:18

Well, here I was.. home a little earlier than usual. I had some time to spare so for once I just sat on the couch, watching a random DVD. Today's choice was Swordfish. After watching this movie, I'm considering a career as an IT consultant for the movie industry. I hope to make a living telling production crew how to make computer stuff credible. If you ignore all the IT eye candy, it's a rather silly movie. The Halle Berry boob scene is not enough to convince me to see it again and again and again. The part where the 'hacker' gets a blowjob while he's breaking a 128 bit key based encryption in less than a minute is also plain silly. The name of the finnish 'hacker' is Thorvalds. Now, I don't know how popular that name is in Finland but it's quite convenient.
