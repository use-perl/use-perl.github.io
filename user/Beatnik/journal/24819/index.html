By superimposing the result over the source, in Photoshop.
They line up perfectly, when the quoted rotations are made. If she'd changed position, the relative positions of her hips, shoulders and chin (amongst others) would have changed slightly, but they're exactly the same. The straps etc. hang in exactly the same way, except those moved by post-processing, in a reproducible manner, and the shadows are the same.
That analysis of the image was generated by copying the transformations - there really isn't any doubt that 'A' is the source of 'B'.
