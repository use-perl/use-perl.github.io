Module::Install 0.63 - Incremental Release

Alias on 2006-06-05T18:10:56

Module::Install 0.63 has been uploaded to CPAN.

This release has a variety of small fixes and changes.

An immediate upgrade is recommended for authors that use the auto_provides command, and you may wish to consider incremental release for any distributions that also include a Build.PL pass-through installer.

Otherwise you should not have to upgrade or release any distributions from 0.62, although as usual it is recommended that authors upgrade Module::Install promptly.

That is all.
