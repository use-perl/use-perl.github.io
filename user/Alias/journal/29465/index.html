Archive::Zip moves into my svn orphanage

Alias on 2006-04-28T15:05:48

Steve Peters has been concentrating his very limited time on the Perl core lately, and hasn't had the time deal with Archive::Zip for about a year.

A couple of dozen bugs have built up and not gotten fixed, one of which is causing some problems for Vanilla Perl.

So Steve has assigned me co-maint permissions for it, and Archive::Zip has moved into my collaborative svn repository.

While I only need to fix my particular itch, now anyone who has an account can fix their problem directly if they want, and I'll press the release button and upload the new version.

If you have an Archive::Zip itch to fix, ping me to have your CPAN id enabled.
