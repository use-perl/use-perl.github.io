Deaf

rafael on 2003-02-07T22:06:01

I'm still ill. The disease has now begun to infect my ears. As a consequence I'm half deaf. Temporarily. I've difficulties communicating with others -- more than usual, that is. I can't listen to music. (And playing music is no more fun.) Phone calls are painful. The treatment is heavy, I'm tired, and of course, alcohol and tobacco are stricly forbidden. As my nose is clogged, I don't even enjoy food.

My wonderful wife found a consolation : she just bought the DVDs of BtVS, season five. I can watch them -- there are subtitles.
