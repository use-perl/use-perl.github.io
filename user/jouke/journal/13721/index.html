YAPC::Europe 2003

jouke on 2003-07-28T05:28:08

The conference is over. I'm starting to feel a little alive again. We had a great time there. Many, many kudos to the organization, I think it's the best YAPC::Europe so far.

I had my own talk on Friday morning about 'Developing and Distributing Perl Applications for Microsoft Platforms'. I think it went well, because I had some very nice comments afterwards. But one doesn't learn from all positive responses, so I'm hoping to hear some negative points from people that attended my talk soon. I'm sure I can improve a lot. Anyway, the slides are online now at my website.

It was very good to meet old friends, and I even made some new friends there. I can't wait until next year in Belfast!
