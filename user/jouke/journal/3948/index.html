How to get there

ajtaylor on 2002-04-03T14:34:22

Make sure you know exactly how to get there, and how long it will take you. Then give yourself an extra 15 minutes because sh*t happens. People are stupid & get in accidents, you get a brain cramp and forget how to get there, etc. This has bitten me more than one time. Luckily it hasn't cost me an interview yet, but I'm working to make sure that never happens.
