OK...lenzo's journal entry embarrassed me. But it's nice to hear such kind words from someone like that!

I'm such a fool. Yesterday I had planned to go straight to my new house from work to clean it up...and before I was half-way there my car stopped...because I was OUT OF FUEL!! Gasstations nowhere in sight, totally unknown to the area I was in, it was raining like hell, and I started walking...after 3.5 km I found a gasstation, went the same 3.5 km back, was soaked to my skin and could continue my way. It was wayyy to late to start working in my house...

And if this were the first time I ran out of fuel, you could say "oh, that can happen once or twice", but this is the so-many-est time it happens. I keep forgetting it.

Tonight: next try...
