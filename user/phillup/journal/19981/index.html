I've done it.

email.pm
staff.pm


Sitting at the "top level" of my SitePerl directory. I'm glad the "name nazi's" have kept others from naming their modules so poorly, otherwise there would be a serious risk of name collision.

Which show a serious lack of imagination in their naming schemes.
