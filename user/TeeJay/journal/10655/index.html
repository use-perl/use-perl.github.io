vcg images from autodia

TeeJay on 2003-02-18T23:52:41

I have just uploaded version 0.4 of VCG to CPAN - this may take a while to work its way to the CPAN mirrors with all the server shit going on.

I have now managed to produce some nice VCG and graphviz UML charts with autodia

  • autodia.ps (postscript generated with VCG)
  • autodia.jpg (jpeg generated with graphviz)
  • autodia.pbm (bitmap generated with vcg)
You can find them at http://droogs.org/autodia/images/

Oh, the images vary between 48k and over 100k.. they may take a while to download.
