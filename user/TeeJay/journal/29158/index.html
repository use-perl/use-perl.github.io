OSDL discovers more to OSS than linux, at last

TeeJay on 2006-03-30T08:40:05

Looks like OSDL has, at long last, started to look at open source beyond the Linux Kernel and drivers.

They will be providing a fund for open source projects, just the kind of thing that could help the development of both Perl 6 and Parrot. It's on groklaw and el reg, as well as the osdl pages so I assume somebody from TPF will have noticed.. urls if you're interested are :

* http://www.regdeveloper.co.uk/2006/03/29/osdl_fund/ * http://www.osdl.org/lab_activities/fellowship_fund/ * http://www.groklaw.net/article.php?story=20060329124431968
