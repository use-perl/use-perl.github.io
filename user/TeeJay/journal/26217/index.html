maypole 2.11 development

TeeJay on 2005-08-10T09:37:26

maypole 2.11 development is moving slowly as I have been busy on workstuff, but all outstanding bugs for 2.11 have been fixed in SVN and I'm starting on the new features.

Thats pretty good progress, no longer firefighting silly mistakes and annoyances and able to focus on making it do what we need better.

The focus for 2.11 is to make Maypole even easier and quicker to develop with by making the factory templates and models provided even smarter and providing improved functionality in how requests are handled.

2.12 is going to continue that theme but also look at security and performance, basically fine-tuning the ease-of-use from 2.11, refactoring the insides and making it easier to develop secure web applications and harder to accidentally make your web application insecure.
