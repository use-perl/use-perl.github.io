Autodia 1.4 hits CPAN

TeeJay on 2003-03-03T23:36:38

At long last Autodia has hit CPAN.

You can also find it at the website (natch).

New features include escaping amphersans when exporting to XML, support for use fields, many C++ fixes, VCG and Graphviz support.

Soon we should have a working SQL and DBI handler and then I will start work on exporting and importing ArgoUML.

I got this from one of my users - it made me smile : 'The ancient chinese saying goes, he who does not contribute code should not bitch about the release date of his needed features'.
Its nice to know I have people who are a) interested and b) nice about it.
