autodia, CDBI and stuff

TeeJay on 2004-10-19T14:39:31

I just put a new version of Autodia (now at version 2.01) on the website and uploaded it to CPAN.

Its a bit of a minor release but useful if the bugs fixed affected your ability to use it :)

I have also been busy working on requisite relationships for my Class::PINT project - Class::DBI now has several new relationships after I got the ball rolling with IsA (Richard Hundt gave me the skeleton class and I documented and maintained and patched it with amendments from the CDBI list as well as my own code), then HasManyOrdered which provides a powerful ordered one-to-many or many-to-many with transparent side-tables rather than the hacks required with the vanilla CDBI has-many. Somebody else has also released a nice CDBI relationship for handling arbitary per-object attributes.. nice.

For my (officially over in a week) day job I have been working on porting a scary Tangram/PSA/Fascgi/jsrpc monster into a simpler friendlier (and working) version based on Class::DBI, Class::PINT, and mod_perl. One of the side-effects is that I now have 90% of a nice CDBI database and class generator, I plan to integrate it with my autodia classes so that I can generate a CDBI application from Umbrello or Dia or Druid schema, or even port from a non-persistant class.
