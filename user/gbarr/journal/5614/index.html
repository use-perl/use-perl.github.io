Writing a book

gbarr on 2002-06-12T18:04:15

After years of saying I would write a book, I have finally found a co-author. Now it might actually happen.

Robbie Allen and I are planning a book of LDAP programming with perl.

Today we got a reply from O'Reilly, they are not interested in it. But the funny thing is, the response we got was identical to a previous response I got about another submission. It goes along the lines

"We did some research and we predict the audience is too small. However we have a book currently in the works where we may need some more authors....."

Ah well, next publisher
