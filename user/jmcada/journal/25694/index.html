Behringer products

jbodoni on 2005-07-14T09:51:11

I am completely sold on this company. I know that others have had quality issues with them, but my luck has been good.

I've got a 1204fx-pro that everything (stereo, TV, guitars, bass, laptop) plugs into.

Outputs go to my bass amp for normal listening and my ha4600 headphone amp for late-night listening.

Bass and guitar inputs are run through my patchbay, then into my bass v-amp pro which then plugs into the mixer. The v-amp is controlled by my footboard.

My band uses a shark for feedback control on the vocals channels of our mixer.

We have a headphone amplifier for practicing, too.

Whew, that's a lot of hrefs. :)
