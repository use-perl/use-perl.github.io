YAPC day 1: lunch

jdavidb on 2002-06-27T15:45:28

If I had wireless, you could see these in realtime, instead of the next day. I'm foregoing checking my email to post these.

Box lunches were provided for the first day. It looked like by the time I got there all that was left was vegetarian meals. However, it turned out the "Hail Caesar" lunch I got was a chicken Caesar salad wrap. Pretty good, and I can pretend I ate healthy because of the salad part. The lunch included a one ounce bag of chips. I don't think I ever saw a one ounce bag of chips before. I'm by no means complaining because the lunch was great and I didn't realize they were going to feed us anyway.

After lunch I posted my accumulated journal entries from the same room where the rt talk had been. The next talk in the room was Simon's Perl for system administrators, which he said was only for people who barely knew Perl. He recommended MJD's secrets of the ivory tower talk instead, except for people who'd already seen, for whom he recommended Damian's Perl 6 talk.
