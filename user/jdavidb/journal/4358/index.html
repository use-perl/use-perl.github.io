X-Files and the Lone Gunmen

jdavidb on 2002-04-22T03:38:37

If you're like me, you quit watching X-Files a long time ago. By dumb luck, I happened to catch tonight's episode, which featured the Lone Gunmen, complete with Yva Harvey Oswald and Jimmy. This was a good story. I watched hoping it would be funny (I thought the spinoff series was the funniest show I had seen in a long time; it is a real shame it got canned.), and was instead treated to a very serious story of heroism.

If you missed the episode, get out and find someone who taped it! I think you will be glad you did, even if the main show doesn't seem interesting anymore.

If you did see it, post here and tell me what you thought about it!
