In the last two months I've been bitten at least eight times by the fact that in an Oracle TO_DATE or TO_CHAR function call, the 'HH' conversion code produces (and expects) a 12 hour time instead of a 24 hour time. I'm trying to decide whether it's Oracle or myself that's brain damaged. Maybe the most recent frustration today will be enough to make me remember.
