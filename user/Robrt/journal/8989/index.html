American Idol Camp Out

Robrt on 2002-11-17T06:46:20

This Monday, FOX is holding the Los Angeles area American Idol 2 auditions. In preparation, hundreds of people have camped out at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena to get to the head of the line. Temperatures will range from 50F to 85F.



So, on Monday the auditions are going to be very fun: Imagine thousands of smelly, wrinkled, twenty-somethings with sniffily noses singing their hearts out..
