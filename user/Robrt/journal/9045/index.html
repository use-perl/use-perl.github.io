Color Kinetics

Robrt on 2002-11-20T23:42:11

A few years ago I learned of Color Kinetics which made LED based lighting instruments with a 24 bit color range. One of the examples they liked to use was being able to change white roses at a party to any color. They were always too pricey for me to use in theatrical lighting.



They've gone consumer with CKSauce, where you can buy cool color changing lights. I think one of these might be a holiday gift for myself.
