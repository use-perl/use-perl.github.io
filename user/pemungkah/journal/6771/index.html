OSCON and after (mostly after)

pemungkah on 2002-07-30T17:40:33

Where to start? I ended up in one of Julian's cool concept shots (which came out really well and is now plastered all over oreilly.com - !).

I admitted I was crazy enough to want to comment the debugger, and then eventually rewrite and replace it; apparently a lot of people think this is a good idea. Yes, Hugo, I will have a patch soon, and I will backport it to 5.6 and 5.004, too. (Secretly, I'm really enjoying myself. Seriously.)

Sessions were great; fragged and was fragged at the LAN party until they shut us down; pointed Dan at a Scheme interpreter written in Inform and running on the Z-machine; had a great time, and I'm happy.

Oh, and I got to see Chip shut Richard Stallman down, and quite appropriately at the time, too. :)

I was rather sad to see the Sheraton from the air as I left on Saturday. I miss everybody, though it's good to be home (aside from 110-degree heat advisories, which I do not miss).

I will put my lightning talk up as a journal entry later today.
