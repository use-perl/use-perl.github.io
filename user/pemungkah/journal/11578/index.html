Fading memory and fine music

pemungkah on 2003-04-10T17:11:53

I ran across quite a bit of really fine music at The Doubtful Palace, a web site run by a fellow member of an electronic music mailing list. I mention "fading memory" because I can't remember who it is here who likes shaped-note singing (and I can't seem to find that via searching the site) - but whoever it is should check out Shouting Distance. A really fine track called "Inverness". But nearly anything from that site is just really fine. Slaw's Snakes and Ladders contains one of the most beautifully harmonized versions of "Greasy Grimy Gopher Guts" ever. :)
