Module::CoreList and /Regexes/

ferreira on 2006-06-12T13:15:02

Have you noticed that the corelist utility (from Module-CoreList-2.05 distribution) now accepts regexes as arguments?

You may say

$ corelist /Data::/

And you get

Data::Dumper was first released with perl 5.005

Obviously, it is on CPAN:

http://search.cpan.org/user/rgarcia/Module-CoreList-2.05/

And Rafael has just upgraded the module in blead as well.

http://www.nntp.perl.org/group/perl.perl5.changes/16163
