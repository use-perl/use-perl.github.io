O&amp;M T-Shirts for Web-CPAN Patches

Shlomi Fish on 2006-08-23T16:18:18

As a special offer, the Web-CPAN.berlios.de project will offer an Ozy and Millie T-Shirt to the first five people who will write a substanial patch to it. Read more about the offer.

The offer is meant as a way to encourage contributions to the Web-CPAN projects, and to bring some publicity (and money) to the "Ozy and Millie" online comics, which is very cool, funny and beautifully drawn.
