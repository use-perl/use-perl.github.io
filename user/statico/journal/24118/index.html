chocolate chip cookies

statico on 2005-04-11T04:18:39

From a Hershey's chip wrapper. These are damn good and the recipe is college-friendly as long as you use a roommate instead of a mechanical mixer.

Ingredients

  • 2-1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 3/4 cup granulated (white) sugar
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar (make sure to pack solid when measuring)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups (12 oz) of semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Stir together flour, baking soda, and salt in first bowl.
  3. In second bowl, beat butter, granulated (white) sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla in a mixer until creamy
  4. Add eggs to second bowl, beat well.
  5. Put contents of first bowl into second bowl gradually
  6. Stir in choclate chips
  7. Drop rounded teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet
  8. Bake 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned.
  9. Cool slightly, then remove from cookie sheet. Cool completely.
  10. Eat.
