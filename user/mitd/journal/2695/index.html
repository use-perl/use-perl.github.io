In the beginning...

mitd on 2002-02-07T03:03:34

I have resisted the blogging (or plogging) for sometime, preferring to use my old .plan and restricted finger access.

But having recently become addicted to Doc Searls Blog I have decided to spit, sputter, and vomit my some own experiences with Perl.

I have been warned by some of my blogging friends not to start off with great historonic dirge of how I owned the computer made from molten lava.

"Start with the present and the past will take care of itself." they yell.

So here is my one biographical sentence. I am 49, have 1 wife and 7 kids and I own a first edtion Pink Camel, autographed by Larry and Randal and held together with duct tape.

I am presently going through the Template Toolkit and I think after years of fighting with CGI's, PHP, Servlets etc I think there is finally a server-side tool I can really live with. The results should start to appear soon on the openphase.com and mitd.com websites.
