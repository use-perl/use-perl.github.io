Jerusalem.pm October Meeting Announcement

offerk on 2004-10-21T20:16:15

On Wednesday, October 27th, the Jerusalem Perl Mongers will hold their regular monthly meeting. The program:

  • 18:00-18:30 -- Motke Keshet: 'Reinventing dates'
  • 18:30-18:45 -- Motke Keshet: 'Charsets to utf - the Perl way'
  • 18:45-19:00 -- Break and refreshments
  • 19:00-20:00 -- Zach Kessin: 'An introduction to Web Services' (in English)

The event will take place in the Exlibris offices, Malcha Technology Park, Building 9, 4th floor. Attendence is free.
For more information, see http://jerusalem.perl.org.il/ or contact Eitan.
