Israel-PM April 2004 Meeting

offerk on 2004-03-25T06:48:05

Once a month, the Israeli Perl Mongers meet in order to exchange information, teach each other and get updated about new developments, technologies, and methods. These meetings complement the busy mailing list. The upcoming Israel-PM meeting will be held on Thursday, April 1 2004. The program:
18:00 - 19:00 -- assembly and social gathering before the lectures
19:00 - 19:40 -- Roman M. Parparov: Using Perl for Geophysical data
19:45 - 20:45 -- Uri Bruck: A SOAP server implemented in Perl
21:00 - 21:20 -- Ran Eilam: A natural flow for web apps

Location - Dapey Zahav building, 23 Abba Hillel St., Ramat Gan. Attendence is free. Additional details can be found on the meeting page.
