Israel-PM May 2004 Meeting

offerk on 2004-05-03T06:47:23

On Thursday, May 6th, the Israeli Perl Mongers will hold their regular monthly meeting which includes intersting lectures. The program this week:

  • 18:00-19:00 -- Assembly and light refreshments
  • 19:00-20:30 -- Pinkhas Nisanov will lecture about TRIZ - Theory of Inventive Problem Solving. Background for the lecture can be found here and here.
  • 20:30-20:45 -- Break and refreshments
  • 20:45-21:30 -- Gaal Yahas will lecture about Perl as an integration language in a Java environment. Background for the lecture can be found here.
  • As usual after the meeting we will go out to a nearby pub/coffee-shop/etc. for a post-meeting social gathering.


The event will take place in the Dapey Zahav building, 23 Abba Hillel St., Ramat Gan.
Attendence is free, but a 10 NIS donation to help buy the bourekas will be welcome.
