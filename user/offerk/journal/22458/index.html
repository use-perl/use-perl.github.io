Jerusalem.pm December 2004 Meeting Announcement

offerk on 2004-12-26T09:23:15

On Wednesday, December 29th, the Jerusalem Perl Mongers will hold their regular monthly meeting. The program:

  • 18:00-18:05 -- Assembly and light refreshments
  • 18:05-18:35 -- Zach Kessin : 'Introduction to XSLT'
  • 18:35-18:45 -- Break and refreshments
  • 18:45-19:15 -- Issac Goldstand: 'Online Payments'
  • 19:15-19:30 -- Raffles

The event will take place in the Exlibris offices, Malcha Technology Park, Building 9, 4th floor. Attendence is free.
For more information, see http://jerusalem.perl.org.il/ or contact Eitan.
