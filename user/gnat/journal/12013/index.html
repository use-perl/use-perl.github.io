Telephone Spam

gnat on 2003-05-05T17:48:53

Phone rings.

Female Texan voice: Hi, I'm from MUMBLE insurance, we have a new branch opening in your area and ...

Nat: Actually, we're on the no-call list. Can I have your name and number please?

Voice: This isn't a solicitation, we have an agent who will call and give you a free quote.

Nat: That's nice. Can I have his name and number, please?

Voice: Do you want me to have him call you for a free quote?

Nat: No, I want to call and complain.

Voice: I won't do that. click

Man, she hung up on me. Illegal and rude!

--Nat
