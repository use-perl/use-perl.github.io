Genius

gnat on 2002-09-25T15:22:58

One of the winners of this year's MacArthur Foundation genius grants is Edgar Meyer. He's an awesome musician and had an incredible sense of humour. His arrangement of Bach's cello suites for the double bass is amazing, but I'm just as big a fan of his bluegrass work. See Strength in Numbers and Skip, Hop & Wobble for two of my top five albums.
