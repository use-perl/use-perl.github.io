The next YAPC

gnat on 2002-09-20T17:03:56

I know, you're all reading acme's journal and are pining for YAPC::Europe. But we're already thinking ahead to YAPC::America::North for 2003. If you've been thinking about putting together a proposal, now's the time to do it!

You'll need to read the venue requirements and talk to people who have done it before. We're working on getting some past proposals online so you can see what to aim for, but basically we just want a budget with line items for room rental, food, projectors, and evidence that there are cheap hotels nearby.
