Movie Reviews

gnat on 2003-12-27T17:16:31

Flying reviews of movies I've seen in the last few weeks ...

Timeline: avoid. Slow and dull. Dear Mr Director: Why should we care about these characters?

Count of Monte Cristo: Not as bad as Nat's prejudice against fancy dress would have you believe.

Return of the King: W00t! Long and strong.

Love, Actually: Brilliant. Fantastic.

Looney Tunes: Back in Action: not bad for a kids movie. Better than Cat in the Hat.

Bad Boys 2: First half is very funny, then it goes over the top and just seems over-long.
