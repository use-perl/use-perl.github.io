ASFSA Launches Quantity- and Family-sized Recipe Database

The American School Food Service Association has launched a recipe database at http://www.asfsa.org/recipes2/. Each recipe offers quantity-sized and family-sized versions. Recipes are searchable by keyword, while the advanced search offers additional search options such as ingredient, food type, theme or meal pattern.
