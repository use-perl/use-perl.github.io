Monday at OSCON

gnat on 2003-07-08T05:47:23

(addendum to Sunday: went to the bar for 45 minutes, had a drink and chatted with obra, Paul Grassie, and others. Jesse (obra) has a great story about how much sex other people have had from his software--well worth the price of a beer if you're ever in the same bar as him ...)

Things that went well on Monday:

  • Kids (no more meltdowns, and Jenine had 'em for all the day)
  • Registration (until the network went down, see below)
  • People (#perl, p5p, perl6, and civilians--I met one guy who's attended the last FOUR conferences! That has to be worth a medal!)
  • Sessions (I did my time in the Perl testing tutorial)
  • the wiki (I'm a convert)
What didn't go well
  • network (UDP floods and poorly-worded "turn off mDNS!" warnings)
  • food (the lack thereof was disconcerting to folks)
  • the hunt for icecream tonight (a lot of the route we wanted to take was under construction, but thanks to Allison for persistence and taking on the almost-thankless task of finding me an affordable sugar fix)
  • the book store (Powell's opened without O'Reilly books, their shipment having been delayed --so the bookstore at the O'Reilly conference had (for a brief while) no O'Reilly books! All fixed now, though)
