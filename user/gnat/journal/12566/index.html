Old Acquaintance Not Forgot

gnat on 2003-06-02T20:00:11

I met Mark Leighton Fisher when I was teaching Perl at Thomson Electronics. It was my first and only visit to Indiana, and now all I remember of Indiana is getting a zippy car from Avis, the swoop of the freeways, the rain, and reading PerlMonks from the hotel room. Thomson was fun, with a lot of serious Perl folks there. Mark was the one who organized the training, so he was obviously the smartest one there :-)

Anyway, that's a long way of leading up to saying that Mark has a use.perl journal. And a CV, as he's looking for a job. If you're in Indiana and need a smart Perl guy, do look him up.
