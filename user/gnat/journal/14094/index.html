112 Gripes About the French

gnat on 2003-08-13T17:09:17

112 Gripes about the French is a real booklet from WW II. Enjoy the answers to such gripes as:

  • "We came to Europe twice in twenty-five years..."
  • "The French aren't friendly."
  • "Why do Frenchmen look so shabby?"
  • "French cities are filthy."
  • "The French are unsanitary."
  • "The French don't bathe."

and that old favourite:

  • "French women are too damned expensive."

If only I'd known about this in time for YAPC::Europe!
