Uncle Webmaster

mattriffle on 2004-02-28T04:00:21

Since I was putting pictures of my new nephew up on my web site pretty much 10-15 minutes after my brother-in-law sent them out, he and I have agreed that he'll send them just to me and I'll point everyone else to the site when it's updated. Thus, I am Jacob's first webmaster. :)

I thought it would be cool to register jacobdwallace.com for him, so I did (no other .com variation was available).

This was all well and good, till I remembered I have 5 other nieces and nephews, all with their own names and nicknames. In the end it cost me $120, and that's with my $8/domain year staff rate.

I'm trying not to think about the overall cost of keeping them all active for the next decade or so. :)
