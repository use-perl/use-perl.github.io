OSCON 5

acme on 2003-07-14T08:26:11

The last day of OSCON was a little quieter. I actually managed to make it up early enough for the keynotes, and have decided that Miguel de Icaza is a complete nutter. I mean this in a good way.

After that I squeezed into the very full "Building a Smarter Search Engine: Artificial Stupidity", where Maciej showed us pretty graphs and explained context graphs. I'd looked at the Search::ContextGraph module before and had been confused, but now it is all clear to me. Then I attended Robert and Ask's "Single Signon in Perl" and oooooh and aaaaahed at Milton's LOTR CGI.

The last day ended at lunchtime, but instead of going to the zoo with everyone we locked ourselves in a dark room and hacked on, errr, something. We got quite far. Wait and see.

Another great conference and I now have so many new ideas and so little free time. Thanks Nat and Vee and all!
