Second Life

pne on 2006-07-14T11:59:38

Second Life is different things to different people.

For some, it's a graphical chat room; for others, a place to make clothing out of textures or buildings out of geometrical primitives and sell them; and lots more. I suppose it depends what you want to do.

I imagine that if you want to make money, you'd need to get into the real estate business (though it's probably hard to get a foothold there). Creating and selling content is probably next.

I've met some nice people there, but I don't spend hours on end in Second Life.
