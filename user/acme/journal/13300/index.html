After last night's craziness we got up at a rather reasonable 9am, popped down for some muffins and OJ and headed off across town to meet sky and coral. They had found a nice cafe by the park (Market and Park) with free wireless and power. I may go back there again. We then met up with gnat and headed off to The Mac Store where we all gazed at the new iSight. James and I snagged one, and we headed back for lunch, bumping into Casey.

Casey then dragged us into the Perl Bugathon, where I squashed lots of bugs and made a couple of parrot patches. It was surprisingly satisfying resolving those bugs. I then helped out Vee by being a guinea pig for the speaker registration process.

A lot of laptop hacking later on, and after doing a lot of laptop wireless tech support we decided to create a breakaway dinner plan and after walking through Chinatown we went to a steak place where I had a lovely rib eye for dinner.

[Insert minor scenes of drinking and meeting people here]. Then we got tired and headed home at 11pm. Sleep beckons, but tomorrow the conference starts for real...
