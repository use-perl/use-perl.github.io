Well, it finally happened. I got around to putting my website under Apache 2 and mod_perl2.

It wasn't very hard at all, because I was using OpenFrame. However, I was using a really old version of OpenFrame and had to fix a few things (eg: configuration and cleanup segments changed) to get it to work. And of course, I just used OpenFrame::Segment::Apache2 and everything magically worked. Well, I was in shock. Also latest news is that Apache::Request for mod_perl2 is well on its way now.
