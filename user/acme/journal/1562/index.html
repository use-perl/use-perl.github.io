Spyro

chaoticset on 2001-12-09T21:33:41

I generally avoid anything that attempts kid-friendliness, but I ended up playing _Spyro_ for a few scant moments and suddenly realized I liked it.

Games with such direct, simple interfaces always intrigue me. I think an inverse relationship exists between interface complexity and world richness sometimes. (That is, the designers will occasionally get so bogged down working a flexible interface together that they forget to bother with level design or plot.) _Spyro_ made up for limited controls with massive, massive worlds, much the same as _Max Payne_ did.

I just compared _Max Payne_ and _Spyro The Dragon_. Clearly I could use a drink. :)
