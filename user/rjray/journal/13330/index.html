OSCON, Tuesday

rjray on 2003-07-08T18:51:44

So far, my OSCON experience has been fair to partly cloudy. Partly cloudy in that my lack of sleep through the day Sunday meant that I was still pretty wiped-out on Monday. Today, thankfully, I'm more of myself again.

Thus far, the biggest talk of the con are the persistent WiFi problems that are being encountered. The good folks at O'Reilly are doing their best, but I dread what will happen when the unwashed masses flood in tomorrow.

Notes of note:

  • Looks like an XForms book from Micah Dubinko coming from ORA. XForms is something that I'm quite interested in. Nice to see a book...
  • The OSCON Wiki has a page set aside for the ongoing wireless network problems
  • There will be a BoF session Wednesday night from 7:00 to 8:00, for the SOAP::Lite toolkit. I'll be there, along with Paul Kulchenko (my co-author on Programming Web Services With Perl), the author of SOAP::Lite. There will be some announcements about the future of SOAP::Lite.
