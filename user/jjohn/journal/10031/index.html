State Secrets is coming along slowly. It's a hobby so I'm in no hurry to finish, however I hope to make further progress soon. However, I did manage to get the game to save your secrets and rumors to disk so that when I reset the server, your inventory will be restored. Play around and enjoy the crazy things characters say. Oh, your movement will be restored every day a 1AM EST.
