Funeral Quest

jjohn on 2002-09-24T19:45:02

I loved the Seth Able BBS Door game Legend of the Red Dragon. Seth has done it again for the web. It's a flash based game called Funeral Quest, where you compete in the cut-throat world of mortuary business. It's got all the outré humor you've come to expect from Able and a bag of chips. Run. Don't walk.

Did I mention it's free to play?
