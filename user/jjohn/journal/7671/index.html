Family Affair

pudge on 2002-09-11T12:14:21

Me, wife, and child sent our email support for the show!

This whole thing is really odd to me. Better than 90% of the people I know who watch Sci-Fi channel watch Farscape. Yet Sci-Fi says that the show hasn't gotten past its core audience, into the mainstream. Hello? This is the Nerd Channel. Nothing about your channel will ever be mainstream. The best you can hope is to get most of the nerds, and by my experience, Farscape does a good job in that demographic.

I have a suspicion that Nerds are not properly represented in the Nielsen ratings.

My letters

jjohn on 2002-09-11T15:04:42

I have sent an email to program@www.scifi.com and I have send an online petition. Additionally, I'm sending this letter to three people at SCIFI.

Dear [INSERT NAME],

I was much dismayed to hear that the SCIFI Channel has decided not to pick up Farscape's fifth season. SCIFI is only one of six channels that I watch regularly and Farscape was the primary reason I tuned into that channel. As you know, Farscape has been lauded by critics and fans. It has won several awards and it has performed very well for SCIFI.

Farscape is a classic Space Opera. It has story arcs that span several episodes and even different seasons. In order for new viewers to get into the show, it is imperative that SCIFI show reruns of Farscape. Last year, after I had gotten into the show (because of reruns), I told several friends about Farscape. Unfortunately, this was shortly before the first the four month hiatus of 2002. Because I believed in the quality of the show so much, I bought my friend a couple of the DVDs.

He and his girlfriend became hooked on Farscape and eagarly awaited new episodes.

I'm an unofficial and unpaid salesperson for SCIFI. Help me to help you sell Farscape to a wider audience by showing reruns and funding a fifth season of this truly marvelous show. This short term investment will have long term benefits for SCIFI, as new fans discover the magic of Farscape.

Thank you for your consideration.

Please steal the body of the message and mail, fax or telegram SCIFI at the addresses listed here. Remember, politeness counts.
