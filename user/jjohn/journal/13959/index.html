Farscape 3.4 on DVD obtained

jjohn on 2003-08-06T20:43:12

While waiting for the immanent return of Farscape, I've been collecting the series on DVD. ADV films has done a wonderful job of making these DVDs. The extra features included on these discs are context-sensative, so that when you read something about a character like D'Argo, the information is relevant to that season covered (life about Moya can be complicated at times). Season 3 was a strong one for Farscape and one episode I've longed to see again is on this DVD: Revenging Angel, in which (Moya) Crighton gets knocked into a "coma" and the viewer is treated to a delightful montage of Warner Brothers style cartoons.

I love it when the Farscape writers go nuts.
