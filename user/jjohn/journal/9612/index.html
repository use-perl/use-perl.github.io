Two Towers seen

jjohn on 2002-12-24T01:55:51

Peter Jackson's second installment of Tolkien's Lord of the Rings sage delivers on the promise of the first episode. Two Towers doesn't waste time on recapping events of the previous movie. It's full-throttle epic story telling that's genuinely gratifying to watch. I note that Jaskson takes more liberties with the Two Towers, but the movie works. Go see it and enjoy.
