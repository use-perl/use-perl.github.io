bikemap

kag on 2005-04-16T02:34:09

Grab a copy of Rubel's Bikemap (both the Boston and the Eastern Mass editions). Has topographical info and recommended roads.

I usually follow the Charles when doing Boston-Waltham. Depends on where in Waltham you want to be, I suppose. The only use for Trapelo is to test your hill-climbing gears.

Main St. out of Waltham (Rt. 117) is a decent ride through Weston and Lincoln. (I was going to Lincoln, so haven't pushed it any further.)

Rt. 1 through Topsfield is my worst experience with "should've checked the topo first".
