New Safari Beta

ask on 2003-01-13T00:58:27

Apple quietly(?) released a new beta of Safari one of the last few days; probably Friday. New User-Agent strings looks like:

Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X; en) AppleWebKit/51 (like Gecko) Safari/51

where before they were

Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X; en-us) AppleWebKit/48 (like Gecko) Safari/48

(via metamark logs and Marcus on #perl)
