My experience

acme on 2002-09-06T11:28:52

Just after OSCON2002 I bought myself a Canon Elura 40MC which I'm very impressed with. (Disclaimer: I work for Canon). My key reason for choosing it was its size: it is smaller than my old Kodak still digital camera! I chose Canon as they're generally good at hardware, and have good image stabilisation (much more important than you think). The 40MC has DV in, which is also dead useful: hard disk is still quite expensive so it's good to move everything back to tape. The zoom is pretty good. I haven't actually taken any photos with it (why? ;-) and basically I'm very happy with it. Oh, it doesn't have a built-in light, which could be a problem but I've been ok so far. iMovie rocks.

Of course, I haven't had much experience with other cameras. I recommend you play with one in real life first.
