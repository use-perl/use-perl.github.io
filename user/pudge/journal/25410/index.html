ziggy on 2005-07-05T18:02:31

It seems like one of the unstated fallacies here is that there is exactly one kind of "socialization" going on here.

I may have been well-socialized going to a whitebread public middle school, but that certainly didn't give me the skills I'd need for navigating a crack house or a shooting gallery.

Similarly, the kind of socialization one learns in high school is very different from what one learns in college, and even varies by college to college. (Commuter schools are different from resident schools, suburban campuses different from urban ones, and add community colleges, transfer schools, strong/weak graduate programs and such into the mix.)

Funny thing is, I never stopped to think about it until I read that Jon Stewart comment...
