You're Clueless

pudge on 2004-11-08T07:53:05

You're Clueless is a song I wrote about 10 years ago. I performed it back then (with my band, Whirlygig) at the grand opening of the campus coffee house at Biola University, the alma mater of me and John Thune, the guy who just beat Tom Daschle for the Senate in South Dakota.

Speaking of politics: the recent election season and its aftermath, and all the people calling everyone ignorant ... it reminded me of this song. So I dusted it off and recorded it with GarageBand.

Enjoy!
