Best DVD Set Evar

pudge on 2005-07-22T16:03:57

I have been waiting for this DVD set for many months. Finally they are releasing it. I kinda wish they could split up the ALCS and World Series into two separate collections, because, frankly, I really only care about having the ALCS. But then again, in 50 years, when the Sox still haven't won again, it will be nice to have the World Series discs too.

Twelve discs, 35 hours, every minute from every game of the 2004 ALCS and World Series. Just awesome. Although -- and this is a given -- I really wish I could overdub the audio with something sane and reasonable. If I had an infinite amount of time, I would download the audio from MLB radio, rip the DVDs, and replace Buck and Tim.
