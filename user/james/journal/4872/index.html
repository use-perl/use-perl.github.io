Let Me Sleep!

james on 2002-05-13T04:15:04

So I was really sick last week, caught a flu bug somewhere (and I mean real flu, where you get into bed and don't get out for a few days). I slept a lot, and now I am paying for it.

Massive Insomnia. I've slept like 5 hours in the last three days and its doing my head in. I've read just about everything I've had stacked up to read, I've played computer games, I've written code, I've done everything I can think of. Since when was nighttime so boring

Seriously, when I was younger I didn't need much sleep, but it didn't matter, I could still find things to do night after night. Three days in to this current bout of non-sleepyness and I'm struggling for anything that will make the time go by, and hopefully make me sleepy.

Ah well, at least I got some presentations done...
