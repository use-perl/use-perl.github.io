ENOPERLCONTENT

I'm in Spain for my Father's 50th Birthday. You may or may not remember how late they have a tendency to stay up.

Because of course, its my Fathers birthday lots of his side of the family is in town (which is Vitoria, Spain). Last night we had a big family meal at an argentinian steak house which was really nice. Everybody ate more than their fair share's worth and a good time was had by all.

Afterward it had all been tidied away the time was about 0200. Of course Katrien and I were knackered. So my parents took us back to their flat, and then they went out again.

Its so strange wondering what time your parents are going to get in...

So, anyway, its roughly 1130 right now, on the day of my Dad's birthday. I would stick around to give him his present, but he's not woken up yet. Sigh...

We're going out in to the old town now, which will be quite nice, and I suppose we'll see them when we see them.
