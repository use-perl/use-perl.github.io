North of England perlmongers groups

g0n on 2007-01-21T13:58:18

Thanks to a DNS provider allowing the g0n.net domain to lapse without telling me, the north of england mailing list existed for a couple of months, then promptly died. It did at least spawn Cumbria.pm before its untimely demise.

Thanks to Andy of Cumbria.pm, the northofengland discussion list has been re-established here. Anyone interested in joining Perlmongers groups in the North of England please feel free to sign up.

At the moment, I'm concentrating on getting a group set up in Yorkshire. Anyone in the Yorkshire area who's interested in joining a Perlmongers group and hasn't already been in touch, I can be contacted privately via /msg on PerlMonks or publicly via the mailing list.
