FSF Awards

ziggy on 2002-09-06T18:00:21

The FSF is now accepting nominations for the 2002 Award for the advancement of Free Software.

Larry Wall won the inaugural award in 1998. Guido van Rossum won the 2001 award. (Given this pattern, hold your nominations for Matz for another two years. ;-)

Recipients of the FSF Award receive a year's supply of emacs - the floor wax that's also a dessert topping!
