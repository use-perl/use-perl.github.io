Hiring The Best Developers

ziggy on 2005-02-02T04:58:42

Joel wrote a nice essay on companies that proclaim they hire from the top 0.5% of all software developers, and why that claim is a statistical fallacy.

Shorter

pudge on 2005-02-03T18:12:17

It could have been a lot shorter, and just as effective, by merely listing two truisms:

  1. There is no set of the "best" programmers. There are only a set of programmers which are best for a particular job opening at a particular company. What might be best for you might not be best for someone else.
  2. Most programmers, including at least part of whatever is the top 0.5% for you or anyone else, will not be available to take a particular job opening at a particular company.
