Local News

ziggy on 2002-10-07T17:42:45

Washington, DC is on the world scene, again. This time it is because of a rash of inexplicable shootings in the area. These aren't your run of the mill mafia/drug war/mugging/turf battle style homicides though. They're apparently totally random, and without warning.

Unfortunately, the net effect is that the lead story every few hours for the last 3.5 days has been either «no new shootings, no significant new developments», or «there's been another shooting, but we don't know if it's related».

It's getting quite frustrating. Especially when the Chief of Police has long since run out of interesting ways to say «We don't have any news to report; here's a summary of what we've said before. I'll answer your questions now.»
