Keysigning: JFDI

ziggy on 2002-07-13T13:49:20

ask and acme both seem to like the idea of keysigning at TPC. The more I think about it, the more it sounds like a BOF isn't the right way to go about it. After all, there are so many great bofs already scheduled (not to mention unlisted parties), I can't imagine that anyone would rather sign GPG keys than chatting about AxKit, Perl6 or Ruby.

There is a way to keysign at TPC though. Lots of Perl people are going to be at some of the bofs (like "Whose Code is it Anyway?", "The Perl Foundation BOF", "Perl Mentoring" and such). I think the most effective way to do it is to announce keysignings around those events (and more mundane things like lunch).

So, bring your PGP/GPG keys. There'll be plenty of keysigning opportunities this year. :-)
