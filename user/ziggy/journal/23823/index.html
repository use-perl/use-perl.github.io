ziggy on 2005-03-24T16:29:19

Here's an interesting bit of "news":

  • Local seafood merchant orders a "large lobster", and gets a 15lb beastie
  • Customers take pity on the animal, estimated to be 35-40 years old
  • Merchant sells the lobster to these customers who intend to "liberate" it instead of "cook" it. Merchant makes $30 on the deal.
  • Lobster sent back on its way to the cold waters off the coast of main, and gains some weight. Repeat with a 16lb lobster.
Smells like a scam to me. PETA's probably taking 10% on the deal. ;-)
