People Oriented development

ziggy on 2004-01-12T17:13:56

From Martin Fowler's Bliki:

Those who are interested in agile processes all agree that process is a second-order effect on project success. The first value of the agile manifesto is that Individuals and Interactions are more valuable than Process and Tools.
Agile processes aside, Fowler's summation is the most succinct expression of the hard realities of software development that I've seen.
