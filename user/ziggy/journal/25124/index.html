Only in Washington...

ziggy on 2005-06-09T22:22:28

A bill was introduced in the House today from a Texas Republican (and former telco employee) to unilaterially prevent any state or local government from providing telco service that is "substantially similar" to those offered by the private sector.

The act is colloquially known as PITA.

I cannot think of a more apt name for it.
