Meeting Announcement

ziggy on 2002-03-01T17:01:53

The DC Perl Mongers are proud to present Paul Ceruzzi this coming Tuesday at our monthly meeting. Paul is Curator of Aerospace Electronics and Computing at the Smithsonian's National Air and Space Museum.

His talk is entitled ""Zeno's Paradox and the History of Computing."

When: 7:30pm, Tuesday March 5th Where: American Geophysical Union 2000 Florida Avenue N.W. Washington, DC http://www.agu.org/

For security reasons, please RSVP with Nat Eiseman <neiseman@agu.org>, including name, employer/organization, email and phone number.
