In other news, a case study on why internationalization matters:

One of the songs on the Lost in Translation Soundtrack is performed by the Japanese artist[*]. Gracenote's [**] CDDB information for this CD contains the name in Japanese. iTunes displays the name, in Japanese. The iPod displays the name, also in Japanese.

That's just cool.
