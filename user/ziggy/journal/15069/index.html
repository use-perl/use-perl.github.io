Only in NYC

ziggy on 2003-10-05T16:20:48

New York's Finest had to subdue a 350 lb. bengal tiger in a Harlem apartment. The tiger is on its way to Ohio.

Locals are concerned that the big cat won't be able to adapt to life away from the city:

"My concern is that the city cat won't make it in the country," said Lynnette Braxton, 49. "He's going to have no jazz, no hip-hop. He's going to miss the Harlem Renaissance."
