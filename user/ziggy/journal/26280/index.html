Shelter Pale Ale, Raison D'Etre, 60/90-minute IPA, Indian Brown Ale, and Immort Ale have easily and handlily trumped any of the previous lagers and ales that had been my prior favorites.

Keep your eyes out for Midas Touch, 120-minute IPA, and World Wide Stout. WWS is only about 21% ABV (in 12oz bottles no less).

One bottle is sufficient to take a programmer pissed off at his PHB's inability to manage projects, and turn him into a mellow programmer. Two bottles is enough to take that same pissed off programmer and make him happy to be alive. (Best followed with a hoppy chaser, like Hop Devel, anything from Rogue or the like.)

Oh, and next time you're in Philly, go hunting for a pint of Yards ESA from a hand pump. Fergie's is a good place to start. It's very nice in the bottle, but another thing entirely off of a hand pump. Much as I love Dogfish Head, Yards ESA (fresh, and from a beer engine) is my desert island beer. ;-)
