Cosmic Coincidences

ziggy on 2004-01-25T17:23:28

Yesterday was the 20th Anniversary of introduction of the Macintosh. Yesterday, orkut invitations were flying, and it seemed that everyone was online updating their orkut networks and profiles.

Hm. In the 1984 ad, what did the Big Giant Head say? Oh yes:

Our enemies shall talk themselves to death and we will bury them with their own confusion. We shall prevail!
Hm. Is orkut the new new thing in social networking, or just a technopolitical conspiracy to waste your time?

You be the judge...
