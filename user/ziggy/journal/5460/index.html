Spam of the day

ziggy on 2002-06-06T16:05:34

I used to think if I wanted to get a joke a day, I'd have to subscribe to some sort of joke-of-the-day list. Not anymore! I get more jokes every day in my mailbox than I can possibly read! That's not even counting the jokes that I summarily delete (Viagra ads, Korean puns and Chinese finger puzzles). Take a lookee at this one: 

To: Undisclosed@panix.com, Recipients@panix.com
Cc:
Subject: YOU ARE IN SERIOUS TROUBLE!!!
X-Mailer: Microsoft Outlook Express 6.00.2462.0000

Internet surfing.

DOOR and the JAIL SENTENCE.

ever watched on the Internet.

http://203.55.86.117/welder/index.php

I think I need a Spam Eliminator, not a hyper souped up Enron-grade Evidence Eliminator. But these things are just too damn funny.
