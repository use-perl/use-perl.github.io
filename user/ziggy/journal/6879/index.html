Old habits die hard

ziggy on 2002-08-03T20:41:31

It's been about two months since I've gone cold turkey on coffee. Occasionally I do miss it, but I don't get cravings for a good cup of joe or a shot or two of espresso.

It's gotten to the point where I'm walking past coffee shops almost oblivious to their presence. But today, while on the way to the barber shop, we stopped into a Starbucks, mostly out of habit. The strange thing was that I got to the counter and couldn't figure out what I was doing there. It was a rather strange feeling, given how much time and money I've spent at Starbucks over the last decade...
