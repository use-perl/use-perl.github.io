The Boston Expos?

ziggy on 2002-10-25T15:00:33

The New York Daily News is reporting that the Red Sox may be time-sharing Fenway Park with the Expos next season:

"This could be a win-win-win for all the parties involved except the 200 season ticket-holders in Montreal," the source said. "The Red Sox get the benefit of the extra revenue, the Expos get a great place to play where people will come out to see them and Boston fans get to see National League baseball again."
Wow. Let's hope this tactic doesn't become too popular. The prospect of the Mets playing in the Bronx, or the Phillies calling Oriole Park "home" is just too much to bear...
