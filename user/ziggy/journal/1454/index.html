Quote of the week

ziggy on 2001-12-01T05:00:51

From Fred Pohl, sometime around 1992:

Global warming isn't about the weather getting hotter.
It's about the weather moving elsewhere.
That's a trip to the wayback machine, from when I was reading a whole shelf full of books about the imminent demise of the ecosystem, civilization, the economy and other happy fun topics. Back when I wasn't eating meat, or wearing leather, but was consuming heavy amounts of roasted caffeine berries.... (it was a college thing)

Anyway, today we have conclusive evidence that global warming is here, in the form of weather systems not doing what they're supposed to be doing: It's now Midnight, December 1st, and it's almost 70 degrees in Washington DC. It's been a mild November, but this balmy weather is positively crazy. Especially when this is the time of year ice skating rinks are supposed to be open (and not as a respite from the heat...)
