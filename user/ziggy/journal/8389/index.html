Journaling filesystems in OS X?

ziggy on 2002-10-15T19:42:52

MacNN reports on an article at eWeek that a journaling filesystem will be coming out for 10.2.2. It'll run on top of HFS, and sounds more like a traditional VMS filesystem than a journaling filesystem like ReiserFS, XFS, ext3 or BeFS.
