I AM NOT A NUMBER! I AM A FREE MAN!

ziggy on 2002-01-04T02:03:55

We made it to the end of the Prisoner last night. The last episode -- Fall Out -- is pretty over the top. It's so weird that it hasn't begun to sink in yet. The last few episodes were sorta strange, with all of the alternate realities and hazy drug induced plot lines.

The entire series is quite well written though. Each line has it's place, and on the second, third and umpteenth viewing, I'm still finding new clues, intertwined stories, and hints.

Maybe it'll make sense when it's time for next year's Prisoner marathon. :-)
