YAPC::Europe in Paris (ir)?regular newsletter, issue 7

BooK on 2003-07-02T06:56:41

Bonjour,

The timetable for the conference is now online. See the pretty colors at http://yapc.mongueurs.net/yapc/timetable/. You can click on the talker or the talk, and have a little more information. We borrowed the look and feel from Munich's timetable.

Here is also a short note to remind you that if your monger group produces some kind of merchandising (T-shirts, stickers, etc.), you can bring them and exchange/sell them with/to the other mongers that will be in Paris.

Speaking of T-shirts, you'd better check that you've entered the correct size in your profile, since this is the size of the T you'll be given. And they go into production RSN, so hurry up.

If you bring such items, we would very much appreciate your donating a few of them for the final auction which will be hosted for the fourth year in a row by London.pm's one and only Greg McCarroll. Prepare your cash. He'll make you pay.

Speaking of donations, even though speakers have their registration fees waived, they can still purchase their ticket if they want to help us. Or spend those 99 EUR during the final auction. I heard they'll be selling... No, you'll see for yourself. ;-)
