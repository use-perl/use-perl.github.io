10 seconds of fame for the Coffeyville Country Club

bart on 2005-02-09T10:45:35

If you go to Google Maps (beta) in simply zoom in, you'll always end up at the same spot, Google Maps' idea of the center of the USA. There is one recognizable orientation point if you set the zoom to the 4th knot (about 3/4 up — if you zoom in too much, you'll just see nothing): the Coffeyville Country Club. That is sure to put them on the map.
