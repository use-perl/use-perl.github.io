bart on 2006-02-18T14:57:22

Looking back to the music from 2005, I found that Effigy and Kalimayantra from Hands Upon Black Earth were two of my most often played tracks. But then, I've always been a sucker for Big Beat.

So I finally broke down and ordered the album from CDBaby. Sorry, Magnatune, I do love you, but I prefer a real CD when (a) it is pressed and not a CD-R, and (b) it is not DRM encumbered. Even when I play the tracks as mp3's most often.
