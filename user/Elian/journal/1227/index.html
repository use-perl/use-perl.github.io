Get an iBook instead

hfb on 2001-11-10T17:32:38

I had a Ti but traded it for an iBook since the screen, though smaller, is sharper on the iBook, the iBook is smaller and more durable and the Ti scratches easily, gathers static and fries your legs when on your lap.

I adore the iBook and for only $1200 you get a small, light, rocking little computer that, in my own opinion, far outclasses the Ti. Maybe the next gen of Ti will be better but give it a pass for now.

And yes, those stores are evil. They have one up at the North Shore Mall near us and it's hard to avoid going there even though I loathe malls.
