1,2 oder 3

domm on 2005-09-20T15:14:00

Josh, my older son (9 years), is participating in 1, 2 oder 3 today. "1, 2 oder 3" is a quiz show for kids produced by German, Austrian and Switz national tv broadcasters. It's been around for ages (I was watching it when I was a kid...)

Together with two classmates and one teacher he went to Munich yesterday (by train). Today they are recording the show. Tomorrow he'll be back, with some prices and maybe the 'main price', a custom made Lego goblet.

He was very nervous this weekend, and now I'm starting to get nervous too. The show isn't broadcasted live, so we'll have to wait until it's over and somebody calls us (I think one of his classmates took a mobile phone with her)
