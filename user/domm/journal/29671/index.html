I just got a bunch of books (plus two T-Shirts) from O'Reilly Germany as sponsoring for the Austrian Perl Workshop (thanks a lot!). Among the books are two copies of Perl Hacks.

I wanted to take a look at this book since I first heard about it, so I started to browser through the TOC. Hack #37 ("Drink to the CPAN") cought my attention, as I have some cider-blured memories of playing the CPAN drinking game during YAPC::Europe::2004 in Belfast. I was quite surprised as I read it! (Finding out why I was surprised is left as an exercise to the reader (of the book, which seems to be very interesting and fun - I'll definitly have to get a copy for myself)).
