Hack-a-thons at YAPC::Europe

domm on 2007-05-27T20:50:08

We recieved several proposals in response to our Call for Hackathons - in fact we recieved too many, as we only have funding for 4 hackathons (500 Euros each - thanks again to ZSI for sponsoring this event.)

So, after a week of voting on the proposals, here are the "winners" (in no particluar order):

  • Perl6-in-Perl6 and Perl6-in-Perl5, moderated by Flavio Glock
  • OpenGuides, moderated by Kake Pugh
  • DBI, DBD::Oracle, moderated by John Scoles
  • POE, moderated by Chris Williams (BinGOs)
We're looking forward to some very interesting hacking sessions!

Oh, and the deadline for the Call for Papers ends in a bit more than one hour, so hurry up if you still haven't sent in your talks proposals yet...
