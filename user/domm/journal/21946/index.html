I'm an uncle!

domm on 2004-11-22T09:27:51

On Saturday evening (19:04, to be exact) I became an uncle!

It came a bit as a surprise, as the birth was scheduled three weeks later, but babies have an interesting tendency of not caring to much about schedules.

In fact, it was quite funny: We (my brother, my kids and I) were at a gaming convention, when his mobile rang and his girlfriend said something like "I think it might start now...". My brother went slightly pale, grabbed his stuff and hurried to the subway...

Everything went OK, he got to his girlfriend and they arrived at the hospital in due time.
