Template::Plugin::KwikiFormat

domm on 2004-10-22T11:04:10

After long and not too successful attemps to come up with a way to let my non-techie co-workers mark up content in the various CMSs we are running, I think I finally found a nice solution: KwikiFormattingRules!

It's easy to understand (I hope), handles tables and is allready used by quite a few people.

The only downside is that we don't run Wikis (government employies and research scientists seem to prefere more strict schemes...)

So, after getting some helpful tips from Jooon on #kwiki, I packed up Template::Plugin::KwikiFormat, which lets you use KwikiFormatting in data rendered by Template::Toolkit. It should be on CPAN soon.
