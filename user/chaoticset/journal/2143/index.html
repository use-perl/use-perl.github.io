More the surrounding flesh, but still...

I purchased Visine, and what a friggin' mistake. I realize that the entirety of humanity save me finds Visine a comforting liquid in their eyes. True, it cleans all that red up. Then, it crusts saline all around my eyes.

I've never had a good relationship with saline. My favorite, had-for-many-years doctor convinced me to try saline spray for my nose, to clear my sinuses. Instead of snot I dealt with crust.

I hate this friggin' eye.

It doesn't matter, though. I have to get my ID wrote up, I have to call the VA person, I have to get to class and get books and...I got a bunch of things to do.

And no, perl, I didn't forget you. I just don't have time right now.
