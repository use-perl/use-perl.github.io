My wife is feeling much more pleasant now. Thankfully I can still count on one hand the number of drugs they've pumped into Chastity. This is the sort of boring part, where Chastity feels great and waits for the urge to push. It doesn't take long, but it feels like forever.

So, I went to the bathroom in our room. Being that each delivery room has its own bathroom, I guess I felt I didn't need to lock the door. Boy was I wrong. A nurse walked in on me and promptly walked out. I was caught off guard. All I could think was, "I swear I didn't pull the 'help' string on the wall." Won't make that mistake again.
