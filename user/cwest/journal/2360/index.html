P.O.D

cwest on 2002-01-23T21:04:45

I think this band is really cool. Yes, their music is good and their message is awesome. That's not even the best part.

The best part is how they are just as prevalent in any teenagers CD collection as secular bands with the same sound. They've done an amazing job of keeping their message real and to the point, all about Him. :-)

They were even given the honor of playing on MTV as the ball dropped at New Years. Not many bands get that honor and I'm glad it was P.O.D this year.

I respect that group. Perhaps I can catch up with them in southtown, san diego when I go for TPC. That would rock.
