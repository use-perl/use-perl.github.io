Write Anything but Code

cwest on 2003-02-28T21:30:54

Today I documented a large portion of how to write poest plugins. This took quite a while, but I'm glad I did it, things are becoming more clear. A server of this nature can get out of control, with regard to understanding, very quickly if you don't write things down fast. The helpful folks in the POE channel have been giving me advice and reading my ramblings and that has been invaluable.

And last night, a fellow author and I entered into an agreement about writing our books. We've both fallen behind and are in need of serious catching up. Having another level of accountability has flamed my desire to write something good. So I was off, and now I am off again.
