Vote 'em if You got 'em!

cwest on 2004-11-02T16:16:02

I voted today. It was a blast. I got to use a lever machine. Good old lever machine. I have a friend who was disenfranchised this morning. She was told she canceled her voter registration card in 1995, even though she had an updated registration card right in her hand. She was given a provisional ballot and, well, she's mad. I don't blame her.

I made a shirt for voting day, in hopes of getting folks interested in voting who haven't yet. I colored it this morning but I figure I'll share...
