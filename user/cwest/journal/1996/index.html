Template Toolkit cookbook

cwest on 2002-01-08T22:38:54

Well, back at TPC 5 I said I'd be willing to assume the roll of "Documentation Manager" for Template Toolkit. It just so happened that the cookbook thing, the first thing I was supposed to do, came up on the mailing list recently.

So I took this opportunity to not take the easy way out. I put the challenge out there. I'm going to treat this like a real book project so the first thing I'm collecting from the community is Chapter proposals.

At least give me the name of the Chapter and, if you're so courageous, a small description of what it covers.

Send them to the TT list or to me directly at casey@geeknest.com.

I figure that by TPC 6 we could have a good first draft cookbook.

Details here: http://www.template-toolkit.org/pipermail/templates/2002-January/002379.html
