cwest on 2003-07-16T02:03:55

FreeBSD ports are not as DWIM or "just work" as I had hoped. It's end of day two and I still don't have gnome compiled. And it's not gnome's fault! Woes, oh the woes. In the mean time I'm evaluating vim and installing cool software on other computers, software that does not come from ports.
