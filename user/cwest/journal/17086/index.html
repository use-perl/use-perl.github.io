Fuzzy Nitrous

cwest on 2004-01-29T14:54:20

I just spent my early morning at the Dentist. I have a fear of Dentistry. I had a cleaning today. They put me on Nitrous and I'm glad they did. I spent the better part of my youth playing wind instruments. That came in handy when I felt really uncomfortable. I may have been breathing too deeply because my hands and face are still fuzzy and awkward.

The bonus here is that I didn't kill the Dentist. I'm proud of myself.
