PerlBugAthon Success

cwest on 2003-07-07T04:14:25

I made those pancakes, and they were surprisingly good. After that I spent quite a while downloading and installing apache with mod_perl. After that, there was much talking about Perl and the theory of Perl Conferences.

I was able to get a ride to the hotel for the PerlBugAthon. This was a very attended event with Robert Spier, Aurtor Bergman, Leon Brocard, coraline, a random subversion developer that had nothing better to do, Piers Cawley, coraline, Ziggy, hydo, Bill Odom, and myself. I'm sure there were others, comment to add yourself. By 6:00 pm (PDT) we squashed one hundred bugs! A wild success, in my opinion.

Dinner was 20 of us at a great Chinese place. I finally caught up with Andy (who's plane was horribly delayed), and now I've got a place to stay.

My wife is now having a party back at home without me. It's for the best, as I've got lots of people to hang with here.
