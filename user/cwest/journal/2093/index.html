So much for a day of nothing.

cwest on 2002-01-13T19:49:13

I forgot to sign up for any excursions for Half Moon Cay. I decided I'd rent some snorkel gear and just swim along the shore to get some exercise.

Little did I know, there are fish right by the shore. And, the water is more clear than the tap water at my house. It was beautiful. I saw schools of fish, and swam right into them. I saw wierd looking fish, some big, some small.

God's creations amazed me today. They should everyday, but today is special.
