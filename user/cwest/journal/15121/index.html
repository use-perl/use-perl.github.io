Addicted

cwest on 2003-10-08T18:47:42

I know I'm not the only one who is addicted to Krispy Kreme donuts. In particular, I can't stop eating Chocolate Iced. Here is the dilemma: you can always find hot Original Glazed donuts, but not once have I experienced the hot Chocolate Iced! For that matter, I've never even seen them produce anything but Original Glazed at my local stores.



Therefore, my terrible conclusion is drawn. They must not bake the variety donuts on site. This morning, having realized this, I purchased Original Glazed instead of Chocolate Iced. They were incredible. They win, when hot, in comparison to the Chocolate Iced. It really is a sad truth. Maybe this knowledge will help me escape from my morning trip to Krispy Kreme. Nah.
