Gyration

cwest on 2003-10-05T02:15:00

I went to Best Buy expecting to walk out with a very basic USB keyboard. That didn't happen. I got the Gyration Ultra GT Compact Keyboard Suite (http://gyration.com/ultragt-compact.htm). I was drawn to this for three reasons. It connects via USB, two devices in one connection. They keyboard is almost exactly the size of my 12" AlBook keyboard. Finally, the wireless optical mouse also works as a presentation pointing device. I can move the pointer on the screen by waving the mouse in my hand. With a 25 foot range, I've got the most impressive portable mouse and keyboard. Once the hard keyboard cover is in stock, it must be acquired.
