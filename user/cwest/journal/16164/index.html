Flat Tire

cwest on 2003-12-04T14:34:36

Monday night, on the way home from work, I got a flat tire. Through many fumbles, foibles, and mishaps my spare was put on the car by the local service station. If you've driven in Pennsylvania, you know that PenDOT has a history of keeping the roads broken for long periods of time. Most folks forget that we've got more miles of road than any other state, not to mention the terrain. In any case, my car must've hit one of those pot holes very hard, hard enough to unrepairably bend my wheel. Rock on, sans the Roll.

My car is not drivable, and I'm waiting for my wheel to be delivered from the Hyundai USA warehouse in California. Urgh.
