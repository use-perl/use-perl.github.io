Vacation Isn't

cwest on 2004-06-25T01:13:01

Just got back from vacation this week. Kids and road trips don't mix. I can't bear to elaborate, I'm much too weak. I tried to bring work with me to vacation. While there I got a call from David Brickner who enlightened me: You don't get enough of one done and you don't enjoy enough of the other. Words to live by.

Personally, I'm glad to be home. The single enjoyable experience was dinner with my wife at Le Crepe en Haut. If you're in Central Florida you must go there! That dinner marked four years of service to one another. Hard to believe it's only been four years (cheap shot).

In any case, back to work!
