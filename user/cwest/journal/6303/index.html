Your poor baby! Kids who can't be entertained and have to stay in their chair make for MISERABLE road trips. It's hell for us to go to Denver. We certainly don't go to California any more.

Seriously--save your sanity and fly. It can't possibly be worse than driving.

dc2000 on 2002-07-15T23:05:45

From Pittsburgh to Oklahoma, 20 hours. 12:00am - 8:00pm
From Oklahoma to Arizona, 16 hours. 4:00am - 8:00pm
From Arizona to California, 8 hours. 8:00am - 4:00pm
Logical, as the best scenery is west of OK, you might as well
drive in the dark on the eastern segments.

Plan ahead for or around construction delays if possible,
and check out speedtraps.
Urban road construction delays are at night
(in CA, at least).
