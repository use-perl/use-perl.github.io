Birthday

cwest on 2004-03-19T17:15:17

As a gift to you on my birthday, I'll golf the birthday song. The shortest I've seen before goes like this.

for(('to you')x2,"dear $ARGV[0]",'to you'){print"Happy Birthday $_"}

Which could be reduced to the following.

print"Happy Birthday $_"for("to you")x2,"dear ".pop,"to you"

So here's my entry.

print"Happy Birthday ",$_%3?"to you":"dear ".pop for 1..4
