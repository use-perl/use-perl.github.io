Car Karaoke

cwest on 2003-09-05T18:36:08

I've been driving into work for almost two months now, after two years of working from home. The drive has been mostly miserable but it has resurrected one of my favorite things to do (when alone): singing my favorite songs at the top of my lungs in my car. If you haven't done this recently, you really should. Grab a CD and go for a drive, you will feel great when you get out of the car. Since I don't have an iPod I just connect my laptop to the cassette player via a (can you guess?) special connector. Then I can war-drive at the same time! LA LA LA
