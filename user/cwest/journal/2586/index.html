Wind

Matts on 2002-02-01T22:45:44

Wind here was cool last night. I live on the "other" side of a hill, so the wind blows over the hill and seems to pick up speed as it reaches us, making it a fantastic blow down our street. Luckily we don't live in a timber frame house though, which really seems to make a big difference to shakiness in wind. Our last house was timber frame, which seems to be how all "modern" houses are built, but it seems to make the wind movement greater - maybe they can stand more stress than a 100% brick built house.

Of course I also live on an island, so wind in the US tends to be relative to what we see every day (modulo hurricanes)
