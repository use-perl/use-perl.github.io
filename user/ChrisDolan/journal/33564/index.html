[non-perl] Cold Cold Heart

ChrisDolan on 2007-06-21T03:54:43

My brother-in-law Joe Hannigan is a member of a fun "country-boogie" band called Cold Cold Heart. They just released their second album called Blue Collar Attitude. The headline song has catchy lyrics:

"I got a white collar job, but a blue collar attitude"

You can listen one-minute previews of any of the songs for free (click the little play buttons) at: http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/coldcoldheart or buy a CD! I know this is a fairly blatant shill, but I really like his music.

Joe has been a part-time-professional musician for many years in the Boston area and has opened for Charlie Daniels and Willie Nelson.
