If you're planning to get to Birmingham by Monday evening, before the conference, then you might fancy joining some of us for a meetup on Monday night.

The plan is:

  • meet in foyer of Jury's Inn about 7pm
  • head to O'Neills on Broad Street for just after 7pm (turn left out of Jury's Inn and the pub is on the next corner).
  • head to Bombay Mix for about 8.30pm (continue down Broad Street, away from Jury's Inn and the restaurant is about 100 yards along on your left).
