Cropredy

barbie on 2002-08-13T09:00:16

Currently recovering from this years Cropredy Festival. Thankfully we caught most of the bad weather at night, though it did arrive early on Friday night, so we opted to miss Richard Thompson and Fairport "The Early Years" and head back to the tent.

The Dubliners, E2K, Richard Digance and Eddie Reader were the highlights of the performances over the 3 days, at least for me. Not a big fan of Fairport Convention, but they did put on a good show.

It was Dan's first camping trip too, and for the most part he loved it. We're looking forward to going again next year. I just hope the rain stays away this time.
