Fame &amp; Fortune

barbie on 2003-12-01T12:37:36

I wonder how many of the London crowd have manged to watch London's Home Movies over the last 3 weeks?

Last Sunday featured a few clips my Dad took of my Mum's family. Within seconds of the show finishing, my uncle's phone was ringing off the hook, with various members of the family and friends ringing to see if he saw himself and them :) Seeing as my Dad and the rest of us all live far beyond the LWT catchment area, my uncle has been videoing them all so we can watch them when he comes up at Christmas. Aparently my Dad features in every single one, as there is a clip in the title sequence featuring him. My Aunt has told us she'll be bring the autograph books with her ;)

The next shows are:

  • Sunday 7th December, 5:35pm to 6:05pm
  • Sunday 14th December, 5:40pm to 6:10pm

I expect they'll be one on 21st December and then the rest will probably be shown in January.
