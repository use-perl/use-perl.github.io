CPAN Stats Update

barbie on 2006-06-07T09:10:48

Stats for May 2006 are now online.

Despite the top testers page claiming to have over 1,000 testers, that isn't really true. There are still a significant number of testers who have used more than one address that haven't been mapped. As such, yesterday I sent out a mail survey to 93 email addresses that have been sending reports over the last 3 months, in the hope that I can map these addresses more accurately. The response so far has been really positive and I have quite a few addresses to update now. Hopefully those addresses that haven't respond yet will get the chance to over the next month.

If you have sent a report and you haven't receive the email, you might like to respond to the survey, if any address you use is listed on the top testers page.

  1. What name can I use to identify you? First and last name or known nickname preferred.
  2. If a CPAN Author, what is your PAUSE ID?
  3. What other email addresses have you used in the past to submit CPAN test reports? See the top testers page for a complete list of the names and unmapped addresses that have submitted reports.

Send your responses to barbie@missbarbell.co.uk, with the subject of 'CPAN Tester Survey'

Thanks to everyone who has responded to the survey request so far, it really is much appreciated.
