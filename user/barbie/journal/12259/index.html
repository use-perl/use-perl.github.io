I'm Not ... Growing Old

barbie on 2003-05-16T21:27:40

20 years ago this week, The Smiths released their first single, Hand In Glove. Being a Joy Division/New Order fan at the time, they were usurpers to the Manc crown. But it suddenly dawned on me ... 20 ... thats TWENTY ... years ago this week.

I mentioned to a work colleague about first getting into The Cure in 1978, he followed up by telling he wasn't even born then!

I saw my first gig at Hanley Victoria Halls on the 11th May 1981, featuring The Mood Elevators, The Au-Pairs and headliners The Beat. A fantastic night. Twist And Crawl, who are half of the original Beat lineup, are doing gigs over the the next few months. Hopefully I'll get to a couple of them. Memories :)
