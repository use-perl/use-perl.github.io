Last Day for YAPC::Europe submissions

barbie on 2006-06-02T14:28:50

If you're planning to speak at YAPC::Europe::2006, note that you only have the rest of the afternoon and evening to submit talks. The deadline is midnight GMT tonight. So please submit your proposals now!!!!

We'll be having a get together next week to go through all the proposals and plan to let those who have submitted proposals, know the results of each of their proposals by the end of next week, if not sooner :)

We have seen a wide variety of talks submitted already, so we're looking forward to putting on a very interesting conference for you all. We'll be opening up registration soon, so keep an eye out for the launch of that.
