Tuesday, 22 July 2003 - YAPC::Europe - The Arrival

barbie on 2003-07-31T14:50:51

Got to the airport for just after noon, and had a spot of overpriced and overcooked Chicken Tikka Masalla (I like my curries) from Terminal 2's "restaurant". It fed me at least, but I've had a lot better. Plane food compared well, never tried goats cheese before. Went on to try it several times in Paris. Nice.

JJ and I landed 65 minutes later at CDG. At first the 10 Euro bus ride to Arc de Triomphe seemed expensive, but in comparison to the rest of the visit, it wasn't so bad after all. After much carrying of heavy bags, we finally made it to the hotel. JJ and I discovered Steve didn't know where he was staying and had been wandering around the locale checking out the Jardins et Palais du Luxembourg (or Luxembourg Park) among other things. We tell him to meet us at the pub in a little while.

Quickly dump bags and freshen up, then onto registration at CNAM before it closes. BooK comes and says hello, which was nice, and I also said hello to a few other Paris organisers who's names I recognised. Collect goody bag and head to the pub round the corner. Greeted by many London.pm'ers who I hadn't seen for ages ... well at least since last Thursday :) Steve catches up with us, and after a quick beer goes in search of his hotel.
