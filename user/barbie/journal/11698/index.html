Hank on Form

barbie on 2003-04-17T12:01:32

Went to see Henry Rollins last night. Awesome. After over two and a half hours he finally took a bow and exited stage left. A good mixture of food for thought, observation and experience made up the set, and entertained throughout. His story of working with the TNT tactical police in Miami, on the film Bad Boys 2, perhaps caused the biggest roar of laughter. In all the time he was on stage, he only stopped to take two gulps of water. No script. Just talk. Awesome.
