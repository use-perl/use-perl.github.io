Nerd Wear

barbie on 2005-10-21T13:46:22

On Monday, sparkes from WolvesLUG launch his new venture, Nerd Wear. Along similar lines of thinkgeek, except based in the UK (Wolverhampton roughly and Willenhall to be precise). He currently has 1502 different products and 79 unique designs and has plans to add even more. The WolvesLUG mailing list has been fuelling him with ideas, so I expect a few bizarre ones to creep into the roster before too long :) I'm just surprised he hasn't got a LUGRadio one in there yet, seeing as he was one of the original presenters.
