YAPC::Europe - Early Arrivals Dinner

barbie on 2006-08-29T08:49:25

Last night, 14 of us all headed off round the corner from Jury's Inn and had a quick pint in O'Neills, then took a short walk to the restaurant. I'd not been to the Bombay Mix before, but on recommendations, it seems to be the best within a short walking distance. We weren't disappointed. Or at least I wasn't, and that's the important bit isn't? ;)

Many were tired after long flights, so everyone called it a night after the meal, except the Dutch :) Steve showed them where a late opening pub was and the organisers headed off home for an early start.

Mostly I'm feeling anxious that everything goes well. It's a mixture of excitement and nervousness that I'll have forgotten about once I see everyone (well nearly everyone) this evening.

Hope you all have good trips here, and have a great conference.
