Busy

The problem with organising a conference, is that once you get close, it kind of takes over. Work and Family have been taking up the other parts of my life. It frustrates me as I want to get the time to fix issues and add a few enhancements myself.

Expect some regular tidbits over the next few weeks about the conference, as we get through the final organisation pieces.
