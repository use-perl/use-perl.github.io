Survey Coming Soon

barbie on 2006-11-23T15:37:36

So far 122 people have responded to the YAPC::Europe::2006 survey. If you haven't had the chance to submit your response, please try and do so. It helps future organisers to encourage sponsorship, and gives them an idea of what we got wrong and what we got right. There have been some criticisms, which we will address in the final report, and plenty of positive feedback. I'm hoping that everyone who is going to submit a response will have done so by the end of November, so I will try and collate all the results for the beginning of December. The results will be made public, so everyone will get a chance to read it.

If you attended the conference and haven't receive your survey request email, please get in touch.
