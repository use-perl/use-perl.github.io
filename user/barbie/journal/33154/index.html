LUGRadio Live 2007 Schedule

barbie on 2007-04-30T09:49:22

For those in the UK, or close enough that you might be interested, the schedule for this years LUGRadio Live event has been unveiled.

I'll be speaking at 12pm on Sunday, and will be doing the Selenium talk I've been touting round on the Birmingham Perl Mongers World Tour. Aq came to visit us last month, and got to see the talk. He was so impressed I was told I was going to do that talk! I've expanded it a bit since he saw it, so hopefully this year I'll get a good crowd. I'll also being doing a Perl Mongers BOF again this year, which proved useful last year. I got a few people along who were interested in the Perl community, so I'm hoping to encourage a few more this year.

If you're planning on coming along, the recommended hotel has very limited rooms left, so you might want to book now to avoid disappointment.

This year's roster of speakers looks very interesting, and even if I wasn't on the bill I'd still be going along :)
