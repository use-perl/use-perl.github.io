YAPC::Europe - Another Year Over

barbie on 2006-09-03T11:36:56

A short post to say thanks to all the speakers and attendees who made this year's YAPC::Europe so worthwhile doing. We've had some good feedback and we now have some good thoughts and suggestions for future organisers.

A number of people have been inspired enough to get involved with local Perl Monger groups and at least one is being created, and hopefully the newcomers will get the chance to come to further Workshops and YAPCs, which for me is always a part of the aims of a YAPC. While many of us attending YAPCs are part of the community, there are several that aren't and it's nice to be able to show them it's worthwhile staying involved.

Yesterday and today I am extremely tired, but it feels like I've been on tour again, which always gives me a great buzz. It was a lot of hardwork putting this together, with the occasional frustration at times (especially finding a venue), but the results seemed to have been well received, so we must have got most things right.

Expect some further announcements for numbers, finances and the like over the coming months, but for the moment we'll probably be taking a well earned rest.
