Finance::Quote module

eco on 2008-12-05T23:25:38

I took over the maintainers job of Finance::Quote somewhere in September 2008. First job was to solve major bugs which made the module far less usable. Two versions where released since my nomination. Those releases seem to have pushed a new live into the module: The mailing list is active and new submodules are submitted and added to the project.

I'm glad the community has been very reactive to the new releases. Seems that the 'release early, release often' principle is very appreciated.
