Three notable elements in Copenhagen

andy.sh on 2008-08-11T16:18:54

Although I was too tired after short sleep before the flight, and even that I've got cold, I made a trip through the city and I like it :-)

* Much water. I saw how long and flat passengers boat managed to make a turn in the place of the channel where it is almost 90 degrees corner and is as narrow as the length of the boat itself.

* Many types of trains. I saw a model of the train that I even could not imagine. Train cabin is surrounded with rubber to make carriages connected.

* Music of Danish group Alphabeat is heard often. Not only in the hotel lobby where I am writing this from, but also as the background sound in TV advertisement.
