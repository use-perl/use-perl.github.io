Comment YAPC::TV

andy.sh on 2008-10-25T22:24:44

Another update of YAPC::TV I've made today. It is now possible to comment any talk.

There are no registration on the website itself, but you'd use your OpenID. I hope most of those who use Perl have got one.

Notable thing is that comments posted on either low or high resolution page are mixed together, and thus you can see all comments to the talk, not depending from where they were posted.
