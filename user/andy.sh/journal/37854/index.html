1 year of Moscow.pm

andy.sh on 2008-11-11T13:50:26

I missed the date myself, but one of the readers of Moscow.pm mailing list have noticed that on 9th of November 2007 the first non-test message was sent to our mailing list.

Thus Moscow.pm is now in the beginning of the second year of its existance. Cool!

During this year we wrote a little bit more than 3.5 Mbytes of messages.

Moscow.pm, including its pre-history form, and with the help of other PM-groups have organized six Perl public events: five workshops and one two-day YAPC. And all that is done withing the last 13 months, roughly one event each two months.

Next year there will be more cool stuff, I hope :-)
