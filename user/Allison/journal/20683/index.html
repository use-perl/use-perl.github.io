2004 TPF elections

Allison on 2004-09-02T01:38:45

We just completed the 2004 elections for TPF committees. Here's the new roster.

It's mostly the same as last year. Kevin Meltzer and Jeff Bisbee have swapped their roles as Perlfoundation.org coordinator and conferences chair. Bill Odom joins us as steering committee chairman. He did an awesome job ringleading TPF activities at OSCON. (Thanks Bill!) Graham Barr is taking over the CPAN chair. He has a long history with CPAN, and some great ideas on how TPF can better help CPAN. David Adler officially takes over the awards position he's been filling since June. Paul Blair joins us to support Gavin in PR, and to act as a second brain for me. Jim Brandt and Kevin Eye join the conferences committee. They did an amazing job organizing YAPC::NA this year.
