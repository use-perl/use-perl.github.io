The Dead Sea Apocalypses

Allison on 2002-08-21T22:13:58

Last night I committed the POD versions of all the Apocalypses to cvs.perl.org. They're in the perl6 repository in /doc/design/apo (the Exegeses are soon to follow in /doc/design/exe). The original POD documents weren't maintained and many revisions have been made to the HTML. So, I've been on a little archaeological expedition. :)

Many thanks to Schuyler for excavating the raw HTML from the depths of O'Reilly's CMS. And to TorgoX for Pod::HTML2Pod, which did all the grunt work.

Now for the real task, updating them to fit the current design. This'll take us a little longer.
