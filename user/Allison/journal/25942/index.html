cell phone blues (part 2)

Allison on 2005-07-29T08:04:57

Er... not actually blues this time. I just got a sexy new RAZR V3 through t-mobile. Most important feature: bluetooth. With a little experimentation this afternoon, I can now dial my normal dial-up ISP from my powerbook with the phone acting as a bluetooth modem. Slower than GPRS, but plenty fast enough for checking email on the beach, or unfriendly airports. And, considering I use about 30 minutes a month for voice, I've got a few to spare for the odd dial-up session (970+).

Will I remember to carry the new phone any better than I did the old one? Unlikely.

My new ringtone is a song Max composed. :)
