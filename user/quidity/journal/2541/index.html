The Fear

quidity on 2002-01-30T23:46:37

I sometimes feel the Fear.

Once I felt the Fear at a Furry convention one of my friends invited me to come along to (having "human" on my name badge was sort of amusing though), although in the end I think I made the convention feel the Fear themselves through excessive Rocky Horror antics.

Today I feel the Fear because tomorrow I both have a job interview, and hear back from another organisation I interviewed with.

Today I also feel the Fear because I managed to embed a perl interpreter in parrot, or, at least enough of one to allow me to write:

new P0, ParrotPointer
new_perl P0
parse_perl P0, "honk.pl"
call_perl_na P0, "HONK"
done_perl P0
end

and, with assistance from "honk.pl" have "HONK from Perl!" dance over my screen.
