Shockingly Bright Idea

Simon on 2002-03-04T19:56:04

I can buy "Spring Water with a hint of Lemon & Lime" for 75p, or I can buy table water for 21p and stir in lemon and lime juice.

It sort of makes you wonder why people make the flavoured mineral waters. Are people really too lazy to add their own flavouring?
