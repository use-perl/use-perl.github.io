10 kyu

Simon on 2002-05-01T21:58:59

Getting there, slowly. Played a fair number of games of go today and seemed to be settling down at about 10 kyu if not a little lower. Only two and a bit months ago I was playing at 17k, so this is a huge leap forward for me.

But here's a scary though. I wonder if the back and neck problems I've been having recently are more caused by go than by typing. Certainly, I've never had any problems from typing in the past, and it's only since I've been spending many hours a day hunched over a go board that I've noticed problems. Something for me to consider, I suppose.
