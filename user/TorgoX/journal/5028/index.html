Without the monthly services, tivo is just a vcr that you don't have to change tapes for. You will have to manually set to record the time period of every show. Loads better than than a VCR but...

With the monthly service you don't set times to record, you set Shows. Get a season pass to Buffy the Vampire Slayer on UPN, and tivo records every episode, regardless of what time it's on.

With the monthly service you get the full programing guide, allowing you to see all upconing movies starring Hrrison ford or directed by Akira Kurasawa.

With the monthly service you get the Tivo suggestions. By keeping a (local) eye on what you watch, the Tivo records other programs that it thinks you might like.

I no longer watch Live TV, between my own scheduled shows, and the Tico suggestions, All that I watch is quality programming.
