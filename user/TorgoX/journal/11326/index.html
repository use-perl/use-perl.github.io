Bush Links 9/11 To Sound Of Ringing Bell (Mar 30 2003)

WASHINGTON, DC -- Today President Bush made an impressive connection between the events of September 11th to the sound of a ringing bell.

"Now I know that 9/11 has been compared to a lot of different things. But, on 9/11," said President Bush, ringing the bell, "America was changed forever. Changed in a way that only people who lived to see 9/11," ringing the bell, "will ever understand 9/11," ringing the bell, "on 9/11" ringing the bell a final time.
