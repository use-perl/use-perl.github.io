Dear Log,

Here, to edify you while consuming a mere 3MB of your bandwidth, is a track of Sundanese music; and I think Sundanese music is the best music east of India.

Sunda is on the west part of Java, in Indonesia, so I really hope the Sundanese didn't get beat down by the tsunami. On the one hand, they're like 1,200 miles away from the earthquake; on the other hand, so is Sri Lanka, and they got stomped.

Between Sunda and the epicenter, there's the whole bulk of Sumatra. Hopefully that kept them out of harm's way.

If you like the music, buy the album -- it's Mojang Priangan by Degung.
