Lisp &#8776; Zappa

TorgoX on 2006-04-11T03:51:46

Dear Log,

Yes, Perl is cartoon music.

But Lisp is Frank Zappa: Complicated. Weird. Unique. Quite well spoken. Amazingly prolific. Influential in some very good ways. More praised than appreciated.

And almost completely impossible to enjoy actually listening to / programming in. That is, most people can get like a bit of it, but only a few weirdos can like all of it.

Zappa ended up with lethal cancer and Lisp ended up with Scheme.
