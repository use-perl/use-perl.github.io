Look at your use.perl.org preferences and you'll notice a new slashbox available for Fresh Air! Readers of my book will remember that extraction from the Fresh Air site was an example I wrote up into a chapter. So I thought "Why not make an RSS feed from the output?". So I wrote a cron job that checks the Fresh Air web site during the day, and rewrites to an RSS file, which I sent Pudge the URL of.
