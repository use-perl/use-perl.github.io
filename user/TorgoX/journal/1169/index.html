Dear Log,

It was a year ago that after many years of consideration, I finally gave up on the entire American news media, and looked elsewhere. It was one year ago, when "we" as a nation were supposedly in a hubbub about who actualy pulled the sword from the stone. Yes, it's been a year. And I haven't looked back since.

I am not one to harbor dreams of disappearing into cyberspace, but I have to admit that the news coverage is much better there than here.
