Good and bad news for this month's Melbourne PM meeting.

Good news, a record (for the new venue) number of mongers (25.5, including what seemed to be a 3 year old perl hacker :)) turned out to hear Paul talk about App::Sweeperbot (funny, but uses the extremely useful Win32::GuiTest), Devel::NYTProf (awesome), PAR (remote repositories look very useful), moose and perl 5.10 regexps.
