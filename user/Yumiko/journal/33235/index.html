Puff, Puff, no smoke? Smoke, smoke, we have smoke!

Yumiko on 2007-05-09T21:47:08

Ran 'make smoke' again for pugs this morning and had the upload fail. Again. Just spits the page back at me with 'error:' from the client tagged on the front. I sat there scratching my head wondering what in the world was going on, took a look at the source code for the server and client and then realized the big glaring message that 'suggests' I install Compress::Zlib or Compress::Bzip2.



After that, of course it uploads just fine.

So yay, another virginity lost. Makes me wonder how hard I have to be hit over the hit to pay attention to suggestions.
