Make your own movies of perl

jjore on 2009-04-21T06:50:00

Not on CPAN just yet but available http://github.com/jbenjore/runops-movie/tree/master>.
This is kind of a "splat" at the world since it may or may not work at the moment. As I used it, I used a forked version of Treemap but I uploaded and didn't test the namespace change from Treemap::* to Runops::Movie::TM::*.

Roughly, to make your own movie:

  perl -MRunops::Movie=run your-program 2> movie.log
  bzip2 -9 movie.log
  bzcat movie.log.bz2 | frame-runner
  ffmpeg -i frame-%05d.png movie.mp4
