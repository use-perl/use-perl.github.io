Simply awesome

Ninjai is simply awesome! It's some of the best animation I've seen in a while, and certainly a showcase for use of Flash. The soundtracks & voices alone are worth the time to listen to. The soundtrack intro to every chapter gives me shivers every time I listen to it. Now if they would just make the soundtracks available as MP3 rather than just snippets from each episode... I have one of the desktop pictures set on my work computer now.

The middle chapters did seem a little slow, but they were more than made up for by the previous & following chapters. I rate it 5 stars and Two Thumbs Up.
