There are now some photos of Portugal on the wiki.

Looking for the stars

Have you written/co-authored a Perl book, or perhaps created a module that everybody uses? Are you a Perl star?

Are you coming to YAPC::EU::2005?

If you think that people knowing you're coming will influence their decision on attending the conference (for good or bad), please tell us so at yapc@perlhackers.org

Getting the speakers on the mailing list

If you happen to know a speaker and know he/she's not on this mailing list, please try to convince him/her to subscribe :-)

Banging his/her head on the wall for about five minutes usually does the trick...

More than five minutes is not recommended, as they still have to be able to speak...
