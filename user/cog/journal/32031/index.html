Dexter

cog on 2006-12-31T18:07:59

I just wanted to say I really enjoyed Dexter.

Dexter Morgan, brilliantly played by Michael C. Hall (nominated for the Best Performance by an Actor In A Television Series - Drama Golden Globe), is a serial killer that kills... well, mostly serial killers!

And the overall plot of the first season is awesome, with everything falling into place episode after episode.
