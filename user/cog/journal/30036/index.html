cog on 2006-06-25T12:32:27

The flight was rather smooth (perhaps the fact that I didn't sleep the night before helped).

Getting inside the USA was also smooth.

I believe most people I know who came here had trouble with customs.

Not me :-)

- And what will you be doing here?

- I'm going to a conference.

- Oh, OK. Are you presenting any materials?

- Yes, I am.

- OK, thanks.

And that was it :-)

Another thing that seems to help is to really look lost and ask a lot of questions :-) Suddenly, everybody's focused in helping you :-)

After the flight, I finally managed to get to the train station on terminal 3. Barbie went to the train station on terminal 2 and ended up in the same wagon I was in. Talk about coincidences, uh? :-)

A few random thoughts:

- Seven-Eleven tacos are nice.

- "Fruit-punch" Gatorade is not :-)

Oh, and I've already finished the slides for one of my three talks :-)
