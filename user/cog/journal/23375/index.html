Why can't I buy tickets for U2?????

cog on 2005-02-25T10:58:23

48,984 tickets for the last concert in the tour.

You can buy tickets at FNAC... if you have their client card...

You can buy tickets at BP gas stations... if you have their client card and at least 1,300 points in it...

You can buy tickets at the stadium... if you are a member of the club and have a life seat...

You can buy tickets at other places... if you their client...

FFS, with all these places selling tickets (and each of them has a number of tickets assigned to it), will there be any tickets left for the common mortal???

I'm not making myself a client of any of those places just to buy tickets for U2. Actually, I will probably stop buying things in any of them if I don't get a ticket because of this [1]!!!

It's just not fair! I know I'm not an official member of the fan club, but I do own all of their cds! I really enjoy their music! Is it fair that I can't buy a ticket now just because I don't like being commercially attached to a brand/company? I am *not* giving my address and personal details just to buy a ticket! I'm buying my ticket someplace else and I will boycot all those places if I don't manage to do it!!

There!!

[1] - Not that I buy anything at the stadium, but they have a mall.
