YAPC::EU::2005 report #10

cog on 2005-06-03T13:55:14

Greetings :-)

The organizers of YAPC::EU::2005 would like you all to know how things are going.

This is report #10 and here are some details:

We have a schedule

If you missed the special report regarding the schedule, know that we now have one (a schedule, not a special report).

MongersStayingAt...

If you know where you're staying, go to the wiki and update the page regarding people staying at wherever you're staying.

It's not just about other people knowing where you'll be, but also about us knowing how many people will be staying at each place (trust us, we need to know this).

If you still don't know where you'll be staying, you should start thinking about it :-)

T-shirt sizes

We're ready to order the tshirts, but here's what comes out of Act at the moment:

$VAR1 = { 'S' => 3, '' => 38, 'M' => 20, 'L' => 18 'XL' => 15, 'XXL' => 11, };

This means 38 people haven't made any choice regarding tshirt sizes.

Those 38 of you, Could you please fill that information? Thank you :-)

Bringing company to Braga?

If you're bringing someone with you (wives, kids, etc) to Braga but not to the YAPC, please tell us through Act, under "update your information", field "Coming with". We need to know how many non-atendees we'll have around, so we can sort some things out.

Behind the scenes action

  • A backup list of talks is being organized (if a speaker ends up not coming, somebody else will talk in his/her place; all these replacements should be noted in each day's announcements)
  • The Summer has apparently arrived to Portugal
  • The conference is still about three months away, but...
  • There's already over 100 people registered for the conference
  • There's already over 200 people subscribing this list

And that's it for now.

As always, the organizers can be reached at yapc@perl-hackers.net
