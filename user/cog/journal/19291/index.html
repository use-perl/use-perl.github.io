Partition Magic to the rescue

cog on 2004-06-16T09:46:00

When I first installed my laptop, I already knew I was going to use Windows for games and Linux for everything else... so I divided the harddisk in two... 30G each...

What I did not know was that I wasn't going to play that much... and that I was going to need that unused space on the Windows partition...

Having just 3G free, I had to use Partition Magic a couple of times to do what I wanted... it was hard and boring, but I finally made it... my Windows partition had now less then 10G... while my Linux partition has around 50G :-)
