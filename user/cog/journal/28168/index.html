So long, car!

cog on 2005-12-30T10:45:31

According to my Mother, I no longer have a car.

Courtesy of my brother, some oil on the road, and a poor tree that didn't manage to run away from my brother, I guess...

It's OK. My brother got out of it unharmed and, quite frankly, I think the experience serves him well... he's a reckless driver who likes speed and the engine rumbling!

Well, the engine ain't rumbling no more and the car won't be moving any faster than a tow anytime soon :-)

In fact, I think the car only has one more trip to get to its final destination...

Oh well...

No more car...

From now on, I'm walking...

To the place where I can catch a bus or a train, that is :-)
