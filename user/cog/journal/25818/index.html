cog on 2005-07-20T19:16:44

On mailing lists, IM systems, foruns, blogs, journals, muds, everywhere!!

People insist on sharing a URL with everybody else just because they liked it.

How about saying what it is all about too, for a change?

Come to think of it... they probably don't have the time...

Because they spend it all sending emails with URL's!!!!!
