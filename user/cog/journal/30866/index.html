Damn airport security

cog on 2006-09-03T20:33:39

I really don't understand.

I went from Lisbon to Birmingham with my backpack as my hand luggage, and everything was OK with that.

On the way back, I was forced to dispatch my backpack because it wouldn't fit the allowed size for hand luggage.

It was the same backpack.

Sure, I had two more t-shirts there, but I also had more clothes on me, and thus the backpack was even lighter.

And what do I see on the way back?

Other passengers with hand luggage way bigger than my backpack.

Great!

The result of it all?

Once I got to Lisbon, instead of simply walking out the airport, I had to stare at luggage for over 50 minutes until my backpack came out.
