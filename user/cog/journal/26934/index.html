Not Water

phillup on 2005-09-29T17:06:10

You aren't paying for water as much as you are paying for the delivery of the water and the removal of the waste that happens when you use water.

In the end, it is a small price for the time that you gain by not having to retrieve and dispose of it yourself. (IMHO. I did a lot of camping this year with a pop-up camper... I was amazed at the amount of time and effort required to carry a 5 gallon bucket of grey water to the proper disposal points.)

Not to mention how much healthier the situation is...
