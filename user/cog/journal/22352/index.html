Try indenting your here-docs

jj on 2004-12-17T17:12:52

A workaround might be to indent your here-doc... e.g
use Filter::Indent::HereDoc;

print <<POD;
  =head1 XXX

  No Pod here!

  POD

=head1 YYY

etc.

=cut
