Books and their prices

cog on 2004-12-14T16:59:48

So I usually buy my sci-fi books from a Portuguese publisher. They are small and cheap; jut the way I like them. Or rather... were!

It seems they now decided that people prefer bigger and better quality books... and more expensive too, of course.

Books I was buying at around 5 euros each now cost around 15. Now... why should I buy this kind of books when I can buy, for instance, a Masterworks by something like 10 euros, at a much better quality than those 15 euros'? Plus, this way I get the books in English!

Masterworks, here I come :-) Hope you got a big stock ;-)
