Gone sailing

cog on 2005-09-25T17:25:21

As part of the company's yearly celebrations (company's aniversary), we went sailing.

No pictures from the boats yet because we had a photographer on another boat taking all the pictures, so that we wouldn't have to take care of the boats and the cameras at the same time. I assume I'll get the pictures sometime during the week.

We got together at 8:00 and had a wonderful day that lasted (for some of us) to Saturday's 05:30.

Our team won the race. Yep, first place.

As usual in this kind of events, the crowd went wild. It always happens, you should've seen the paparazzi.

One of the best moments was when we managed to convince the guy who arrived late to the dinner party that everybody was drunk and was wondering around as a group. He was quite surprised (OK, we scared the hell out of him), when everybody jumped from below the tables.

Another great moment was the Vulcan mind meld.

As everybody knows, humans attempting that move can only result in violent behavior.
