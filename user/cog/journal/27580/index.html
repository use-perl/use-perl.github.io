Working from home

cog on 2005-11-15T18:05:02

Now that I finally have internet at home, I can make use of one of the company policies: log@home!

Pick a Tuesday or a Thursday (only if you want to, of course), and you get that day to work from home (but nothing stops you to go to the office and work from there if you want to, of course).

There's a few rules to this (you have to be within 1 hour radius from $work, you have to show up if you have a meeting, etc), but nothing really relevant.

Working from home:

  • No stress with the traffic
  • You save time going and coming home
  • Doesn't matter if it's raining
  • You save time at lunch because you can cook yourself instead of deciding where to go
  • You're more relaxed (no phones, no nothing)
  • All your stuff is around you (food, books, whatever)


Yay! :-D
