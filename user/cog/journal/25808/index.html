YAPC::EU::2005 report #14

cog on 2005-07-20T13:30:33

Greetings :-)

The organizers of YAPC::EU::2005 would like you all to know how things are going.

This is report #14 and here are some details:

More subscribers on the mailing list

Yesterday we sent an email to people on Act that weren't on this mailing list, asking them to subscribe it.

We now have over 250 people here, and growing.

More hotels with special prices

"Hotel Parque", "Hotel Elevador" and "Hotel do Templo" contacted us with special rates for the conference.

These are four star hotels.

There are still three recommended hotels for the conference (one of which is completely booked already), but we now have 7 hotels with special rates, which is great.

These new three are located on top of the mountain, which is a very good place to hang around.

Check BragaHotels for more information.

Conference dinner

Looks like we're going to have a conference dinner afterall. The price is included in the registration. For non-attendees going to this dinner we will probably have a price, but just not right now.

The dinner is going to be held on Thursday, at the University canteen.

Meanwhile, if you're a vegetarian, contact us.

If you're bringing someone with you, contact us.

The conference dinner also brings me to the next point, which is...

REGISTER YOURSELF

And convince your friends to register themselves too, if they're coming.

If you know you're coming, please do take the time to register yourself at http://conferences.yapceurope.org/2005/register.

It takes about 50 seconds, on average.

For the conference dinner, for instance, we have to say now how many people we're expecting. We can make a few arrangements to that number in the beginning of August, but nothing fancy. I assume we can also show up with a few more people than expected, but if lots of people we were not counting with show up... You get the point :-)
