The Kwalitee man

cog on 2005-03-25T16:34:15

So domm processed the Kwalitee ratings again and came up with some highscores.

The fact that I rank 7th out of 341 people pleases me very much, thank you O:-)

But enough about me :-D Let's talk about you ;-)

Do you have modules on the CPAN? If so, how did you perform relative to Kwalitee? If you're thinking "oh, that doesn't bother me"... well it should!

It should because the metrics have been well thought so far, and having higher Kwalitee means your modules satisfy a bunch of standards that are really good to the CPAN.

Anyway, there are some very interesting graphs here. The second one, in particular, comes to confirm my suspicions: that a bunch of people apply for CPAN IDs and then don't upload any code!

Hey, I'm not complaining about those people, I'm not! But there is something wrong, ain't there? Do they find it too hard to create a module? (hint, hint) Do they lose interest for several reasons? Do they suddenly evaporate after clicking the submit button?

:-|

Perhaps it's a bit of everything... apart from the evaporation process, I'd hope...

Anyway, just my $0.02 :-)

Yours, with Kwalitee, cog
