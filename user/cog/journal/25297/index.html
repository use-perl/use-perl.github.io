To do list - New Strategy

cog on 2005-06-21T12:53:59

I have a *huge* to do list.

It has over 150 items, and it does not include work related stuff (I have a different one for that).

Anyway, things tend to linger in that list, mainly because I lose track of what's in it.


...

OK, OK, and also because sometimes I forget to look at it O:-)

Anyway, one of my new strategies is this:

Every morning, when I turn on my laptop, I add four entries to the top of the to do list. Those entries are "tuit".

Each time I clean an entry, I also clean a "tuit", and all "tuit"s must be out of the list by midnight.

No, you can't clean two "tuits", it doesn't work that way :-)

Anyway, I think it's helping me :-)

Only 162 things left...
