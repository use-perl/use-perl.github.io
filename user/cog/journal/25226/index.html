Well, not quite. While, Canadian football is similar to American football they really are different games. IIRC, the Canadian football field is larger, can have more motion before the play starts, defensive players have to line up at least one yard away, and three downs instead of four. See http://www.football.com/rulesdiff/index.shtml for more details.
