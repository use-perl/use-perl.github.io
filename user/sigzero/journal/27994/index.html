A leak is found!

sigzero on 2005-12-15T03:06:10

No not a software leak. A leak in my bathroom. Here is the deal though. My wife notices the leak a few days ago but decides to see if it will go away. I give her a blank stare when she says this to me. So I go in and look at the ceiling in the bathroom and there is a water stain that is 5 inches long. Tomorrow we are expecting snow and ice so the chances of getting someone out is very slim but the chances that the water stain will get bigger...pretty good. Had she told me this when she found it we could have had it fixed by now.

No...leaks just don't go away by themselves. Not when it has a chance to thin out my bank account anyway.
