Party down

runrig on 2004-12-15T00:17:11

This year's company party was unusual in that there was a band that played what seemed to be alot of original songs. The only cover songs I recognized were:

  • Time Has Come Today - The Chambers Brothers
  • 1969 - The Stooges (Iggy Pop! yeah!)
  • Run Run Rudolph - Chuck Berry
I think their version of "Time..." was more influenced by The Ramones version than The Chambers Brothers (it didn't last nearly as long as the CB version, for one thing). All in all, I liked the band, but they didn't seem to be universally appreciated. Oh well, as the saying goes..."ya can't please everyone."
