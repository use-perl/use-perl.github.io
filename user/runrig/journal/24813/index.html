The last place you look

runrig on 2005-05-21T22:52:55

I hadn't seen my copy of John Burke's Fiddle Tunes for Clawhammer Banjo book for the last year or so, but today I found it in a box, with Miles Krassen's Clawhammer Banjo book right there beside it. It was hard to see since it was a well worn book, with spine shredded and pages falling out (a testament to its greatness). Now I can remember how to play Rickett's Hornpipe, Sail Away Ladies, a beautiful version of Soldier's Joy, the second part to Arkansas Traveler, and more. Oh happy days :-)
