It's like a video game

runrig on 2005-04-12T06:29:40

This weekend, I cleaned up my old pachinko machine. It had been laying around and in and out of storage for about the last 30 years...the first thing I cleaned out was the ashtray where there was a cobweb and a black widow. My daughter had a friend over, and neither of them could be torn away from the thing. The friend's comment was "It's like a video game, but your eyes don't hurt after you play it." (and I made sure I had my turn before I let them at it :)
