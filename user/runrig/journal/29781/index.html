Grizzly!

runrig on 2006-06-01T18:07:09

I don't own a camera, but I borrowed my wife's, took a few snaps, and signed up for a flickr account. If you ever wanted to see a real live California grizzly bear, well, you're out of luck, because they're extinct. But through the magic of taxidermy, you can experience the next best thing (Q: How can you tell it's a California grizzly? A: By the flag at the bottom). The fuzzy bit is where I edited out my daughter and our visiting french exchange student utilizing my non-existant photoshop skills. I may someday post the original if I ever feel comfortable about posting family photos, but until then...(or I may go back and take another picture). Oh, and it was our exchange student's last day here today :-( She is, though, almost certainly the only kid in her class to get a picture with a California Grizzly :-)
