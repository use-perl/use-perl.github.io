Breadsticks

runrig on 2006-01-03T04:19:24

We used to buy Pillsbury breadsticks but lately they've been hard to find at the grocery store, and unless they're on sale, they're kind of expensive anyway. But I thought "hey, I've got a bread machine...and breadsticks are just, well, bread!" So I started with a normal white bread recipe, but add extra butter and sugar, and throw in some cheese (parmesan, cheddar, etc.), olive oil and a tablespoon of this "garlic bread" spice (where the directions say to add something like three tablespoons to half a cup of butter, spread on bread, and toast, but I figured it'd be good for this also).
