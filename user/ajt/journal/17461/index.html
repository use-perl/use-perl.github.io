Test Your Backups!

ajt on 2004-02-17T22:40:56

Yesterday I installed MozCC to my Mozilla Firebird Browser, and managed to erase my Bookmarks.html file in the process!

Thankfully, I started to create backups on my system at home this weekend, nothing fancy just a tar.bz2 of key files on to another partition. The backup had worked perfectly, and I was able to recover my broken Bookmarks.html file.
