GNU/Linux and Perl Spotted in Brittany

ajt on 2005-08-11T12:08:37

I've recent returned from a cycling and camping holiday in Brittany. While buying provisions in a small local supermarket I was surprised to spot a French GNU/Linux magazine nestling on a shelf of Windows magazines. A quick flick through even revealed a Perl article by the Paris Moungueurs. I could have bought it, but my French is nonexistent, and weight is factor when you have to bike everything around - only essentials are allowed.
