CVs on the Eurostar

ajt on 2003-03-25T13:55:38

Last Friday I went to Paris and had a jolly nice time. To entertain myself on the train I read through a bunch of CVs for the position we have at work. I'd have to say we have had a very diverse selection of candidates, and overall good well prepared CVs. My only complaint would be that agencies don't tailor CVs properly, so you get pages of waffle, and not much meat....

For anyone who has not been on it yet, the footpath/park on the disused rail line from Bel-Air is excellent, and gives a very unusual view of the city, and at this time of year is starting to bloom rather nicely. I'm also told that some of the best computer shops in Paris are in the arches of the elevated section, but I wasn't allowed in....
