Upgrading from Windows XP to Debian is easy...

ajt on 2004-09-27T19:53:28

A colleague at work recently converted his desktop system at home from Windows XP to Debian via Knoppix. His wife, skeptical of changes, seems to have taken to OpenOffice.org and Mozilla without a flinch. He was so happy with the experience that he's done his in-law's machine too.
