Gardening and c

ajt on 2003-03-30T13:49:16

Today has been a morning of glorious warm sunshine, so I've been working in the garden. I don't really know much about gardening, I just follow instructions, work hard, and hope it comes out okay. It's the same with c/c++, I don't know what I'm doing, I just follow the instructions, let the compiler work hard, and hope it comes out okay.
