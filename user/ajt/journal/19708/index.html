Drip, drip...

ajt on 2004-07-06T12:28:19

While on holiday I was blissfully unaware of the latest raft of security problems afflicting Windows/IIS/IE/Outlook et al. Ironically, as I was walking with a group of people, we chatted about things, and where possible I suggested they migrate away from IE. Again at work today, I suggested to someone that they would be best not using IE. Not everyone does actually download Opera or Firefox, but some people do. Those that do, don't go back to IE...

With CERT, SANS and even MSN Slate suggesting it's time to switch from IE to anything else, the drips could soon be a more substantial flood...!
