  • I need to make public my simple templating system. It's designed to work for small scale sites, running Apache and CGI-BIN only. For anything other than a very simple site, Matt's AxKit is clearly a better solution.

  • I've got a lot of good books to read. When I have no TV and Internet access, I can read a good book cover to cover in a typical weekend. Alas I have 24 hour Internet access, since January this year a TV (I lived without TV for several years) plus DVD player, and most importantly I don't live alone any more.

I don't smoke, and I've never drank much in the way of alcohol, but when I go for a bike ride I still come back feeling like death warmed up....

I suppose I should do something productive today, especially as my better half is away for the week....
