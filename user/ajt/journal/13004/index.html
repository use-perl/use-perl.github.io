Just running Analog on a few years of web log files. Not very fast, it has to do a lot of DNS lookups, as at the moment it doesn't have the luxury of a local cache (it's building that as it goes along). It's quite amazing what you see in web log files.

On our low profile box, gets about 50% of it's "hits" from virus infected Windows boxen. IIS is one of the scourges of the earth and should be put out of it's misery. After the automated virus hits, we get a selection of less automated cracking traffic, some robots, and a few actual users. Probably only 5-10% of the traffic in a given day on the box is actual end-users....
