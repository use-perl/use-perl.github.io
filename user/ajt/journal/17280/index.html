Orkut

ajt on 2004-02-08T12:51:56

I seem to have been sucked into orkut.

The site is MS based, something fairly alien to Google, and has had a fair degree of critisism for not being very stable as a result. I have no idea if any of the critisism is fair, but it has kept The Register entertained for a few nano seconds.

So far I've been impressed with the rendering speed of the site, my systems at home are all very under-powered, and many sites just take to long to render, but orkut seems to come up quite quickly.

Other than the obvious of not being too graphicaly heavy, it's doesn't have clean modern xhtml, it's quite old fashioned and a bit messy. In fact some people have pointed out that the mark-up fails the most basic of accessability tests. While badly written sites, with excessive graphics, and broken Javascript are the norm, you would think an all new site, with Google's name on it would be better....
