Home made Naad bread

ajt on 2004-01-25T20:45:03

I'm not the best cook or baker in the world, but at weekends I do like to try and do something creative. Today was a curry thing and home made Naan bread. I've done Naan in the past, but it's messy and most of the dough seems to end up all over the kitchen, and not in the oven. Today I tried again, but I used our bread machine on dough setting, and it worked a treat. Okay in the final stages it was still messy to shape, but for a change most of it went into the oven and we got to eat it rather than clean it off the kitchen!

Flushed with success, I may do this more often!

I even got OpenOffice installed on Debian/KDE, and opened old MS Office documents up, but that's not as impressive as the Naan bread.
