Death by SPAM

ajt on 2002-06-19T12:14:26

I have considered writing a journal entry for some time, but have not felt that I had anything to write that was worth reading. Now I no longer care, I have to say this, even if no one reads it.

I HATE SPAM! Yesterday evening I got a polite email from my email forwarder, informing me that I had gone over my limit of emails, and that all additional email for that day would be held and forwarded later on. In my in-box was SPAM, there wasn't a single piece of actual email in there.

Now, I know that there are lots of perfectly good tools for dealing with SPAM, but it annoys me that I even have to do something about it.

What amazes me even more, is that somewhere in the world there are people gullible enough to fall for "penis/breast enlargement for only $9.99", or "re-mortgage for free", or whatever insane idea the spammers are selling....

Technically I think under new EU guidelines they are breaking the law, but I don't think this is going to help me now
