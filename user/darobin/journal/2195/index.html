Bandits

darobin on 2002-01-16T21:03:15

I went to see Bandits this evening as it just came out in France. It was rather fun and some of the scenes are really worth it. It's a pity that the director chose to kill pace at regular intervals with slower scenes.

I should really go to the cinema more often, I can't believe the number of things I've wanted to see and missed last year. I think I'm going to take one of those unlimited cards that allow you to go to a cinema as many times as you want for a monthly subscription of €16.40 (~$15). Going twice a month makes it worthwhile, and I really need to get back to my old habits of seeing pretty much everything that comes out. The cool thing with a subscription is that you're not afraid to waste money on a bad flick. At worst, you walk out and it didn't cost anything.
