Content Management with Perl

darobin on 2002-03-15T15:39:42

My talk on Perl CMSs has been accepted for TPC, YAY!!! The general idea of the talk is to have an overview of Perl CMSs, what they do and how they work, with the goal of providing people with the information they need to pick the one that best suits their needs.

I probably can't do every single Perl CMS including all the tiny ones, but apart from Slash and Bricolage my list is still in flux. If there's an option which you think is good and should be talked about, tell me about it (in the comments, or by mail whichever you prefer).

Oh, and a few beers from you people that write CMSs in Perl could help ;-)
