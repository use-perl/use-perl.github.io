And indeed there was champagne there, lots of it.

There was also some very good music as you can imagine. They had a jam session with very good musicians, and amazing singers. Unfortunately I had to leave at some point, so I wonder if Eric got to play. He plays the piano really well as those that heard him at TPC last year will no doubt attest.

This is going to be yet another very productive day if I can judge from the speed at which my brain seems to be processing information. My current schedule shows a party every other day until the 21rst... I'm afraid that if I keep that pace going, I'll be able to pretend I'm on IRC by piping Eliza there before the end of the month.

Oh well, I'm about to turn 25 so chances are my neurons are starting to rot away at an increased pace anyway, I might as well help a little ;-)
