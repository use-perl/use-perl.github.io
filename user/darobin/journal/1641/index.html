Re:Blame ASCII, Not Me!

darobin on 2001-12-15T18:08:15

Hahaha, sorry, I was probably a little too fast in writing, and I didn't intend to blame use.perl alone :-).

This is a general problem due to the brokennes of application/form-data. Types in the application/* space can't have charset parameters, which means that browsers can't tell the server the charset they're using. Because of that, browsers basically POST back in the encoding they received (well, Netscape 4's a bit broken there, unsurprisingly). Of course, due to the poor UTF-8 support in older browsers, this means that for now we need to stick to ASCII (or some local charsets, such as ISO-8859-1). I got bitten by that already, badly.

I'm looking for ways of dealing with this cleanly, any input in that area is very welcome.
