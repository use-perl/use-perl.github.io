Tirolian XML

darobin on 2001-12-11T15:36:43

I was running through my logs today and noticed that the Dahut document got its fair share of hits (compared to the usual amount of hits a non-existant website gets). I also looked a bit at the referers to see where those folks were coming from, and unsurprisingly it turned out to be this journal, astray.com's scribot, and axkit.org's support page.

One more surprising bit however was some poor soul typing "XML AND Tirol" into Google (German) and finding the dahut. I can only presume that he was looking for an XML job, or something along those lines. I guess he must have fled and decided that XML was too dangerous a thing to do in the Tirol area.
