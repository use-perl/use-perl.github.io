Post-conference is always a good time to think about things to do, because you usually get some good ideas at the conference. In my case, YAPC being over is also helpful because I get a life back.

* Start working with Kevin M. and Jeff B. on a conference module to help the next YAPC group. Maybe Conference::Toolkit?

* Post here once in a while.

* Hang around my house. :)
