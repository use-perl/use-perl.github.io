Hola, amigo.

Louis_Wu on 2004-05-03T06:16:51

When you mark someone as a friend (click on the yellow face image next to a name to start that process), that friend's journal is added to your amigo list. I made a link to my amigo list and put it in my "User Space" slashbox (which can be modified in your preferences). The following link should be a page of the journals of your friends - http://use.perl.org/user/cbrandtbuffalo/amigos. I also look for journals of people I haven't read before in the "Recent Journals" slashbox.

You can read the journals I read if you really want to, http://use.perl.org/user/louis_wu/amigos. Enjoy the journals, they're fun.
