2shortplanks on 2003-02-20T23:43:39

So on Wednesday I'm going to mailbox to move my server. It's not much of a move - in fact it's going one rack over to be placed in robin's rack next door to it.

acme's going to come along to shove his new 1U box in the rack too. Oh, and we get UPS as well. Yey! Boy, would that have been handy with the brown outs during the time the advent calendar was running on it.
