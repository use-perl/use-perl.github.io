use.perl.org/search.pl?op=journals for recent journals41

mr_bean on 2005-05-08T10:37:56

I was frustrated by not being able to see a list of the most recent journals while logged in, the same way that you can when not logged in, so I felt quite pleased with myself, realizing http://use.perl.org/search.pl?op=journals gives you that list, logged in or not.

At the same time, I wondered why it took me so long to realize that I could enter by hand the URL, looking at it from the link on the front page.
