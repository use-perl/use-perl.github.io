Beauty Is Our Business

jplindstrom on 2004-07-13T00:35:49

Beauty is important in engineering terms because software is so complicated. Complexity makes programs hard to build and potentially hard to use; beauty is the ultimate defense against complexity. David Gelernter in Machine Beauty: Elegance and the Heart of Technology

Beauty is an emergent property. You focus on the other stuff (functionality, performance, maintainability, ...) and, when you get that stuff down, you get beauty for free. William Grosso
