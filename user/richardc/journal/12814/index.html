Light bit of benchmarking. Even with our rapidly multiplying navigation styles mariachi is twice the speed of mhonarc for a full regen of the siesta archives. I don't know which to be more surprised by, our performance on lightly optimised alpha code, or mhonarcs apparent lack with mature code.

Of course, Hypermail kicks our ass - 0.3 wallclocks, we barely scan and prep the mails in 2!
