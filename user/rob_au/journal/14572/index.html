Remember ...

rob_au on 2003-09-08T11:05:04

When you drop and reload a database, all of the information in this data store which you have not backed up will be lost ... This is an important thing to remember :-)

This realisation came after staring blankly at my terminal screen trying to figure out why some of my development tests dependent upon certain database records were failing.
