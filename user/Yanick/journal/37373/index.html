OS Bootcamp is a cool initiative to inform and initiate university students to the wonders of the Open Source world.

OsBootCamp 8, which took place on August 28th, was about interpreted programming languages. On the agenda were short introductions of the three big kids on the block (Perl, Python and Ruby). And yup, the honor to ramble about Perl was given to li'll old me. For the morbidly curious, there's a video of the resulting show. :-)
