Happy birthday to me

vsergu on 2002-01-16T18:49:05

At least two others here have had birthdays in the past week, which would be particularly appropriate if we were Japanese and celebrating Coming of Age Day now. Like them, I'm celebrating by going in to work.

One advantage of being born this time of year is that we get another crack at resolutions shortly after we've managed to blow the ones made at New Year's. I hadn't actually made a resolution to write in my journal, but it's not too late. Today's my birthday, and I've discovered I somehow have a fan even though I've yet to make a journal entry, so I thought I'd better give him something to read. Not that I'm guaranteeing it will something good to read.

This journal, if it persists, will probably be yet another collection of more-often-than-not non-Perl-related ramblings. I don't know why anyone would want to read it, but then I don't know why I've been reading other people's for the past couple of months.
