This story made me pause to consider my own mortality and the mortality of my modules.

It is something that I think about from time to time. So here is my last will and testament in relation to my CPAN modules.

  I, John McNamara, being of sound body and mind bequeath ownership of
   my CPAN modules to the first person, upon my death, to answer the
   following two questions:
  1. How does this work:
         du | perl -lpe '${_{_}}+=+${_}}{${_}+=+${_{_}}'
  2. What is the last binary record in an Excel file.
  Any disputes pertaining to this bequest should be resolved by a game
   of Perl Golf in a public forum.
