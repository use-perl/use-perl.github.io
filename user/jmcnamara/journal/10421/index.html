The Love Song of J. Alfred Perlfrock

jmcnamara on 2003-02-05T22:57:29

No! I am no Perl Guru, nor was meant to be;
Am an attendant lord, one that will do
To patch a module, write a script or two,
Use vi; no doubt, an easy tool,
Deferential recommender of use
strict, -w and or warnings to use;
Full of one-liners, some a bit obtuse;
At times, indeed, almost ridiculous --
Almost, at times, the Fool.

J. Alfred Perlfrock
