Shop-specific tokens are a bad idea anyway. Better to get generic ones so that the recipient can go to weird specialist shops instead of being stuck with the bland high-street names. The large book token and theatre token schemes are also less likely to go bust even if individual members of the scheme do. Unfortunately, I don't think that record tokens exist any more.
