Water, water, water. And not a drop to drink

nicholas on 2006-09-22T00:12:51

So I ran a "stumbler" all the way from Waterloo East to Grove Park. [about 8 miles]. In that time it [KisMAC 0.21a] found 511 wireless networks. (which surprises me as somehow it finds them even when the train is travelling at 60mph, sometimes observing packets for up to 8 seconds)

Anyway, the security conscious will be pleased to known that of these 511 networks, none are practical for establising an ssh connection to a known host.
