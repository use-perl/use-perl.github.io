Email stats - since everyone else is doing it.

koschei on 2002-01-24T07:38:11

A little analysis with a nice little program results in the revelation that:

  • I sent 2326 emails.
    • That's 6.37 emails a day.
  • I sent to 2529 distinct email addresses.

Must've been some good CC's in there.

Out of the addresses sent to, generally the highest figures were to mailing lists (in positions 1-2, 5-8, 10-12, 14-17).

Then there were personal ones -- my Dad (3), guy who lives down the Hall (4), the boss (9), gf (18), Mum (19) and some other friends (13,20).

Position 20 had 25 emails, position 1 had 155.

I think I need to send more personal emails. Of course, the figures are slightly skewed in that most people I know have a few email addresses (my gf has about 3 that she uses), so the figures are only really for addresses sent to rather than people.

Oh, as for USENET - no need to do any analysis, since I rarely read it, and post even less often.
