And for those who want a faster pace

mst on 2005-08-05T02:27:21

There's DBIx::Class - a new currently-experimental project to create an ORM inspired by Class::DBI, but with much more powerful join/prefetch/view support and more emphasis on resultsets as well as rows.

DBIx::Class also provides a Class::DBI compat layer which is already capable of passing the vast majority of the Class::DBI test suite and is accumulating tests of its own and active committers at a good rate. For those who're interested -

Wiki: http://dbix-class.shadowcatsystems.co.uk/
SVN: http://dev.catalyst.perl.org/repos/bast/trunk/DBIx-Class/
List: http://lists.rawmode.org/mailman/listinfo/dbix-class
IRC: irc.perl.org#dbix-class
