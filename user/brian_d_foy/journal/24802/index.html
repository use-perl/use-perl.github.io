Camel Beach 2005

brian_d_foy on 2005-05-20T21:19:47

The Perl Review is sponsoring a Perl retreat to Fire Island, New York for the weekend after the 4th of July. That's Friday to Sunday, July 8-10.

I'll be there, and I'm working on some other people to show up. We'll decide what to do and what to talk about based on who signs up and what they want to do. We'll use a Just-In-Time organization.

Space is limited since it's a beach house, and there will be a nominal fee to cover basic expenses and food, but it will be much less than a random person would expect to pay for a weekend on Fire Island.

If you are interested, let me know. I think there is still some odd anti-spam thing going on with my regular address, but bdfoy@cpan.org should work since that goes to GMail.
