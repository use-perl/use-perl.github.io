Big Nerd Ranch Perl Bootcamp, Frankfurt, Germany, Nov 12-15

brian_d_foy on 2007-08-17T07:12:32

I'll be teaching Stonehenge's Intermediate Perl course for Big Nerd Ranch in Frankfurt, Germany, from November 12-15.

Big Nerd Ranch is door-to-door, soup-to-nuts service, and all you have to do is show up on Sunday and stay until Friday. You'll get to learn Perl without having to worry about anything else because they take care of it for you. It's like going to an all-inclusive resort where you get to use computers without feeling guilty. :)

Also, once the day is over, I'm still around to help you with whatever you want to work on, even if it's something that's not related to the class (or to Perl necessarily). Need a little help on a work or personal project? Just ask! :)

Not only that, but Big Nerd Ranch does the same thing I offer at Stonehenge: any of my students can email at any time asking questions. I do my best to answer them or point them to the answers. I don't offer a time limit on that.

Want more? How about a free Big Nerd Ranch t-shirt? :)
