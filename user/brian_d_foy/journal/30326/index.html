Ten Things You'll Experience Only at OSCON 2006

brian_d_foy on 2006-07-18T16:44:17

The latest email from O'Reilly inviting me to attend OSCON (even though I'm already registered) is really odd. It's meaningless marketing stuff that's just plain wrong.

The mail is titled "Ten Things You'll Experience Only at OSCON 2006".

Out of those top ten things, 7 of them were at YAPC Chicago (about 400ish attendees), and most of them are at most YAPCs. These are the actual things that O'Reilly claims you can only experience at OSCON 2006. I haven't changed the headings:

1) Sessions

2) Tutorials

3) Keynote Presentations

4) Speakers

6) Sponsors and Exhibitors

7) Events

10) People


The ones that weren't at YAPC Chicago don't seem all that special:

5) O'Reilly Radar: The Executive Briefing

8) OSCamp

9) The O'Reilly Booth in the Exhibit Hall
