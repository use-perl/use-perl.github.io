Appeals ruling in JMRI case: Artistic License holds up

brian_d_foy on 2008-08-14T21:41:33

A long time ago, people were worried about the Artistic License in the JMRI case. Some people got a bit too excited, and I told everyone that the Artistic License was just fine.

The district court, which ruled that the Artistic License was no license at all, has now been smacked by the appeals court. The district court got it wrong.
