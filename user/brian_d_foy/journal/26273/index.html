You might be the next presenter of Conferences for Beginners!

Even though OSCON is over, more conferences are on the way. Indeed, there are so many conferences that neither Jim Brandt nor I are going to make it to all of them. (Anyone want to pay my way to Amsterdam for Euro OSCON? Randal says Geekcruises' Perl Whirl should be enough exotic travel for one month).

The OSCON kwiki still has the Conferences for Beginners that we put together. Let's consider this Creative Commons material. Present it at your conferences! Add to it, expand it, and get more feedback! As more people present Conferences for Beginners, the better it will get.

Even if you aren't going to a conference, you can pretend you are. Add your thoughts and comments to what we already have.
