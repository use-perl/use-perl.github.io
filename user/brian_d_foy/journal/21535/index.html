Red Sox just keep on winning

brian_d_foy on 2004-10-27T06:32:34

Ever since Chris put the Sox logo on use.perl, the boys from Boston have not lost a game. They haven't even come close to losing. Now they are 3-0 in the World Series, which gives them 4 chances not to screw it all up in game 7.

It's vitally important that the Red Sox win tomorrow. If the Series goes all the way, no one in Massachusetts or Rhode Island will be sober enough to wake up on election day, perhaps giving up the state's 12 electoral votes to the other guy. The next president is likely to choose at least one Supreme Court justice, so we'd better get the right president.

So, if things go right and the Sox win tomorrow, averting a presidential disaster, you'll know who to thank, even if he does live on the wrong coast now.
