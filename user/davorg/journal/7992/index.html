Norwich.pm

davorg on 2002-09-25T09:38:55

One of the things that always bugged me about the list of Perl Mongers groups was that Norwich.pm was listed as being in "Great Britain" whereas the rest of the British groups were listed under "United Kingdom". Now whilst there is a difference betweem GB and the UK[1] and Norwich is in Great Britain - I've always thought it made more sense to list them all together.

So last night I moved Norwich. Fear my awesome geographical reclassification powers :)

Then this morning I got email from someone who assumed that I don't know British geography and explaining the relationship between the UK and GB and suggesting that I make exactly the change that I'd just made.

[1] The UK's full name is "The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland"
