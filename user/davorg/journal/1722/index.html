A Long-Expected Party

davorg on 2001-12-19T12:38:30

Tonight is the night that The Fellowship of the Ring opens world-wide. It's been a long time since I've looked forward to the opening of a film this much. I can't remember the last time I saw a film on the opening night (perhaps it was The Return of the Jedi in 1983).

So a group of london.pmers are off to see it at the Streatham Odeon tonight. I've got my Rivendell.pm t-shirt on and 50 pages of the book to (re-)read before 7:15 this evening.

It's all jolly exciting.
