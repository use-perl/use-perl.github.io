Security through obscurity

petdance on 2002-12-17T20:06:42

I would never post anything here, or on the web in general, that I wouldn't want someone to see. I think it was wise old Ann Landers that said that you shouldn't write anything down that you wouldn't want your mother, your spouse, your boss or your clergyman to see. (And that was before the days of Google)

Hoping that your words won't be seen is simply relying on security through obscurity.
