lower than Member of the British Empire (MBE) as MBE is the highest civilian honour that can be bestowed by the Queen. The Order was created by King George V in 1917 to reward those involved in WWI whose service merited recognition.

Re:Officer of the British Empire (OBE) is

hfb on 2001-12-31T16:20:14

Officer is higher than Member and the Order in descending order is as follows;

  • The Sovereign
  • Grand Master: The Duke of Edinburgh
  • Five classes with Military and Civil Divisions
  • Knights or Dames Grand Cross (G.B.E.)
  • Knights or Dames Commanders (K.B.E.or D.B.E)
  • Commanders (C.B.E.)
  • Officers (O.B.E.)
  • Members (M.B.E.)
  • Medal: (B.E.M.)
