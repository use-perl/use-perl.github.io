Spambusters

davorg on 2002-09-20T12:27:28

It's nice to find out that the company that you work for is one of the good guys.

One of Bibliotech's main services is a web mail system called Postmaster. Apparently, a few years ago someone used Postmaster addresses to send loads of spam and Bibliotech took them to court. They offered an out of court settlement which we refused. We went on to reach a settlement whereby he was banned from sending any spam and ordered to pay $1000 to anyone to anyone who he spammed in the future.

It even made the Register.
