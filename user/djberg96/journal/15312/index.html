djberg96 on 2003-10-21T15:39:39

This journal entry is in response to Ethan's journal about CPAN authors rating their own modules (which I agree is lame).

My solution (which I mentioned in a reply to Ethan's entry but wanted mentioned here) would be to require a minimum of 3 (or whatever) ratings before a rating actually becomes "visible" for that module. This prevents that silliness and helps guarantee a more balanced review.

Just an idea.
