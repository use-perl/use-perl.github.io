darobin on 2002-09-03T22:36:08

I wouldn't go for fruit juice over a litre a day. I did that (I was never a true caffeine addict) and unless you have a very solid digestive system it'll play unpleasant tricks on you.

Nothing's good as a primary drinking source apart from water. Even using a single mineral water brand over a certain dose will have side effects. The best thing imho is to change frequently between drinks that have different compositions. As far as I'm concerned I got quite used to drinking tap water after a week or two, and only go for something else when I need the sugar (that's excluding alcohol of course).
