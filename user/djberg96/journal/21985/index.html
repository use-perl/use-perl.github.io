The Lathe of Heaven

Hughman on 2004-11-24T18:57:08

The author of "The Lathe of Heaven" is Ursula LeGuin. It was originally produced for television by PBS in the '70s, starring Bruce Davison as George Orr, Margaret Avery (she would later play Shrug in "The Color Purple") as his lover, Heather, and Kevin Conway as his psychiatrist. It was an extraordinary production for its time. I've refused to see the Caan-Bonehead version.
