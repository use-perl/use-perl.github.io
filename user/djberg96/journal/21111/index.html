James Lipton, in case you haven't heard of him, does a great show called "Inside the Actor's Studio" where he does a pretty good one-on-one interview with just about any actor you can think of. It's not the usual "I'm just promoting my latest movie" crap. Sometimes it's deep. Often it's funny.

Probably the best one I ever saw was an extended interview with Mike Meyers. Oh...my...god - I almost fell off the couch laughing when he started doing impressions of his mother-in-law. Lots of other humor and generally a good interview.
