Dream interpretation

djberg96 on 2002-10-12T00:50:47

I normally don't remember my dreams. At most a day or two before it starts to fade. However, about a week ago I had one that stood out in my mind.

I was sitting on the pavement after some sort of accident. I'm ok, so I get up and start walking nowhere in particular. Soon, I'm jogging along a paved street, then a gravel or dirt road as the trees get more and more dense.

Soon a man I don't recognize slowly but surely begins to jog past me. For whatever reason, I feel compelled to follow him. He eventually leads me into a dense forest and into some sort of underground maze. It's not long before I realize that I'm hopelessly lost. Feeling apprehensive, I decide that my best bet is to keep following him.

"Where do this path lead?", I ask.

The man slows, turns and says, "Mercy Street".

Now, what do you think *that* means?
