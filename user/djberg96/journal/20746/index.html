G5 iMac Upgradability

deasys on 2004-09-05T17:02:14

According to http://docs.info.apple.com/article.html?artnum=86812, almost everything but the video hardware is serviceable/upgradeable.

I completely agree about the RAM--the machine is being severely choked with just 256 MB. Twice that is a good starting point. IMHO, Apple is shooting itself in the foot over this; reviewers will do their thing on the stock unit and conclude that, 'it's a fine machine but its performance sucks.' Apple should raise the price if it has to, but it shouldn't ship any G5 iMacs with less than 512 MB.
