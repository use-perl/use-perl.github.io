A trip though IMDB

gav on 2002-10-11T03:27:02

After "C.S.I." and now with "Without a Trace", Jerry Bruckheimer seems to have made the transition between being the golden boy of screen (Beverly Hills Cop, Top Gun, Bad Boys, Crimson Tide, Black Hawk Down, etc, etc.) to the golden boy of TV.

Anthony LaPaglia does a great job as the lead. I always thought he did a decent job taking over from Daniel Benzali in "Murder One". I think Benzali is a fantastic actor and manages to really carry a role. I am really interested in seeing Dead Heat which stars Kiefer Sutherland, Anthony LaPaglia, and Daniel Benzali. What a fantastic cast. I hope it's not a disappointment. Kiefer deserves a half decent film, he's showed enough talent in "24" and one of my favorites, Dark City.
