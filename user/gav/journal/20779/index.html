A Kitten Looked On

gav on 2004-09-08T04:40:37

Return of the Dragon: In some ways Bruce Lee's best film, great fight scenes, a classic showdown at the end, somewhat marred by a poor quality print, dreadful overdubbing, and a so-so story. The real highlights are Lee showing off with nunchaku and the showdown in the colloseum with a young Chuck Norris, watched by a cute kitten. Seven-time world champion Norris pits his Karate agains Lee's Jeet Kun Do. It's one of the most impressive fighting sequences on film, and it's worth the wait, even though you know that the good guy can't lose. I've always wondered if the fight had taken place for real who would win.
