Stupid billing systems

brian_d_foy on 2004-04-30T21:42:08

Near the end of my graduate school career, the finance office owed me 4 cents, and they sent me a check for it. I kept the check in my desk drawer, along with the envelope with the 32 cent stamp on it.

After 90 days, they sent me another check for 4 cents, because the other one had expired. Again, another 32 cent stamp.

This happened once more. I was tempted to go to the cashier to demand my 4 cents, but then I would have to walk across campus. I much preferred them to keep sending me the check.
