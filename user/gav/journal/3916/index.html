Memento

Absolutly fucking excellent! 9/10

First film I've seen in a long time that I have to really rave about. Top story, top direction, top acting, and gut wrenching plot twists. You get to watch everything backwards in this confusing, dark, though provoking masterpiece.

Guy Pearce is great, I've liked him since Priscilla and L.A. Confidential. Who'd have thought that an Australian soap called Neighbours could spawn such talent (along with Kylie Minogue, Natalie Imbruglia and Jason Donovan)?
