Being Ill

gav on 2003-05-22T22:19:13

This week I've been under the weather suffering from coughs, sneezing, running nose, the usual suspects. Tonight I've called in the artillery, NyQuil. I respect the fact that in these days of nice tasting medicine it's still green and nasty. It resembles something you'd get in a bar if you were brave enough to order something "strong and green". My advice? Throw away the stupid measuring cup thing. We all know the proper dose of NyQuil is a good swig. Enough to tickle your throat and leave an unpleasant taste in your tummy. Time to sit back and wait to be better.
