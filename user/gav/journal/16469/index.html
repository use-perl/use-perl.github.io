I've had cable internet for a couple of weeks now and it seemed to be at least as speedy as the DSL I had before, but I hadn't really downloaded anything big. Tonight I updated fink and downloaded Perl 5.8.2 on my iBook and noticed that my downloads got up to 390k/s, averaging 200-350k/s depending on the site.
