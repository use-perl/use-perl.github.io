Fiction writing

Christopher Blanc on 2007-09-28T15:04:45

I write short stories and am working on a book (see Chris Blanc, writer) and am looking at incorporating Perl as a form of pseudocode into these stories as allegory for human consciousness. I was intrigued by Perl poetry, but am looking for ways to code as if the variables I am using affect consciousness itself.
