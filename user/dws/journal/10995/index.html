dws on 2003-03-11T22:29:54

Judging from the 5 police cruisers parked in front, and what could be overheard through the front window, last night my neighbor, a college student who is living in his parents' rental unit, brought a bar fight most of the way home. SUVs where parked askew, the police had the street blocked off, handcuffs were applied, and there was even a latex-gloved crime-scene unit, though with far less gadgetry than the ones on CSI. My college experience was considerably more sedate.
