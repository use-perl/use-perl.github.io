Children's Christmas Play

Mr. Muskrat on 2003-12-10T06:25:52

The children's department of my church put on a play entitled "The Good News Christmas Cruise" this past Sunday morning. As the only adult with a part in the play (due to lack of boys who could memorize a part), I stuck out like a sore thumb.

Just in case you were wondering, I played the part of Mayor Ramirez; the mayor of Laruba, Costa Rica. I wore a black wig, fake mustache and makeup and spoke with my best hispanic accent. It was so much fun. I would definately participate again, if asked.
