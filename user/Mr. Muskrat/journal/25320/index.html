Naco Supreme

Mr. Muskrat on 2005-06-22T23:44:47

Taco Bell has a new item on the menu called a Crispwrap Supreme. I didn't really know what was in it until I got it home and took a bite. I couldn't help but say (in my best Ron Stoppable voice), "It's a Naco!"

For those of you who have watched Disney's Kim Possible (if you've got kids, you've probably seen it at least once), you'll know that Ron eats a Naco Supreme every afternoon at Bueno Nacho. So what is a "Naco"? It's Ron's greatest (and possibly only) creation; part nachos and part soft taco (or was it burrito? anywho).

It's a bit messy but really good.
