"Mindless Muskrats"

Mr. Muskrat on 2003-09-23T19:54:26

About a month ago my brother-in-law asked me to play fantasy football with him. I was not sure if I would like it. You see, I have never really gotten into football.

All through the draft, I kept asking him for advice on who to pick up. I really felt dumb.

We are now three weeks into the season and I can honestly say that I am having a great time! My team is still undefeated (even though I have ignored all advice about who I should drop/add/trade).

I now watch the games when I am able to. I even yell at the TV like every other NFL fan.

That trophy is sure going to look good sitting on my monitor at work. ;-)
