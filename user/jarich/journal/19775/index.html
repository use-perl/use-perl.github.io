Unless I paid cash.

Then he just bemoans the amount of cash (unaccountable funds) that get spent.

Well... maybe not that a regular job - I still get to work from home and do the laundry in my break times. And maybe not that a regular income - apparently I'm getting paid every 4 weeks, not the normal 2. But it should be the same amount each month. And certainly not that regular hours as far as the last 2 weeks have indicated....

Well... it'll make my tax return easier next year anyway.
