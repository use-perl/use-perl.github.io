Salesmen are funny

gizmo_mathboy on 2003-06-13T02:02:33

Is it wrong to tell a salesman to his face that you don't like his wares? An HP dude stopped buy trying to hawk Itanium.

To be honest I don't know if 64bit machines are in my (near)future. He did have a point about whether I'll even need it in the near future.

Ah, the joys of being sysadmin for a university engineering department. :-)
