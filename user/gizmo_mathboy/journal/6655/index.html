Feh Upon Those Houses!

chaoticset on 2002-07-27T23:07:12

My mother/stepfather and mother-in-law have been trying to convince me that, after the marriage (whose date has been changed yet again -- YAMDC?), my best bet is to get a house.

I don't know. Spend a shitload of time working on the place, mowing the lawn, installing siding, etc., and minor home repairs, vs. having a fairly snappy landlord who looks into these things more thoroughly than I ever would. It sounds like an easy choice to me: Rent.

They say it's the only investment you can ever make. I fail to see why I need to give up large chunks of time to make huge payments on my investment; precious metals sound like a much better deal.

I wouldn't mind owning a house as long as I had enough money that paying to keep it up wasn't prohibitively expensive or hassling. Otherwise, it sounds like a waste of time.
