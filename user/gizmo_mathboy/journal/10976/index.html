Strange Theory

jmm on 2003-03-11T19:17:45

A friend once told me of a strange theory, that seems to have some basis in fact, although I haven't tried to track down details.

He said that there is a left/right brain distinction between the processing of transmitted versus reflected light. It was determined by comparing how well proofreaders did when converting from paper to CRT displays - they would find some errors on the display, but miss many that they would have seen on paper.

I know that I prefer to read things on paper instead of on a CRT screen. However, I prefer paper to a LCD screen too, and that does use reflected light instead of transmitted light for its display.
