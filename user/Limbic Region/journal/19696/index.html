Spend money and save face vs do the job right

Limbic Region on 2004-07-05T14:24:02

Ok - so I am back from a trip that I can't talk much about. A major problem was uncovered elsewhere and while the people were brainstorming what to do about it - someone who probably wanted to sound hip, cool, and "in the know" mentioned Perl.

The next thing I know my boss is asking me if I can go tomorrow morning. Now my boss is a nice guy and would not have pressured me if I had declined because my wife who has been in the country since April 19th doesn't have a SSN, driver's license, access to cash or public transportation etc would have to be home alone. I did however learn that this was being very politically motivated so I agreed.

It turns out that the problem involved getting the intersection of two tables - SQL is NOT on of my areas of expertise. To top it off, they have resident experts that also know the environment. I learned a lot in the experience but it amazes me the lengths people will go to make other people happy instead of doing the right thing.

I am once again home again with Jean and am very happy to stay here ;-)
