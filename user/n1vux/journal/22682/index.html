NASA Details Earthquake Effects on the Earth
"The researchers concluded the Sumatra earthquake caused a length of day change too small to detect, but it can be calculated. It also caused an oblateness change barely detectable, and a pole shift large enough to be possibly identified." - JPL Release, via Science.Nasa.Gov and Slash-Dot
