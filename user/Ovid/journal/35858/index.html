On Consulting

Ovid on 2008-03-07T12:02:10

I'm still getting recruiters contacting me left and right. They've now started emailing me at the BBC (grrr ... don't do that unless you can guarantee I'll get rich). I also have no desire to leave the BBC. I'm quite happy here, but the thought of consulting has crossed my mind. I can't legally quit (again, I don't want to) and open up my own consulting shop here in the UK unless I want to get deported. However, I can work for up to an extra 20 hours a week in my own field and I confess I'm a bit tempted. I'd love to go into organizations and either get them set up on testing or create new testing strategies for them. I don't think it will work, though, as I am not available during working hours. Damn.
