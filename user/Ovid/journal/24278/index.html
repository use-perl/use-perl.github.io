I've used my second bedroom as an office for a long time. Recently, however, I've decided to take a roommate to help build up some cash. Schwern moved in but it turned out to be a bit difficult to move my office due to some wiring problems. Since he's been here, I've primarily been working on my iBook but, while I love the iBook, it's not the same as working on my Linux box).

A few days ago we had the landlord in to do a bit of rewiring, I went down to Fred Meyer and bought a corner desk and moved my computer. Wireless is back up and running, and while my bedroom is now my office, I'm quite happy to back at my regular computer. Of course, I'm sure that Schwern is quite happy to have it out of his bedroom, too.
