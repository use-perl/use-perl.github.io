Ovid on 2003-06-16T18:47:22

Sweeeeet! In checking out the Perl jobs site, I stumbled across their RSS feeds. One way to get a list of Perl jobs is to simply include the following on a Web page:

<script language="JavaScript" src="http://jobs.perl.org/rss/standard.js"></script>

Now I have list on the TOC of my CGI course.
