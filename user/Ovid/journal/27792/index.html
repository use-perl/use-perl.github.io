TPF: Nick Clark steps down

Ovid on 2005-11-29T20:05:51

As of November 20, 2005, Nick Clark has resigned from the Grants Committee. He's been on there for a while and decided it was time to step down and let someone else take his place. He'll be tough to replace as he has deep knowledge of Perl and the community (can one ever separate the two). Elections will be held soon to replace him and possibly add another couple of folks.

Good luck Nick! You'll be missed.
