TPF Grant reports

Ovid on 2006-03-07T18:29:13

Part of our work on the grant committee involves receiving regular reports of grant progress. In reviewing these reports, decisions can be made on whether to approve grant payments, give feedback to the grantee on issues or questions they may have or just have a sense of how things are going.

These reports will now be posted to the TPF blog. You can read about Nicholas Clark's progress on improving Perl 5 and Adam Kennedy's work in creating a PPI refactoring editor.

We also have Atom and RSS feeds.
