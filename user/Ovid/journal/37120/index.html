Allison Randal Visits the BBC

Ovid on 2008-08-07T11:48:54

Allison Randal was here two days ago. She gave a talk on Parrot which ran about two hours (an hour for the talk and tons of questions). Afterwards, many of us hit a local pub and continued the conversation. The following day, there was a fair amount of internal chatter about how awesome Parrot is. When it's ready, if we can use it here, we will.

There was also fascinating conversations about things which can't be discussed yet. Let's just say that the future of Parrot, in my opinion, looks very, very bright.
