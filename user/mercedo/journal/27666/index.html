Blue Rose

mercedo on 2005-11-20T19:29:32

My ex-wife and I went to the flower festival in our city. There 'blue rose' was exhibited, and because it was widely advertised, just too many people were making a queue to take a glace at the rose.

We too wanted to see the rose but it would take about an hour to reach the rose.

'Blue rose' in English ideom, means something impossible. We were able to understand the real meaning of the rose. Of course we didn't queue in a line. Apart from that, we had another perfect holiday since 30th of October when we went to another orchard &flower garden. The flowers of cosmos are still in the festival, except a few very cold days we are having unusually warm days these days.

It is certainly a blue rose to love my ex-wife once again but we are now on good terms with one another, that's going to be a good reason for us to spend another warm holiday. That was possible.
