Yesterday I cycled to Ayr and back. Nearly killed me. About 40km in total, and wind in my face on the return leg. I feel good for it though - it's good to be doing some sort of exercise now the snowboarding season is over.

It's difficult to cycle at the right time for photography. I really need to be biking around sundown, but then I'd be returning home in the dark. I have lights on my bike but I don't really trust Canadian drivers - some of them seem rather oblivious to cyclists.

Anyway, I did manage to find a spot with lots of shade and some old farm equipment. Great photographic opportunity. So this week's pics will probably all be from that place.
