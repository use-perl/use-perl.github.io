Taken this morning on her last day.

Shelby had gone downhill so fast we really didn't know how much longer she had left in her. The crippling arthritis in her back legs and spine almost prevented her from walking. And finally today she couldn't get up from outside in the snow, so it was time.

We'd had her 11 years, and she will be missed more than she could know.

Goodbye Shelby - no more pain now. Run in your sleep forever.
