I've had long conversations with friends, usually over cards and involving intoxication, about this very issue.

(btw, I'm afraid this won't be very original, as everyone else seems to have already mentioned mine)

For me, the only one I can see as Hannibal is Anthony Hopkins.

B.A. is a tough cast. Do you try to imitate Mr. T, chains, mohawk and all? I'd recommend no. I see a guy like Michael Clark Duncan or Ving Rhames doing well.

The Face. It's hard to classify what Dirk Benedict was in the 80's. I guess he was supposed to be sexy. I do like the Ben Affleck suggestion someone had, that hadn't occurred to me. My thought was Matthew McConahay.

Ahh yes, Murdock. This is the hardest role to cast. It can't be Jim Carrey, he'll just be, well, Jim Carrey. I know it sounds odd, but after recent viewings of 12 Monkeys and Fight Club, I lean a bit towards Brad Pitt in this role. He's proven he can be the on the edge lunatic. The other way to go might be Ben Stiller, though I wonder if he'd be able to disappear from the screen, of if he'd be just "Oh, look, Ben Stiller."
