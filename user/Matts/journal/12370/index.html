Matrix Review

Matts on 2003-05-22T12:38:39

It rocked.

What, you want more than that?

OK, so it was a slightly weaker story line, but I was definitely in the mood for a non-stop action movie, and that's what I got. Amazing new effects. Agent Smith is *hilarious*. I nearly peed my pants when he said "It's all me, me, me". Fantastic new scary bad guys.

Yes, Reeves can't act his way out of a paper bag.

Yes, the dance-love scene needs to go. I hope it's cut from the DVD (or at least I can skip it).

Yes, you could pick this one to pieces a lot more than you could the first.

But I don't want to. I just enjoyed it for what it was - a great action movie with a cool geek side.
