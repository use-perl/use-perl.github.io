Decorating porn, and the wisdom of TiVo

petdance on 2002-08-15T03:07:20

Mrs. Petdance loves "Trading Spaces" on TLC. She has a season pass on the TiVo for them. She watches them, and discusses various decorating debacles with her mom and sisters. It's a family-wide love affair.

Well, now, since we have the TiVo, she doesn't even watch the show. She just goes for the money shot: Right to the end of the episode when the couples see the rooms. No suspense, no buildup: Just hot vicarious decorating action.

In other TiVo news, the TiVo is always interesting in deciding what we might like to watch in addition to Austin City Limits, Teletubbies and The West Wing. Today it decided to tape an hour of the Weather Channel.

And isn't it kinda funny how we still call it "taping"?
