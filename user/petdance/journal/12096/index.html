A symptom of Hollywood

barbie on 2003-05-09T16:04:13

Obviously, in the terms of this movie, any school where the kids have to study is bad, just as a school where the kids can run around and raise hell is good.

Unfortunately Hollywood plants this seed in several school related films. You're not cool unless you skip school (Ferris Bueller), treat school as a joke (Grease) or the only sane one in a bunch of weirdos (The Faculty). You also get the view that 'jocks' are all bozos. Maybe they are, but I seriously doubt it. There probably are films that do treat studying responsibly but I can't recall one.

Personally I find too many of the "comedy" movies coming out of Hollywood very lightweight on any real humour. The last great Eddie Murphy (IMHO) was 48 Hours, since then he's used one of two characters (straight and comedic) in every film. Films such as Dumb and Dumberer only further the cause into mediocrity (then again I am not a Jim Carey fan at all).

Slightly OT, but having seen recently the "initiation ceremony" that has been featured in the news, perhaps its about time Hollywood took a turn for the better and made nerds and geeks cool for daring to want a decent education. They're certainly the ones who epitomize American success stories :)
