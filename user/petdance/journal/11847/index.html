Amy: Perl, it's a programming language he uses. I don't understand either.
Me: Come on, you know some stuff! Who wrote Perl?
Amy: Randal?
Me: No.
Amy: Damian?
Me: No. Ok, maybe it hasn't rubbed off yet... But at least you knew the names!
