myperl.eu has come out of beta

karjala on 2007-06-08T08:46:13

MyPerl (http://myperl.eu/) is a site that offers RSS feeds for all of the Perl modules.

You can subscribe to a module's RSS feed, and get snippets of that module's Changelog in the feed with new versions of the module, as they come out.

If you log on with OpenID, then you'll be able to create a custom RSS feed of modules you choose.

And finally you can subscribe to the "All Modules" feed that gives you notifications for every module there is, with a short description for the module.

Soon we'll have a forum for each module.
