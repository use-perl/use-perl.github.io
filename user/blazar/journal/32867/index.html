blazar on 2007-04-01T12:27:41

And I was being effective too, whoa! Come to think of, I realize today's date and... I wholeheartedly hope that is not to be interpreted as a destiny's April Fool's joke on me! Anyway, /me is still fighting...

Second part: as I woke up I read USENET as usual. I found an April Fool's joke in twice, because it is a recurring theme there. (Namely that Alan Moore is visiting Italy, and more precisely Florence on this particular day.)

Then, divinely inspired, I posted my own joke to isam, the/an Italian martial arts ng.

A very nice and effective joke was played to all users on Italian Wikipedia too.
