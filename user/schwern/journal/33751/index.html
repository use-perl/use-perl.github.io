At Last The Perl 5 Wiki

schwern on 2007-07-09T08:34:17

For a long time now people have been grumbling about making a Perl 5 wiki. Some place central and official-looking to write down all things Perl. Well, at long last here it is! Announcing the Perl 5 Wiki!

And like any new Wiki it needs content. Content provided by YOU the user of Perl. Here's some suggestions.

  • Add to the glossary.
  • Help write up lists of recommented CPAN modules.
  • Write down anecdotes and bits of lore. Maybe weird things that happened at a Perl Mongers meeting or conference, like the infamous London.pm quicksort dance.
  • Or add yourself and others to the people of Perl.
  • Add profiles of businesses known to use Perl.


Above all, be bold and add content! If you're not sure its appropriate, add it anyway.
