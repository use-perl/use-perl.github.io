The Brain That Wouldn't Die!

schwern on 2006-07-13T07:44:01

During OSCON a group of us will be attending a performance of THE BRAIN THAT WOULDN'T DIE which you may know from MST3K.

"Alive... without a body... fed by an unspeakable horror from hell!"

This is a live, theatrical performance done straight up. They're not lampooning it, this isn't an MST3K rip-off. They're actually performing it. The result is said to be... well, something to be seen. These are the same folks who last year did a theatrical performance of "Manos: The Hands of Fate" (which I missed, damnit).

Its an easy walk (10 blocks) from the conference. We're going Thursday night. Show starts at 8. $12. I have no idea how popular this will be or how big the theater is so I'd suggest buying your tickets online beforehand.
