That's not a new feature

jmm on 2003-05-28T13:31:43

The original Macintosh case (128, 512, plus, SE) required a Torx screwdriver with a 12 inch (30 cm) shaft to undo or tighten the screw that held the shell of the case to the base. You had to buy the screwdriver specially because nobody had evre previously owned one. Initially, only Apple service people could get them, although tool makers eventually covered this market.
