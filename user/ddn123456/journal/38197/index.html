Belgian Perl Workshop February 28th, 2009 - 2nd edition.

ddn123456 on 2009-01-01T16:29:04

I am very pleased to announce the Belgian Perl Workshop 2009 - 2nd edition.

Among the invited speakers are Jonathan Worthington, Abigail and Matt Trout.

The theme for this year's workshop is "Discovering Perl".

Track Themes are:

  • Perl Discovered by Companies
    • Perl on the work floor empowering companies to make ICT-based business happen, grow, flourish.
  • Perl Discovery
    • Perl Tech Topics: from Beginner's up to Expert Level
    • Perl Lightning Talks
  • Perl Catalyst Discovery
    • Perl Catalyst Tech Topics: from Beginner's up to Expert Level
    • Perl Catalyst Hands-On


And of course a Perl Social Event in the evening :-).

Official languages are English (preferred), Dutch & French.

This event takes place on February 28th, 2009 in Leuven (30 kms east and only 20 minutes by train from Brussels ).

There is still room left for speakers and participants, so do subscribe.

For more information please visit www.perlworkshop.be.
