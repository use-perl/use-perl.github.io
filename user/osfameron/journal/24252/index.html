Ah, OK, it is possible to get data access bundles (I thought the phone trainers instore were supposed to know their stuff so they could sell to you).

They're called "Orange World Access", and it's
  • £8 for 10MB
  • £16 for 25MB
  • £41.13 for 80MB
  • £52.88 for 400MB
  • £88.13 for unlimited (subject to fair use 1GB)


So when salesman said it was unlimited, he was sortof right - it's advertised as that but means 1Gb. (Though he was wrong about it staying at that level, which would have been great).
