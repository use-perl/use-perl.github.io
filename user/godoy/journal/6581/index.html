Visited places

godoy on 2002-07-23T17:06:46

  • Bordeaux --- Very nice city. Friendly people. Living there might be nice. Or not. It depends on what you're going to do...
  • Paris --- Very fast visit. I didn't like the people... Sorry for who lives there, but you have to agree with me that people are not all that friendly.
  • Wilhelmshaven --- VERY beautiful city. Very small. In the North Sea, in Germany. I want to live there --- if there are jobs with computers (fortunately Internet helps a lot here) :-)
  • Toulouse --- Nice city in France, again. I could live there too. :-)
  • Sergovia --- In Spain. Interesting old city. Small. Friendly people. Very beautiful girls.
  • Madrid --- Very good. VERY hot. I could live there with air conditioning. Everywhere. It was 40 celsius degrees on Friday. I love cold places...


These are the cities I've been at, in those two weeks in Europe. France, Spain and Germany. I need to go back soon, since there are lots of other countries and cities to visit.

Germany was exactly how I imagined. France was messier. Spain hotter.
