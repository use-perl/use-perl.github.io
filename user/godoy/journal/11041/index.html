vsergu on 2003-03-15T13:24:41

The HTML/XHTML entities are &mdash; and &ndash;, not and [XHTML spec]. But for better browser support, you might want to use &#8212; and &#8211; instead.

Test:

  • &mdash; is displayed as —
  • &ndash; is displayed as –
  • &#8212; is displayed as —
  • &#8211; is displayed as –
