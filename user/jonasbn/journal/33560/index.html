So the assimilation proces has been halted since I am on total control of what I am doing, how I am doing it and when and I even get time to do some coding.

Absolutely marvellous, I am working on selling this workpackage to another department with the client, I think I mentioned this before. Let's hope they go for it, because this is actually turning out quite nicely (and it lets me ignore the fact that I still do not have contract).
