Perl Pub Quiz and Perl Birthday Celebration

jonasbn on 2007-12-19T21:09:47

Last night Copenhagen Perl Mongers celebrated Perl's birthday. Not many showed but it is understandable this close to Christmas. Anyway we finally got around to playing the Pub Quiz developed by the Wellington Perl Mongers.

So we had a nice time and I can only recommend playing the pub quiz if you need something to do at one of your social meetings.

I am going to write our feedback to Wellington.pm when I get the time.

Contact Grant McLean, Coordinator of Wellington.pm if you want to have a look at the quiz, but do not spoil the fun and put the answers somewhere public.
