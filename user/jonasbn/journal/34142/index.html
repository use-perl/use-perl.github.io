CONTRACT, signed

jonasbn on 2007-08-17T12:04:05

I went to my office Wednesday. I had not been there for a month and stuff was piling up at home, work related stuff that had to go to the office and I had to check the snailmail.

Anyway just after I got in the snailmail arrived - and guess what the long awaited contract was there.

I read through it and I could not help laughing. The contracted ended on the 15th. August - which was Wednesday.

So I stuck the contract in my bag and went on with my work.

Thursday I was back at the client's office I signed the contract and sent it via internal mail to the Sourcing Office. I set the signature date to the the 16th. of August and notes that the CEO's signature was the 16. of July.

Anyway now I have the contract in place and can send an invoice and I can start looking into prolonging the contract for 3 months more.

It has takes 3 months to get the contract in place, so perhaps I will have the next contract by medio November when it expires, lets see how it goes this time around.
