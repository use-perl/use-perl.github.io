Reading Period!?

jonasbn on 2005-07-20T13:46:01

I am currently enjoying my summer holiday on the danish island Bornholm located in the baltic. This is my first holiday with my son and he takes up a lot of time I normally spend sleeping or doing other things (I cannot really remember what I used to do, I must have had oceans of sparetime). Apart from my son it has been necessary to bring along a load of work.

I am able to squeeze in some work slots when the little guy is sleeping and I am not sleeping, eating or going for a run, so I am fairly occupied with work and family activities - and I have found out what suffers from this - reading.

I do not seem to have time to sit down with a book, which is what I normally do during my vacations I guess. I brought along "Randal Schwartz's Perls of Wisdom", which I am currently attempting to finished in order to write a review.

Hopefully I will find some time in August, when I am back at work to finish the book and the review.
