jonasbn on 2006-12-15T08:37:50

As a sort of spin-off from the 'Give up your modules!' thread on the module-authors mailing list - I have moved the Workflow CVS repository and project activities to Sourceforge.net.

I sent a mail to all of the people whom I have been in contact with, while I have been the maintainer and invited them to get involved.

So are you interested, here is the invitation:

Hello,

I am writing to you because you are somehow involved in or have an interest in Workflow, Chris Winters' module on CPAN.

I just want to write you to inform you that I have moved the project to SourceForge (http://www.sf.net), which mean it will be easier to get involved.

https://sourceforge.net/projects/perl-workflow/

The name Workflow was occupied so I took the next logical name which sprung to mind.

I have moved the CVS repository and have redone the latest changes (releases 0.24 and 0.25) this morning, so we should be up to speed.

http://perl-workflow.cvs.sourceforge.net/perl-workflow/

I have set up a mailinglist for discussion of development issues and ideas:

https://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/perl-workflow-devel

Please forward this mail to whomever you find appropriate.

I hope some of you will be interested in getting involved as project members, I expect to take a role as project admin, release manager and developer/maintainer, but I do not have all the time I could wish for and it does not balance well with my TODO list and ideas on the drawing board so, consider this an invitation to get even more involved.

The project homepage is in the making and will soon be uploaded.

Feedback is welcome,

jonasbn, current maintainer of Workflow
