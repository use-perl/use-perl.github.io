2003

jonasbn on 2004-01-01T10:45:00

2003 was the year where I got my PAUSE id, I have uploaded 4 modules of my own and several releases of these and two releases of modules where I act as maintainer.

It has been a good learning experience to put all these modules on CPAN, I have had them tested on more platforms than I have available at home and I have had some nice feedback on the modules from many people.

I hope 2004 we be an even more active your for me on CPAN and that I will be able to contribute with even more modules (and more releases).

Generally CPAN seem to have grown from what I can pickup here at use.perl. I am happy to be a part of this trend and I look forward to see the CPAN grow even more, making Perl a more flexible language and seeing what other things Perl can be put to use as.

Happy new year everybody
