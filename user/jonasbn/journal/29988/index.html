NPW 2006 over

jonasbn on 2006-06-21T12:08:18

Back from 2 marvellous days of Perl in Oslo, the workshop was great, I can feel that everytime I go back to the community things I tend to enjoy it more and more - I now people from all over the globe and even hanging out the the Copenhagen Perl Mongers is nice, since I do not get to do that ever so often.

Stayed at the same hotel as Brian d foy, so I got to talk a lot to him about a lot of things, he is a great guy and I hope he will come by Copenhagen some time.

We announced that the next NPW (2007) will be in Copenhagen, it's been a while since we have had the workshop here and our decision not to go for YAPC::Europe, makes is more interesting to host the NPW again, however if ANYBODY are interested in hosting a NPW in perhaps, Finland, Iceland or another place in one of the nordic countries please let me know asap.

Audrey wrapped the NPW up an a lightning-talk presentation, which kind of amazed me I have never seen presentations used in such a poetic manner, my own presentations are pale in comparison, I have a long way to go...
