Nordic Perl Workshop 2005

jonasbn on 2005-10-28T19:44:54

The NPW of 2005 is over - and I can only say I enjoyed it very very much. A great variation of talks and only a single track - social events in the evenings and I got to talk to a lot of people.

So thats to Claes the organizer and all of the attendees for making it a great workshop.

I can mention some of the things I found really good.

The venue, was ok, but the room in which the talks where where good, it had kind of a class room feeling to it, which seem to fit in with the workshop concept.

The one-track event, which made things very settled, not all the confusion of people moving around, so things where really relaxed.

I am looking very much forward to the Nordic Perl Workshop in Oslo next year, I hope they will be able to make an event of the same caliber.
