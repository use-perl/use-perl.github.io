Yet Another Community and the Multiples

jonasbn on 2002-11-11T09:08:07

Yesterday I submitted a photograph taken by one of my fellow Copenhagen Perl Mongers to http://www.deviantart.com/ (with his conscent of course).

Only a few minutes past and I received the first comment, here is a really active and dynamic community, evolving around arts, check it out.

All this was going on while I was watching a TV programme about a swedish guy whom had gotten in contact with a canadian woman who suffered from split-personality syndrome (referring to herself as a multiple).

The funny thing was that she praised the Internet and it communication capabilities and all her personalities used the Internet.

Well I do not know why I mention this here, but I really had an amazing sunday evening.
