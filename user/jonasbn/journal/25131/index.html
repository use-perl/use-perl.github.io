Somewhat Disappointed

jonasbn on 2005-06-10T09:57:30

We are attempting to arrange a Perl event in Copenhagen (next week) called PerlDag (PerlDay), you can see more at: http://www.perldag.dk/2005 due to the postponing of the Nordic Perl Workshop.

The event is a week away and we do not have enough papers to fill in the slots we need, so I have been mailing high and low to get some more talks.

We have a lot of people who have indicated interest in participating and we have the sponsors we need, it bothers me that what might kill the event is a lack of technical content.

We attempted to annouce this event on use.perl quite some time ago, but it is still pending. This would probably not have any influence on the number of papers/talks, but I am disappointed that use.perl, which is kind of the bulletin board of the Perl community is not addressing our story, if it should not be approved then disapprove it - just make a decision.
