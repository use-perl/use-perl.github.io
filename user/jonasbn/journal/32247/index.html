NPW underway

jonasbn on 2007-01-24T20:23:12

We just had a meeting today in our endeavor to organize the next Nordic Perl Workshop.

The meeting was very fruitful, we got assignments divided and we discussed some nice things we want do with our workshop. In general we want it to be less conference/YAPC and more workshop.

So tonight I have sent a few mails to the Nordic Perl Workshop list and to my fellow organizers whom are taking over stuff I initiated - I got a few things of my back, this mean I can now focus on other - I am busy but I took the time to update my Workshop-howto document the document has been converted to POD after suggestion by Maddingue.

Now I need to write a Call for Papers, luckily I have some of the CFPs from earlier years in subversion.
