Extraordinary Musical Experience

jonasbn on 2006-07-14T08:02:50

Wednesday I had an exceptional opportunity to go to a local bed and breakfast in Allinge/Bornholm and listen to a concert with american "Mercury Rev", this place is VERY small and having a visit from an american band is quite special and it being a band as good as "Mercury Rev".

The concert was marvellous and I MUST catch up on my "Mercury Rev" I have not followed the band since about 1990 when I got introduced. I have heard a few of their newer songs, but the concert really convinced me to invest in some new CDs.
