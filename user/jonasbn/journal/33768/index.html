Threats from a Spammer

jonasbn on 2007-07-10T11:45:58

Received this spam today:

Hi It`s lara again. Will you ever contact me? I made those nude pictures especially for you and I wont write to you again! If you wanna see them just drop me a line at: blara93@freemailwap.info

I particularly noticed this line:

I wont write to you again!

What a threat!

If only they would actually stop writing, but I do not believe spammers to be people you can actually trust.

I am not a spammer, so I am not sure how spamming works in detail, but by exposing spammers to spammer addresses, would these people spam each other? I guess they do pick up some addresses by scanning web sites etc.

I was about to obfuscate the address in the spam mail, but then I thought, why even bother.
