YAPC::Europe Day 1

jonasbn on 2002-09-19T12:51:53

Well first day is (almost) over. The party I am travelling with (Copenhagen Perl Mongers) and I got here a bit late, so we actually only made the lunch and Michael G. Schwerns presentation on tests...

Well as always Schwerns presentation was a chaotic experience, but anyway it was both entertaining and very informative as always.

I am trying to lay out plans for what other presentations I want to hear and I must admit there is a lot of interesting stuff such as:

- Source Filters - ExtUtils::ModuleMaker - ELRIC (agent technology)

So tomorrow will be a long and hard day.

Right now I am in the mode to drink beer or catch up on some Perl programming, but I am not sure I am fit for the latter, and the first is probably more realistic eventhough we have to be here at nine tomorrow.

Well I am feeling the same experience as when I attended TPC5 last year, a nice feeling of inspiration and urge to hack some Perl and I guess the urge will be even greater tomorrow after having listened to the presentations mentioned above.

Source Filters sound very interesting, but I have never had a chance to use them, perhaps after tomorrow.

ExtUtils::ModuleMaker could be expected to very much in line with my thoughts on some of the POD utilies I have on the draing board.

And finally agents have for a long time been one of my interests, sad that the PerlAgent mailing list is so low-(no)-traffic.

Well that is it from me for now, hopefully I can write another entry tomorrow.
