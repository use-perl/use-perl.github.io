My Main Perl Project, Dada Mail is having a New Features Fundraiser! Yeah!

The details of it can be found at:

http://dadamailproject.com/fundraiser.html

The fundraiser itself is being hosted by Fundable at:

http://www.fundable.com/groupactions/groupaction.2008-11-24.5962353285

And I'm holding a vote for which feature(s) will get included at:

http://dadamailproject.com/support/boards/viewtopic.php?f=23&t=608

I'm hoping for at the very least $1,000 for a month's work on a feature, which works out to a little more than $10/hour full time or $20/hour halftime - which is about the amount I got for interning at a web design company,

when I was nineteen.

But, I don't want to gouge my users with some ridiculous goal that will never be reached, so a little amount to offset the costs of developing and allow me to focus on features, rather than simply maintenance I think, would benefit my entire community.
