Our next release will be 5.2, because we've restructured the engines a little. (All the engine classes now have ::Base abstract classes, and the actual class with try to DTRT ( ::Apache will pick the right ModPerl version for you and so on). We've also added APR support for fcgi and renamed it to FastCGI to be more consistent.

You won't have to change your application, but it's not really backwards compatible, and you will have to regenerate the scripts, so we signify that with a more significant version number. So when looking for the sweetest Catalyst release, go for X.x.x.x.x.x.x.x .... :D
