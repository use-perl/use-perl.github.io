As with just about any site, there are temporary hiccups. The guys behind the scenes are very quick to respond and it's rare that it's out of action for any long period. Having said that the last hiccup was probably about a year ago, if not longer, so it's definitely not a frequent occurance either.

As for other sources. There is Kobesearch (Randy Kobes' alternative search CPAN site) or BackPAN if what you need isn't recent.
