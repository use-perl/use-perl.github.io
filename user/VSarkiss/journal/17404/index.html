Flunking kindergarten

VSarkiss on 2004-02-13T18:32:57

I mentioned here a while ago about adopting our dog from the animal shelter. They guessed her age from her teeth since she was a stray, but based on that, we figured she turned six months old last month. So I signed her up for an obedience class from our city's rec department. She's very affectionate and gets along great with people, but doesn't behave well around other dogs. I thought the class was a great opportunity to socialize her.

Yesterday, after getting yet another blister on my hand from her pulling on the leash, I decided there's no point in continuing right now: she's just not "getting it". She's a smart dog, but she keeps backsliding. I figured I'll try again in a few months, and keep working with her at home in the meantime.

My wife says this is the first time she's heard of anyone flunking out of kindergarten....
