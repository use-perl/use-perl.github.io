Slashcode slightly underdocumented...

bart on 2002-11-13T18:26:29

(I can figure out Friends, Fans, and Foes, but what's a "Freak"? In the slash sense, I mean)
You can? It took me hours to get the basics on this "friends" system. The main slashcode does not in the least document how it all works. Blimey.

A "Freak" is the same in relation to a "Foe", as a "Fan" is to a "Friend". Same relationship, other end.

But later, when I added ziggy as a friend, a new "~ziggy (25)" tab showed up.
That one had me baffled too. It turns out that, whenever you click on a user info link, this person becomes the subject of that tab, except when it's you — you have your own tab. Well, that's what I make of it. It has something to do with the root of the subdirectory you're in. For example, if you go to this link, you'll see the tab for Pudge appear — except when you are him.

And it sticks. If you leave the site and come back in a day or two, it'll be the same person on that tab. Even if you log out, and log in again, manually.
