VSarkiss on 2004-12-07T16:11:31

I finally got around to upgrading Firefox from 0.9.x to 1.0. Very nice.

Not only does it seem faster, but all the extensions I like were available as well. Most importantly, all my AdBlock entries came over without a hitch.
