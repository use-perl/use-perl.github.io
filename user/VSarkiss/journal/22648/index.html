Rain, rain, go away

VSarkiss on 2005-01-10T22:29:37

OK, I know the midwest is getting snowstorms, and this is nothing compared to earthquakes and tsunamis, but I'm sick and tired of all the rain we've been having.

This is the longest streak of rainy and gloomy weather I can remember since moving here 20 years ago. I'm really ready for it to end.

The adjective for southern California is usually "Sunny" or "Hot". If I wanted weather like this I'd move to Seattle or Vancouver or some such.
