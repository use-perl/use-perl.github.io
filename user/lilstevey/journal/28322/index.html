with superfood tomato

lilstevey on 2006-01-12T09:20:23

I bought breakfast from boots the chemist this morning. After purchase I noticed a nice looking logo with a green leafy background on the box containing my Tomato and basil chicken salad. The logo contained the following text:

with superfood tomato
I was slightly perturbed - what had they done to the tomato to make it superfood?
the answer was on the back of the packet:
Superfoods are everyday foods that we believe are good for you, Their natural levels of important nutrients may be beneficial for health. Find out how superfoods can benefit you at www.boots.com/food
That'll be a bog standard run of the mill tomato then.
