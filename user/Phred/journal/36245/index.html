Re:Just a little patience required...

Phred on 2008-04-28T14:39:45

I emailed Schwern yesterday and consulted his wisdom. It turns out that the gravatar needs to be associated with your cpan.org email address, ala 'phred at cpan.org', or in your case 'wonko at cpan.org'. I logged into PAUSE, made the necessary changes to have my cpan address forward, and voila! Gravatar is back in action.
