darobin on 2002-04-16T01:18:58

That's something that came up on p5ee while it was changing from a potentially interesting effort to something bogged down in committee talk (I don't know much of what happened afterwards).

To summarize: when there's an important thing to be discussed, people assume that others are competent and are afraid of looking silly discussing something they're not confident they fully grok. Whent it's something that anyone can have an opinion on -- such as the colour of a multinationational's bike shed -- they want to make sure that their voice is heard.

Thankfully, such traits of mankind have led us to invent beer, which wasn't such a bad idea ;-)
